Closed rhinoplasty

Recovery After Rhinoplasty In New York

Closed rhinoplasty means that the incisions are made inside the nasal cavity. Closed rhinoplasty is more difficult than the open, but the advantage is that it is not damaged arteries that feed the tip of the nose.

Thus, the recovery is faster than the open method. Also, the operating incision is smaller, swelling is less pronounced, the likelihood of rough scars is less. Nose reshape surgery lasts less long time and the final result is achieved earlier.

Open rhinoplasty

Open rhinoplasty means that the incision is made at the narrowest point, and then the rest of the skin over the cartilage peels off and ascends. Thus, all the cartilage become bare, and the work is carried out on all stages of the nose reshaping.
