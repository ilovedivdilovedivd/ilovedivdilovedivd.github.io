What is a prudent length of time for drains after explant with capsulectomy?
Drain removal after capsulectomy
Feb 12, 2015
Hi and thank you for the question. The timing of drain removal cannot be accurately predicted before the procedure is performed. The drainage will be impacted by the amount of dissection that needs to be done to remove your capsule. Generally, I like to leave drains in place until they are putting out less than 30ml of fluid for three days in a row. This will decrease the risk of seroma formation. Hope this helps, good luck with everything.
