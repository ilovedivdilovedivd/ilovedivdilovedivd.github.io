Indications for rhinoplasty

Indications for rhinoplasty are:

– very big nose;
– violation of proportions;
– atypical form;
– a small hump;
– thickened tip;
– overhanging tip;
– large, extended wings and nostrils;
– bending;
– violation of the physiological position of the nasal septum, leading to difficulty breathing, and discomfort.

The relative and absolute contraindications

Surgery on the nose is prohibited in the following cases:

  1. chronic and acute diseases of internal organs;
  2. diabetes;
  3. blood disease, coagulation disorders;
  4. viral infection;
  5. inflammation follicle in the nose area;
  6. mental disorder.

Modern methods of performing rhinoplasty in Iran

The surgery is performed under general anesthesia and usually lasts no more than 2 hours. Currently, surgeons use several techniques of nose beauty surgery.

1. Open rhinoplasty.

This method is used in case of a significant volume of surgical intervention or during re-intervention. The surgeon makes the incisions in nose skin folds between the nostrils and in the base of each nostril.

Asian Rhinoplasty in Mashhad Iran Photo

The skin is separated from the cartilage, after which the doctor performs the necessary manipulations on removal or correction of the opening portion of cartilage or bone.

The advantage of the open rhinoplasty is a convenience for the surgeon, who sees all volume the work, respectively, the result is better . Beautiful tip of the nose can be made only by means of open access.

2. Closed rhinoplasty.

Closed access implies endonasal access. Symmetric incisions are performed along the nostrils, the surgeon manipulates the tool in an area of restricted visibility.

It requires a lot of precision, high qualifications and extensive experience. This method is good with insignificant corrections of the nasal bridge.

My personal experience says that the open rhinoplasty is always gives a nice and predictable result, particularly when it comes to changing the shape of the tip of the nose, or a significant change the nasal bridge.

Of course, if you need to remove a small hump back of the nose (no more than 2 mm), the surgeon can use a closed access.

But at a volumetric surgical intervention to expect beautiful aesthetic result is simply impossible.

Imagine, in order to reconstruct the back of the nose is necessary to change both the bone and cartilage of the nose.

If the bones can be corrected with the help of the osteotomy and grinding, but to correct the cartilage of the nasal bridge properly is simply impossible.
