How deep can dermabrasion go on surgical scars?
How deep?
Apr 6, 2017
Skin regenerates from the hair follicles, sweat glands, and sweat glands within the dermis. Any dermabrasion that goes deep removes a portion of the dermis. The dermis is the part of the skin that regulates body temperature, has sensory nerves, and protects the deeper structures. There are other methods of addressing scars such as DermaPen. This type of treatment does not remove any of the skin, but can actually increase the collagen content and help repair scars.
