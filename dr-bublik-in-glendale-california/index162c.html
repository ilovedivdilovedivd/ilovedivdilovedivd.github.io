Gauge earings

How to know when your nose is FULLY healed after rhinoplasty
Healing after rhinoplasty
Apr 5, 2016
Thank you for your question.  Depending on the type of rhinoplasty done, it typically takes about 6 months to a year for a majority of the swelling to go down.  However, the nose can still have small changes for many years after surgery.  In terms of the tip of the nose, it could be scar or cartilage grafting that was placed and this gives the nose a more solid feeling then before surgery.  I would visit your surgeon to make sure your healing is going well.  Good luck.
