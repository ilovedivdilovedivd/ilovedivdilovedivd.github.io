Rhinoplasty Recovery Time and Downtime?
Rhinoplasty recovery time
Dec 3, 2017
Rhinoplasty recovery is different for each patient and timing for dressing and suture removal can vary amongst surgeons. For my patients, I typically remove dressings (tape, cast, and sutures) at 1 week from surgery. At this time any residual bruising is beginning to resolve. You will notice varying amounts swelling of the nose for several weeks following surgery, but most patients are comfortable being in public once the bruising has resolved at 10-14 days….
