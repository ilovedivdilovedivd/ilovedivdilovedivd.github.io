Is it possible to add composite graft to my alar base after having alar base reduction?
complication of alar base reduction
Jan 7, 2020
Alar base reduction can cause nasal obstruction and nostril asymmetry if not properly executed. Too much resected alar base is difficult to reverse. Composite graft at the nostril base helps a little but complere reversal is not possible in most cases. If you are having breathing difficulty by alar base resection and nasal valve problem, composite graft and functional rhinoplasty can be of help to you. Please find nearby facial plastic surgeon who can help you. Thank you.
