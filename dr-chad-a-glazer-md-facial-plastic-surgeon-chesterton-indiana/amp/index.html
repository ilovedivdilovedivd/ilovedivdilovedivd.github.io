How much Restylane would I need to fix one of my tear troughs?
Tear Troughs and HA fillers
Jun 15, 2017
Tear trough deformities can often be softened with only 1mL of Restylane or a similar product. With any filler it is always better to “go slow” and add more as needed. Our office uses a cannula for injection to decrease the risk of bruising and swelling post injection. Any filler not used in the tear troughs could be used to soften naso-labial folds or another area of concern
Is it safe to have Restylane injections, chemical peels, and microdermabrasions while pregnant?
Pregnancy and Cosmetic Treatments
Jun 15, 2017
Studies have not been done to conclude how injectables and chemical peels affect a developing fetus so we do not recommend having these procedures done while pregnant. You should also consult with your primary care physician and OBGYN if you would like to have any procedures performed while breast feeding.
1064nm laser. Does a man's beard area have to be avoided?
Laser Genesis vs. IPL
Jun 15, 2017
Laser Genesis targets water and blood (wrinkles and redness) in the skin whereas IPL targets pigment (dark spots) that can also pick up pigment in the hairs causing hair loss. Laser Genesis does not target pigment and hair removal is generally not achieved. We ask our male patients to shave the day of the procedure as surface hairs can singe, be slightly uncomfortable and produce an unpleasant “burning hair” aroma.
