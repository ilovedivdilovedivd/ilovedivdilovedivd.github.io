Hitting my nose 6 month post op?
unlikely
May 10, 2016
It is unlikely that the injury that you discribed could cause a deformity to your nose. The normal course of swelling after a minor injury should subside. Seek medical attention if you feel that normal process is not happening.
Asymmetrical nostrils after closed rhinoplasty. 8 weeks post op
yes
May 10, 2016
Swelling stays in the nose for at least an entire year. Since you had a closed rhinoplasty, any nostril assymentry is either what you naturally had, or is from swelling. be patient.
Uneven nostrils, is it because of the swelling?
give it time
May 10, 2016
Swelling in the nose lasts a long time. about half the swelling will be gone in 4 weeks, the rest will take another 11 months or more to clear.
What are your thoughts regarding rhinoplasty and cross fit?
Ask your surgeon
Oct 23, 2015
My own bias is to keep patients away from any activity that will raise their blood pressure for two weeks. Then I let patients ease their way into their normal exercise routine over the next two weeks. Most patients are doing everything they like by a month after surgery. Ask your doctor what they recommend.
I am afraid of brain damage and death. What can you tell me that will ease my fears?
You take more risk driving in your car
Oct 23, 2015
There is risk to everything we do. You can be killed crossing the street. The risk for an elective surgery in the united states is very small. If you got in your car and drove for three hours, you would probably put yourself at greater risk (I'm not talking about your driving ability, just statistics.) Discuss these concerns with your surgeon prior to your surgery so he can address your concerns.
My nose bridge is too thin after septorhinoplasy! Will it get wider in time?
go back an ask your surgeon
Oct 23, 2015
Only your surgeon knows what he did at surgery. Go back and ask him what to expect.Remember to be patient; rhinoplasty surgery takes a long time to settle. Changes happen for a full year.
Rhinoplasty. The office staff sucks. Should this even matter?
yes it should matter
Oct 22, 2015
If a surgeon does not have control of his office staff, he may not have control of his operating room staff. What would happen if his operating room staff were not paying attention at a critical time in the operation? Maybe that staff is underpaid and resentful and would try to sabotage your experience. The office staff is a direct reflection of the physician. Maybe it means that he simply doesn't care about your office visits or your overall experience. Find yourself another provider....
Wide bridge and bulbous tip - will I get good results?
Have more consultations
Oct 21, 2015
The wide tip of your nose could be narrowed. This would give your nose a more feminine appearance. The volume of the tip of your nose is produced for the most part by cartilage in the tip. This cartilage can be reduced and adjusted to reduce the size of the tip of your nose.
Is it possible to reduce the width of my nostrils and nose tip?
have a consultation
Oct 21, 2015
See a surgeon and have an evaluation. It is certainly possible to thin the tip of your nose. discuss incisions and scar concerns with your doctor. Many techniques can be done from inside the nose.
Pimples right after rhinoplasty? What can I do?
go back to your surgeon
Oct 21, 2015
Show your surgeon the problem. There may be an office treatment that could be done right then. Most cosmetic offices have skin care departments that could also guide you as to treatments and products. The best place to start is with your surgeon's office.
Does it look like they are healing nicely?
they will drop
Oct 21, 2015
It looks like from your pictures that you fairly recently had your surgery. In my experience, a 550 cc implant will take a full six months to drop completely. This change will not be real noticable in your clothing. Be patient!
Interested in Breast Augmentation. seeking natural results. Any suggestions?
Two things...
Oct 21, 2015
Two things to make sure you discuss with your surgeon are the incision and the desired result. The result can be simulated on a 3D image. This, I find, is a very helpful way to communicate. Second, with your history of scarring, you need to be well versed in incision choices and scar treatment. No matter what incision is used, I would recommend using Scar Recovery gel by SkinMedica for 6 months after sergery on the scar.
Incision around nipple inside of the areola?
for good reason
Oct 21, 2015
There are several good resaons not to do thawt incision. My first concern would be the amount of scar introduced into the duct system of the breast. This could severly harm ones ability to breast feed. Second, this puts nipple sensation at greater risk. Finally, good studies show that capsular contracture rates are highr in any peri-areolar incision and should be done with proper reverence.
Planning future Breast Augmentation in approx 1 year. How soon should I begin consult?
Look at board certification
Oct 21, 2015
Start by checking board certification. The american board of Plastic Surgery with certify that a surgeon is "above board." This is a great place to start. Next, try to find a surgeon who can provide 3D simulation. This allows you to see what diferent shapes and sizes of implants look like on you. Finaly, choose a surgeon not based on their address, but on their bedside manner. In short, like anything else, choose a practitioner who is compotent,caring, and consistent!
