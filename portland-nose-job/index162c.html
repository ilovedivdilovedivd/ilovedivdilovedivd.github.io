Portland Nose Job

Rhinoplasty (nose surgery, nose shape correction) in Portland, as well as other aesthetic surgery can significantly improve the appearance. The patient must to think carefully the wishes and the expected effect and discuss this with the surgeon before will be decided on operation.

If the patient is physically healthy, psychologically stable and realistic in their expectations, he will be satisfied with the result of the nose job.

There are certain limitations associated with the individual anatomical features. Much depends on the type and condition of the skin, as well as the features of the process of scarring.

At the first consultation, the surgeon explains to the patient possible changes in shape of the nose in his case.

When planning the operation should be taken into account the patient’s age. Rhinoplasty surgery is best done after a young person has finished growing.

The most favorable for a rhinoplasty is between the ages of 18-25 years. However,the elimination of defects and deformities resulting from trauma or caused by “time”, age does not matter.

Sometimes treatment is appropriate at an earlier age if an internal defect is causing serious breathing problems, but it seems that is not the case here.

Rejuvenating rhinoplasty (plastic surgery anti-aging)

As we age, the skin in our face including our nose becomes thinner and over a period of years the tip of the nose “drops”.

Plastic Surgery Of Nose In Portland, Oregon Before And After

As we age and develop, the nose undergoes several changes: an increase the convexity of the nasal bridge, sagging of the base and tip of the nose, reducing the nasolabial angle. Are changing the proportions of the face, while the lower part is reduced, and the nose and ears increases.

Therefore, these the proportional changes are taken into account when performing rejuvenating operations. For example, when a facelift is often required nose reshaping.
A rejuvenating rhinoplasty is unique in each case , since it is carried out taking into account the individual characteristics of each patient.

The most common objective of the rhinoplasty in Portland is lift the tip of the nose, that is, the change in the nasolabial angle, sometimes intervention is required in the area of the nasal bridge.

Selecting the form of the nose (the new structure of the nose)

The Perfect Nose Job In Portland, Oregon Can Correct The Nasal Structure

Individual differences in the form of external nose determined by the location of its constituent of bone and cartilage elements.

The nose proportions with aesthetic standpoint great importance is attached to the profile angle, which is defined between a line connecting the forehead with chin and line the nasal bridge. For women, more suitable bit snub nose tip that is just rising above the nasal bridge. Such a nose rejuvenates the face.

It should be remembered that the analysis of the size and shape of the nose, as well as other components of the face, the surgeon is carries out considering the face as a whole. A rhinoplasty is aimed at nose reshaping that will be in harmony with the patient’s face, giving it a unique identity.
