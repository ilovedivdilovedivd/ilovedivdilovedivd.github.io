Juvederm Voluma XC for chiseled jaw
Aug 21, 2018
Voluma XC is a dermal filler that is often used for adding volume to the mid face and jawline. The main component of Voluma XC is hylalutonic acid - a naturally occurring acid that is produced in the body.
Voluma XC is used around the chin and jaw in order to add more fullness to those areas resulting in the chiseled jawline you desire.
