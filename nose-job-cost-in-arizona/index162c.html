Improving the aesthetics of the nose in any case should not violate the respiratory function. Inhaled air during the passage of the nasal cavity is cleaned of dust, allergens, bacteria and viruses, as well as the heated, humidified and disinfected.

In addition, with proper the nasal breathing occurs reflex regulation of brain blood circulation, heart, urogenital system, ventilation of the lungs.

Most patients have a visible or hidden from the eyes of a deviated nasal septum, and as a consequence is a shortness of nasal breathing. Then, during one operation is necessary to solve two problems at once – to restore nasal breathing and improve the shape of the nose.

This technology provides a high quality aesthetic surgery is called functional rhinoplasty in AZ. All patients are screened on a special device – Rhinomanometry that allows you to objectively evaluate the nasal breathing. It is very important for comparison with the results in the postoperative period.

In addition to respiratory functions, the doctor need to evaluate the condition of the skin. Worst patients for rhinoplasty are people with thick, porous, oily problem skin. This skin is prone to severe post-operative swelling, scarring, and thickness subcutaneous fat in the area of the tip can not allow the doctor to create accurate contour.

People suffering from acne must prepare the skin before plastic surgery for nose. After nose job procedure, the skin pores will be closed a dense cast bandage not less than a week, so you need to improve the skin condition.

The functional aesthetical rhinoplasty is conditionally divided into 2 stages:

Plastic Surgery Of The Nose Can Help You Achieve A More Sculpted, Better-proportioned Nose Tempe AZ

Stage 1 – functional (ENT stage). During this phase, endoscopic correction of intranasal structures is carried out to restore nasal breathing. The main attention is paid to nasal septum as the main supporting structure of the external nose.

Stage 2 – aesthetic. If the surgeon chooses the open technique, the essence of it lies in the fact that through inconspicuous incisions are peeled off the skin of the nose to correct the shape of the nose – bones and cartilage, guided by aesthetic canons and the patient’s wishes.

There are many surgical techniques. However, the surgeon selects only necessary, depending on the specific problems in a given patient.

On this basis we divided all our patients into 4 groups:

1 group – patients without major visible defects of external nose, but wishing to make any changes in their appearance – the so-called image-building operation.
2 group – patients with the consequences of a nose injury.
