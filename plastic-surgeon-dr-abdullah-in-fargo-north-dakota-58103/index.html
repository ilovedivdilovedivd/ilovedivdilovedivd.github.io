At just four months post-rhinoplasty, your final result has not yet been achieved. How long can I expect noticeable swelling?
Duration of rhinoplasty swelling
Jun 29, 2015
Swelling following rhinoplasty is at its peak in the days following the surgery. However, you should expect to see some degree of swelling for up to a year afterward.
