Can my tip be more defined to be less bulbous?
Tip Rhinoplasty
May 23, 2018
A tip Rhinoplasty refines the tip of the nose. The results are affected by the tip cartilage quality (strength) and the thickness of the nose skin. While a tip Rhinoplasty has  an easier recovery, for most noses a complete Rhinoplasty produces a nose with overall better balance. In other words, sometimes you do more to make it appear like less was done. That is especially true of nose surgery.  Dr. B.
What kind of rhinoplasty would best be suited for me? My nose was broken when younger and healed croo
Broken & Crooked Nose
May 12, 2018
The fact that your nose was broken at some point in the past does not affect the actual surgery at this time. A rhinoplasty for you needs to address your concerns of crookedness, extra width, lack of definition and your breathing/septum, if the latter is an issue. Dr. B.

Rhinoplasty Photo Simulation

Photo simulation for Rhinoplasty surgery can be a powerful communications tool between the patient and the surgeon. The simulation allows each side to convey their vision of the surgical outcome. It can be equally helpful to review the before and after photo gallery of a surgeon to familiarize yourself with a surgeon’s aesthetic vision and actual results.
