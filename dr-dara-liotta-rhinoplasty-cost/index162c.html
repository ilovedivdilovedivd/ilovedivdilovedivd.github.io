Rhinoplasty revision. Should I wait longer or get it done soon?
How long should you wait to get a revision rhinoplasty?
Mar 10, 2017
In general, I suggest waiting a full year before undertaking revision rhinoplasty, as there is subtle swelling yet to resolve at the 6 month-mark, and the scar tissue beneath the skin is not 100% mature. That being said, there are exceptions to this rule for me.  If you have a good relationship with your surgeon, I suggest that you discuss your concerns with her or him. Best!
