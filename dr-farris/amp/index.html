Botox for Sweating - Can Anyone Get Botox for Sweating?
Botox works like a charm for underarm sweating
May 3, 2011
I tell patients that using botox for underarm sweating is like turning off the faucet. You won't sweat 4-6 months so I suggest you have it in the early spring and it will last until late summer.  For hyperhidrosis under the arms, botox is a huge home run!
Lips drooping after Restylane
Fix this Restylane problem with Hyaluronidase
Jun 24, 2010
It is so important when injecting fillers into the lips to get the product in evenly and symmetric. If you had a dental block before the procedure this can make it harder to get that nice even look.  Have the doctor who injected you dissolve the Restylane with a little hyaluronidase. Your lips will swell after the hyaluronidase injections but in a couple days you will notice your bulging uneven lips disappear. 
Immune to Botox?

Are large blisters common after laser therapy?
Blisters after laser need treatment
Jun 23, 2010
First of all, throw away the neosporin.  The neomycin in this product causes terrible allergic reactions including blisters and may be making things worse. A few blisters or crusts after laser hair removal does happen from time to time but what you are describing sounds far more worrisome to me. I suggest you go back to see the physician in the office where you were treated.
