General information

Rhinoplasty simulator can not guarantee a 100% accuracy of the results of future operations and should we understand this.

It is impossible to take into account all the nuances of predicting the future contours of the nose by photo:

– skin type;
– patient age;
– anthropometric facial features;
– anatomic features of the nose;
– the internal structure of bone and cartilage tissue;
– regeneration of the skin and scarring.
