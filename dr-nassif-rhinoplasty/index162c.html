Re: I had a rhinoplasty in 2012 to correct a hooked nose; it was perfect for the first few years but now looks deformed.
Dec 9, 2018
Hello and thank you for your question and photos.After rhinoplasty, the nose takes many years to heal.  This often involves 3D shrink-wrap healing of the skin and soft tissues.  As this occurs, the normal cartilage of the nose can become deformed or warped.  If the nasal cartilage is weak or thin, the overall shape and structure of the nose can become compromised.  I hope this advice helps you and I wish you the best of luck.
