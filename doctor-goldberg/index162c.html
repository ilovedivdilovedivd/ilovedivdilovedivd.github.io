Asymmetrical flanks/love handles?
Flanks
Sep 13, 2015
Everyone is at least a little asymmetric. Some of us are more than others. Liposuction is a very good correction for the flanks as the skin there is typically very elastic. If you should regain weight, it will distribute evenly throughout the remaining fat cells in your body. I would be happy to see you for a consult in the Scarsdale office.
Can I get a Tummy Tuck, plus BBL and lipo, if I have an appendix scar?
Tummy tuck
Aug 30, 2015
Tummy tuck is definitely possible and safe now. However, there is a limit to how much liposuction an be done at the same time. While it will further distort your abdomen in the short run large volume liposuction is best done at least 6 months prior to the tummy tuck.
Is there a non surgical way to treat mild ptosis of both eyes?
Eyelid ptosis
Aug 11, 2015
Eyelid pros is can be corrected medically temporarily with phenylepherin drops. This is useful only for a few hours that you may have an event or such. For a permanent correction surgery is needed.
Is this a normal belly button 3 weeks post op tummy tuck?
Belly button
Jun 5, 2015
Scars are alwAys red and raised at the three week mark. Otherwise it looks perfectly normal. Try silicone sheeting to the scars to minimize their appearance.
After losing weight, I had Tummy Tuck, breast lift & arm lift. All scars were OK but arms are hypertr
Scars
Feb 14, 2015
The arms tend to scar worse than the other parts you have mentioned. I would recommend silicone sheeting for about six months after the wounds have healed. Intermittent steroid injections can also help.
Can you get a fat transfer to breast while implants are still in?
Fat transfer with implants
Feb 14, 2015
Fat grafting to the breast has certain concerns. When an implant is present, there is the additional concern of either infecting or rupturing the implant. That said, it is possible and frequently done.
I have large areolas and sagging breasts. What is the best procedure for me and what's the average co
Breast lift
Feb 2, 2015
A breast lift will address both of these issues. Scars are around the areolas and possibly down, but not in all cases.
Do I need a tummy tuck?
tummy tuck
Aug 4, 2014
Abdominal contouring is best determined by physical exam. Loose skin is generally best addressed by some form of abdominoplasty or tummy tuck. How to handle the belly button is a separate decision that can be made by your surgeon.
Can I please have more information about a Tummy Tuck?
tummy tuck
Aug 4, 2014
Considering what you are writing about how you feel about your body, I would recommend changing your mind before surgery to change you body. A tummy tuck is a bad option for anyone planning to have children, and especially anyone as young as 15.
Tummy tuck. Two weeks post-op. Does this look like an infection?
tummy tuck wound healing
Aug 4, 2014
It is certainly difficult to say whether there is an infection from the photographs. Your surgeon can help you manage the wound healing with some local wound care. They can determine whether you need antibiotics.
I had a breast lift and augmentation six weeks ago. My right breast is bigger than the left. Is that
breast augmentation, breast lift
Aug 4, 2014
This is very early in the post-op phase. I would certainly discuss this with you surgeon. Rest assured, the problem can be corrected. Most likely it will resolve on its own with a little time.
I'm 6 weeks post op after tummy tuck, and I had a complication. Why is my stomach not flat?
tummy tuck
Aug 4, 2014
Even several months after a tummy tuck, there will be swelling in the lower abdominal area. This is very typical and need not raise concern. Once instructed by your surgeon, you can consider massage of this area to help with the swelling. If there is still too much thickness at six months you can consider liposuction.
Pain and numbness on day 9 after healing so well, is this normal?
Breast augmentatioin
Jun 6, 2014
This look very typical. the pain and swelling should resolve in a few weeks. the numbness may take significantly longer. You should do well.
Im 19 and my breasts sag. What to do?
Breast sag
May 21, 2014
Surgical options would be either an implant alone or a lift or both. At 19, I would try to avoid a lift if at all possible. An exam would be necessary to tell for certain.

itchy scar abdominoplasty
