Las Vegas Plastic Surgery For Nose

Rhinoplasty in Las Vegas (or nose surgery, nose shape correction with the help of the operation) is one of the oldest in the world of aesthetic operations.

A nose job is one of the most complex surgical procedures. Rhinoplasty is also used for the correction of the respiratory system.

In the process of breathing air from outside passes through the nasal cavity is heated, humidified and cleared.

The nose filters the ambient air, as well as performs an essential function – the sense of smell.

Correction of nose shape with the help of a rhinoplasty helps to significantly improve a person’s appearance in case of injury.

Directly surgery is preceded a preliminary consultation with a plastic surgeon, during which the patient can describe in detail their wishes on the shape of the nose.

It is important that the patient was confident in their desires and expectations. In this case, it will be pleased with the results of the nose beauty surgery.

What is the best age for rhinoplasty?

During a scheduling nose reshaping should take into account the physiological features of the nose, which depend on the age of the patient.

The optimum age for rhinoplasty 18-25 years, because the nose and its cartilage tissue is still at the stage of final formation until the age of 18.

How rhinoplasty is performed?

Rhinoplasty For Man In Las Vegas, Nevada Before And After Photos

At the beginning the plastic surgeon carries out computer simulations of the nose forms tailored to personal wishes of the patient and the anatomical features of the face.

Careful medical history is going to. During rhinoplasty is using general anesthesia.

Surgical access to a particular place of surgical intervention is performed in two ways, which chooses a plastic surgeon:

  1. open: the incision is performed in the nasal septum,
  2. closed: incisions are carried out by oral mucosa (through the nostrils).

The whole procedure take approximately 1 to 2 hours. Volume of of surgical intervention depends on the original medical history, indications and methods of surgery.

Rhinoplasty Recovery Time: How Much Do I Need?

In the first days after the Las Vegas the rhinoplasty patient may feel a slight discomfort or headache that is associated with the natural healing process at the site of nasal surgery.

Also, within 1-2 days the swelling are observed in the periorbital area. After 1-2 weeks the nose becomes acceptable view. It should be noted that the final form of the nose will appear in 6-12 months after nose job.

We strongly recommend that to patients after rhinoplasty to follow the recommendations your doctor and beautician.

Patients, who underwent rhinoplasty, contraindicated physical activity for 1-2 months after surgery.
