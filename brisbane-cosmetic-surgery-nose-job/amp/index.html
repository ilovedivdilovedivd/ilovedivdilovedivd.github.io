Brisbane Cosmetic Surgery Nose Job

The operation is to change the outer contour of the nose by removing, smoothing or moving the bones and cartilage that form its skeleton.

Rhinoplasty in Brisbane is one of the most frequently carried out plastic surgery. The plastic surgery of nose can be performed after 18 years.

Modern views on the problems of pathology external and internal of the nose and contemporary methods of operation allow you to perform a one-time surgery on the nose on various anatomical structures of nose (for example, during deformation of the external nose and nasal septum deviation).

Depending on the indication in each case be chosen open or closed rhinoplasty techniques.

Preparation for rhinoplasty surgery (nose job).

The patient must be in good health (no cold or other disease during this period).

You must undergo a medical inspection(blood tests, urine tests, glucose level, ECG, chest X-rays), and inspection anesthesiologist.

If you smoke, you should stop smoking at least 2 weeks prior to nose reshaping and to refrain from smoking after it for at least 2-3 weeks. Otherwise the risk of impaired wound healing increases dramatically due to the fact that during smoking the blood flow in vessels is greatly reduced.

If you are taking any medication, you should discuss this with your doctor on prior consultation.

Anesthesia

QLD Nose Surgery Before And After

Rhinoplasty is performed under general or local anesthesia, depending on the wishes and general condition of the patient.

Stages of rhinoplasty

You will be placed to the hospital the day of rhinoplasty and you will passes a short preparation to it.

Incisions skin and nasal mucosa are performed so that the formed scarring after surgery are less noticeable. At the same time they can be found under careful examination.

Typically, scars are located on nose column (visible part) and on the internal surface of the nose (invisible part).

QLD Rhinoplasty Operation To Enhance Your Appearance By Balancing Facial Features

The duration of the nose job is an average of 1.5 – 3 hours (depending on complexity). After surgery emplastic or gypsum bandage superimposed on the face to 7-10 days, and to stop bleeding (or to fix the correct position of the skeleton) is introduced tampon into the nose or special plates of silicone which are removed after 1-3 day.

The recovery period

The postoperative period after rhinoplasty has a number of features. Immediately after nose job, edema develops of the nasal tissues and the face.

The main part of swelling (90%) disappears within two – three weeks after the surgery, although some swelling may persist for several weeks or even months to complete disappearance.

Depending on the complexity of the nose beauty surgery (usually the intervention on the bones), skin color changes on some areas of the face (“bruising”) due to the formation of intradermal hemorrhage.

Brisbane Post Nose Surgery By Dr Paul Belt

Discharge from the hospital is carried out for 2-5 days after rhinoplasty. Time when the patient starts to work, determined by its state of health. Typically, light work is allowed after 5-7 days.

It is forbidden to wear glasses for 2-4 weeks after rhinoplasty. It is necessary to limit the thermal and physical activity.

Immediate results after rhinoplasty in Brisbane can be assessed after 3 months, and the final results 1-1.5 years after completed the formation of scars.

Possible complications.

After rhinoplasty may develop any general surgical complications (abscess wounds, bleeding, thrombosis, thromboembolism, formation of keloids, etc..), And the following complications specific to this operation:
