Nose Cosmetic Surgery in Seattle WA

Rhinoplasty in Seattle is an reconstructive or aesthetic surgical intervention, aimed at changing the shape and size of the nose, restored breathing through the nose.

Nose reshaping can dramatically change the proportions of the whole face: for example, allows you to achieve the effect of increasing the visual eye and make the common features more harmonious.

If rhinoplasty was carried out correctly it can change a person’s appearance for the better, raises your self-esteem and gives confidence.

Rhinoplasty is less than any other plastic surgery to fit in the standard solutions.

The surgeon must take into account the condition and type of the patient’s skin, bone development department, the structure and elasticity of cartilage and tissue, to anticipate the future process of internal scarring, and much more, which is often determined by the individual characteristics and reactions of the organism.

Depending on this, a specialist selects a specific way of solving the patient’s problem.

In addition, it is necessary to have good spatial imagination. And, of course, perfect skills that are acquired only during practice.

I have quite a neat nose, but sometimes sniffs so that the most audible. A familiar medic said that, most likely, the nasal septum is curved. But I had a straight nose!

Nose Job In Seattle Before After Photos

A sniff is a sign of the complicated nasal breathing. The reasons for this can be many: a deviated nasal septum, the consequences of vasomotor rhinitis, polyps, and even foreign bodies. We must look for the cause and to be treated.

The curvature of the nasal septum is the internal deformation and is not necessarily associated with the curvature of the outer the bones and cartilages and it may be invisible from the outside, but more often it is the result of trauma.

The curvature of the nasal septum is eliminated only by nose surgery. A visit to a specialist should not be delayed, as this can lead to chronic inflammation of the paranasal sinuses, hearing loss, problems with the the cardiovascular system and other diseases.

In what cases is used an endoscopic method?

Dr. Naficy Procedure Of Rhinoplasty in Washington Picture

Endoscopes in rhinosurgery are used to diagnose and, increasingly, for sparing surgical treatment of diseases of the nose. With the help of an endoscope can be examine the body in great detail from the inside and display the enlarged image on a computer.

Surgeons with high accuracy eliminates deformation of cartilage and bone structures, polyps, adenoids, excessive mucous membrane – all of which makes it difficult nasal breathing.

Endoscopes are used for more precise aesthetic result and less of traumatisation tissue in aesthetic correction shape of the nose.

Will there be visible scars after the surgery?

In the closed method, the incisions are made on the inside of the nasal the mucous membrane, and scars are not visible. After an open rhinoplasty scar remains in the form of short broken line below the tip of the nose to the columella. It is less visible than a straight scar.

After a few weeks after the nose job the scar is difficult to see. None of the patients appeals with a request for correction of the scar on the columella. When reducing the wide and large nostrils the scar is hidden under the natural skin fold between the cheek and the nose wing.

In very rare cases, the scar can be seen when in the patient the porous and oily skin.

Does a nose job hurt?

As a rule, the nose reshape surgery is performed under general anesthesia. It is easy tolerated by the patient, and patients do not feel nothing during the surgery. Pains in the area of the nose is not present in postoperative period. You may be concerned about the necessity breathe through your mouth.

Tampons are inserted into the nose to support tissue and the shape of the nose. After three or four days they are removed.

It is possible to make a rhinoplasty under local anesthesia, especially since the mid-nineties to the vast majority of these operations were carried out without general anesthesia.
