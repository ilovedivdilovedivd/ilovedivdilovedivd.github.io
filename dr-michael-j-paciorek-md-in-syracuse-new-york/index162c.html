Rhinoplasty for deviated septum
Post traumatic rhinoplasty
Jan 21, 2018
A deviated septum may cause a whistle in the nose . If a deviation is caused by a traumatic event the external nose is often deviated as well .Insurance will usually cover deviated septal surgery but often make getting coverage for external nasal repair difficult , classifying this as cosmetic. I would recommend going to a double board certified ENT/Facial Plastic Surgeon for another opinion .

The midface is most important
May 6, 2017
A tired appearance is usually related to the midface dropping , and more specifically the creation of deep shadows in the tear trough area . Anything you do to improve the infra orbital shadowing will make you look more "refreshed". Injection of filler , fat , a midface lift or laser will help .
