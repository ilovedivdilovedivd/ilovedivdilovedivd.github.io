Nose Plastic Surgery In Florida

Rhinoplasty is a plastic surgery aimed to reshaping of the nose, it corrects congenital and acquired defects.

The main types of rhinoplasty in Florida:

– reduction in the nasal bridge (removal of the hump)
– lifting back of the nose using grafts or implants
– tip rhinoplasty
– plastic of the nasal septum (septoplasty)
– narrowing of the base of the nose wings.

Purpose:

a) correction of physiological or acquired defects
b) full restoration of the nose, which is absent due to illness or from birth.

Indications:

– improvement of nasal breathing
– dissatisfaction with the size and / or shape of the nose
– excessive magnitude
– proportions violation
– atypical form
– small hump
– thickened tip
– overhanging tip
– large, extended wings and nostrils
– bending
– violation of the physiological position of the nasal septum, leading to incorrect breathing and to discomfort.
