Nose Surgery In London Prices

Rhinoplasty is the most popular plastic surgery in London. With its help, the nose correction is performed, for aesthetic and medical reasons.

During the operation, is performed the separation of the skin from the bone and cartilage, and then eliminated the defect.

The operation can be conducted in the nasal septum, the tip back and other parts of it.

To date, there are two main method of rhinoplasty in London – open and closed.

With open rhinoplasty surgeon has full visual control over the course of surgery and easy access to all parts of the nose, what does not exist during closed rhinoplasty.

Open rhinoplasty is assigned if the patient requires a complex surgical correction. It is important to take into account that in the course of performance an open rhinoplasty surgeon will perform incisions in nose natural folds along the lines of the nasal septum and in the base of the nostrils (if necessary).

External incision is carried out only on the skin between the nostrils, all the other incisions are made in the inner part of the nose.

May leave a small scar after this surgery that is almost imperceptible.

The operation takes less than two hours. If you need a complex operation, it is carried out in several stages.

Open rhinoplasty is most often carried out only if it is impossible to get the final result by other methods.

Nose Refinement Surgery In London

Such operation is prescribed:

  1. If re-rhinoplasty, if necessary to remove any shortcomings of previous operation;
  2. Installation of transplants that change the shape of the nose and its size;
  3. If a complex anatomical configuration of the nose, if you can not use other methods of correction.

Disadvantages of open rhinoplasty:

  1. post-operative recovery takes longer than with closed rhinoplasty;
  2. it is impossible to predict 100% the final result as in a closed rhinoplasty.

The advantages of open rhinoplasty:

Procedure Of Rhinoplasty In London Before And After Photo

  1. During surgery the surgeon has the opportunity to visually monitor the whole process and instantly make some adjustments if necessary;
  2. in the process do not need to twist or stretch the tissue that gives the ability to precisely match the tissue after all procedures;
  3. there are no difficulties with the installation and fixation of grafts even the most complex forms;
  4. it is possible to obtain a perfect symmetry of the nose during the nasal surgery.

After open rhinoplasty in London may appear swelling, bruising, slight hematomas – all of this commonplace. Usually swelling disappears after a few days after surgery, and after a few months – you can to evaluate the intermediate result.

It is necessary to be aware of the more serious side effects:

Nose Operation In London Before And After

  1. too dropped or upturned nose;
  2. beaky nose;
  3. saddle nose;
  4. bending and other changes to the nose;
  5. Difficulty of breathing;
  6. intracranial complications;
  7. problems with the partition;
  8. toxic shock;
  9. hematoma.

In order not to encounter these problems after the surgery, you need to follow all the recommendations of the recovery, which the doctor gives .
