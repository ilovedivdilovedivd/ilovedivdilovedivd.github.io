Will I need a septoplasty or a rhinoplasty? My nose is obviously deviated.
Rhinoplasty and Septoplasty
Nov 11, 2017
Septoplasty is usually performed to improve breathing, and generally works on the portions of the septum not visible externally. Rhinoplasty reshapes the nose, and often involves treatment of the upper part of the septum, separate from surgery to improve nasal breathing. These two are often combined and not mutually exclusive, but generally have different purposes.

Will Swelling Be Inevitable After Restylane Injection Under the Eyes?
2- 14 days, usually
Nov 11, 2017
Treatment with Restylane involved placement of material through a needle or cannula under the eyes. Ideally, this is placed below the muscle and against the bone. Even at this level, the covering (skin, muscle) is still very thin, and the area around the eyes “hides” the normal amount of swelling and bruising less effectively. Generally, these subside in 1-2 days, but occasionally can last 2 weeks or longer.
