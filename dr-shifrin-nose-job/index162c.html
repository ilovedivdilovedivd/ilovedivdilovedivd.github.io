I broke my nose when I was younger causing nasal deformity, now that I’m older would it be difficult
Rhinoplasty
Feb 29, 2020
You have a nasal deviation with likely underlying septal curvature. You also have thick sebaceous skin. These are the challenges in doing your rhinoplasty. A nice open septorhinoplasty Can be achieved though with reasonable expectations in an experienced surgeons hands. Congrats on the good health and exercise and best of luck!
Is it possible some deviations just aren’t correctable?
Quarternary revision rhinoplasty
Feb 16, 2020
Each time you have surgery scar tissue is laid down which it more resistant to long term change. That being said, you’ve had a couple septoplasties and a sinus surgery but you did not list a rhinoplasty. To correct the deviation a complete rhinoplasty would be needed with cartilage grafting holding the septum in place. Essentially, your entire nose needs to be taken apart and put back together with appropriate cartilage support to achieve an improvement in the straightness of it. Best
