Does rapid/flash/quick/fast recovery breast augmentation cause more internal scarring?
Breast augmentation is routinely fast recovery
Aug 21, 2015
The technique that I use for breast augmentation is routinely fast recovery. You can go back to work in 2-3 days and resume all normal activities within 2-3 weeks. The technique involves submuscular placement of the implants. There is generally minimal bleeding from this procedure. We use self dissolving sutures so there are no sutures to be removed. There is minimal swelling or pain afterwards.
