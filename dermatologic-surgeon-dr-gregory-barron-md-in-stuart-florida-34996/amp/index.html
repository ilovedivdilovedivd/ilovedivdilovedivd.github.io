Does laser hair removal work for blonde facial hair?
Blonde Hair
Jun 12, 2014
Unfortunately, laser hair removal does not typically work for blonde, white, grey or silver hair. It can be somewhat effective for light brown hair. It works best for dark coarse hair. The laser is attracted to pigment which blonde hair is lacking. Generally other modalities such as electrolysis are more effective for lighter hair shades.
