Why do I have to wear a sports bra for 6 weeks?
Reason for sports bra after breast augmentation
Jun 30, 2014
We ask our patients to avoid underwire bras for the first 6 weeks following surgery because during this time the implants need to drop into the pocket, and underwire bras create the opposite effect by pushing the breasts upward. Underwire might also irritate or be uncomfortable over your incision in the inframammary fold. A sports bra is a reasonable wire-free bra that will provide good support and compression during this time period.

I have white discharge coming from my nipple 8 weeks after Breast Augmentation from small 32B t
Discharge after breast augmentation
Jun 30, 2014
Although it does not happen commonly, the stimulation of breast surgery may actually activate your milk ducts. This discharge, in this case, is self-limiting and should resolve spontaneously. However, if it persists, you should definitely return to see your surgeon to rule out other causes of breast discharge.

Liposuction for Saddle Bags

Will an implant rise at all after Capsulectomy and silicone implant replacement?
Implant Replacement
Jun 30, 2014
An
implant may temporarily rise for a few weeks after implant placement or
replacement if it was placed under the muscle. The pectoralis muscle may
contract in response to surgery, displacing the implant into a higher position.
This is temporary and typically resolves in a few weeks. It is more likely to
occur or to be more significant the first time the muscle is stretched in the
original augmentation. Many people experience little to no rise in the implant
after an implant exchange.

Exercise After Augmentation?
Exercise after Breast Augmentation
Jun 12, 2012
That's a great question. We recommend you do begin walking lightly immediately after surgery. The main thing is not getting your heart rate up for about 2 weeks after surgery.
