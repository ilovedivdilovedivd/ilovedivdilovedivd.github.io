Statement: Dr. Craig Barbieri is a board-certified phlebologist and hair transplant surgeon in the Kansas City, KS area. Along with practicing at the Vein Clinic of Greater Kansas City, where he treats vein disorders and venous disease, Dr. Barbieri is the founder of Aesthetic KC, where he personally performs all cosmetic injections himself, and the primary hair restoration surgeon at Hair by Robotics in Overland Park. Dr. Barbieri is particularly well-known for his expertise with ARTAS robotic hair transplants, a type of FUE hair transplants.

FUE hair transplants (follicular unit extraction transplants) are the most advanced method of cosmetic hair restoration to date. Dr. Barbieri was one of the first surgeons to offer robotic hair transplant technology in the Kansas City area because he recognized the ability of this cutting-edge procedure to truly transform his patients’ lives. Dr. Barbieri has been recognized for his accomplishments in this work by ARTAS itself with the prestigious Clinical Center of Excellence Award.

A native of Arma, Kansas, Dr. Barbieri graduated summa cum laude with a bachelor’s degree in biology from Pittsburg State University in Pittsburg, Kansas, before earning his medical degree from the University of Kansas School of Medicine in Kansas City. He then went on to complete his internship and residency at Baptist Medical Center. He is double-board-certified by both the American Board of Family Medicine as well as the American Board of Venous and Lymphatic Medicine.

Dr. Barbieri is dedicated to providing considerate, quality care to all of his patients and is passionate about fostering a family-like, comforting atmosphere at his practice. Dr. Barbieri lives in Lenexa, Kansas with his spouse and three wonderful children.

Does hair grow back after hair loss without hair transplantation?
Hair growth
Aug 6, 2016
Hi. Thanks for your question. Typically you will not see hair grow back from medical therapies such as Rogaine, laser therapy or Finasteride. However if there is a hair in the area of loss, even if it is very small or fine, you can see that hair get stronger and regain pigment. Therefore it can give the appearance of growth. I tell my patients to expect to see stabilization in their hair loss. If you want more hair, you should consider a transplant
