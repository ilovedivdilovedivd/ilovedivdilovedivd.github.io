Cheek Implants. Do They Require Replacements? Risks?
Longevity of cheek implants
Nov 12, 2012
Cheek implants made of polyethylene (a solid material) will not degrade over time. If they are secured with screws, they will not migrate either. Although the implant will not change, your facial soft tissue will age the change in soft tissue overlying the implant may cause a chnage in facial appearance with age. These volume changes can be treated with fillers, fat grafting, or surgical repositioning of the tissues.
