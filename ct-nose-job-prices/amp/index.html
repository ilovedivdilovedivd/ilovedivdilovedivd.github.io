Connecticut Nose Job Prices

Rhinoplasty in Connecticut is a special operation, during which the plastic surgeon alters the dimensions or shape of the human nose.

You can also restore the normal functionality of nasal breathing. The people most often want make the nose job due to various injuries after accidents and accidents, but also the operation is very popular among people who are just not satisfied with the appearance of their nose.

Rhinoplasty is a surgical procedure in a cartilaginous or bone part of the nose. A good plastic surgeon will be able to easily lift or narrow the tip of the nose to eliminate asymmetries or eliminate nasal septum deviation or hump.

Today, rhinoplasty is divided into several types. There are open and closed rhinoplasty, as well as non-surgical rhinoplasty.

Plastic surgeon chooses one or other type of surgery according to the goals and objectives. It is important not to disrupt respiratory function of the nose during nose job.

The qualitative and relatively inexpensive rhinoplasty is performed by experienced and highly qualified plastic surgeons in our clinics. Here you can easily and quickly correct any deficiencies and problems with your nose.

The concept of primary rhinoplasty refers to patients who had never before done any kind of surgery on the nose. In this case, there are great opportunities for the modeling nose and rhinoplasty result is very close to the expected.

Primary rhinoplasty in CT solves the problem of a small hump, curved, wide, narrow nose, copes with congenital or acquired deformities which appeared as a result of injury.

Rhinoplasty Recommendations

Before And After Plastic Nose Surgery In New Haven Connecticut

  1. During the 20 days after nose job is not recommended the excessive exercise.
  2. You need to stick to partial load mode, giving preference to rest.
  3. During the first postoperative week should wash your hair with someone else’s help, avoid water contact with the bandage.
  4. During the first postoperative week wearing clothing that fastens in the front, avoid sweaters and golf.
  5. The skin of the nose is necessary to process by lotion after removing the bandage.
  6. Do not wear glasses for a month after the nose beauty surgery – they can lead to deformation of the of the nasal bridge.
  7. You need to eliminate the solarium, thermal and physical loading for 1.5 months after the nose plastic surgery so it can lead to swelling of the nose.
