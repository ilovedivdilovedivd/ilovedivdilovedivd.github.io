Can a broken nose create a bump?
Broken nose hump
May 4, 2020
Hi there. Most humps are not related to nasal trauma. However, is is common to have some irregular contours after a nasal fracture and that the nasal profile looks different from different sides – this indicated an underlying asymmetry. Both correct of asymmetry and contour refinement are conventional goals of septorhinoplasty. Good luck!
