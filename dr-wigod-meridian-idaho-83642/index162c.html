If I live in London (GB) but I want to have a mommy makeover in Miami, is that possible?
Be careful with medical tourism
Nov 14, 2017
Unless you live in an area that a service is not available or need to travel to be cared for by a friend / relative, medical tourism is an unnecessary risk.  Local surgeons have a reputation that you can research easier.  Operations need frequent follow up.  If you have a problem and traveled, you surgeon will not be available and you may be on your own.  I happily do surgery on out of town patients, but I do make sure they understand the downsides.

Implant feel

Is breast augmentation safe for a patient with asthma?
Asthma and surgery
Oct 29, 2017
Patients for elective surgery who have asthma should have good control and no recent emergency room visits.  If not, the patient should be seen by her doctor before proceeding.  She should bring her inhaler and take a dose pre and post op.  The patient should discuss her asthma with both the surgeon and anesthesia provider.

Oct 13, 2017
Sometimes, you just have to sneeze.  Vigorous sneezing may be uncomfortable and cause some bleeding, but it would be unlikely to affect anything structural.  Claritin  or similar drugs may help.  A gentle controlled sneeze would be best.

Are my breasts tuberous/tubular?
Tuberous Breast
Sep 3, 2017
A tuberous breast has a distinctive look.  Not only is there underdevelopment of the lower pole of the breast, there is also scar like constriction and commonly herniation of the areola and nipple (puffy look).  An augmentation approach that releases the scar bands and a more firm and or shaped implant may add to the result.
Tummy Tuck before or after weight loss?
Weight loss before tummy tuck
Aug 26, 2017
You want to have optimal healthy weight loss with good nutrition and exercise that you can maintain before having a surgery.  That will give the best result with the least number of problems.  If you have done these things and are still not at an ideal weight, it is reasonable to proceed with surgery in most patients.
Can I get a Tummy Tuck if I have had several surgeries on my stomach?
Abdominoplasty with previous scars
Aug 20, 2017
Prior scars on the abdomen do not rule out an abdominoplasty.  In fact, a prior scar is not unusual at all.  The operative plan, however, may need to be altered to do it as safely as possible.  The main issue is blood supply.  Every patient is an individual and you could have many scenarios, so a Plastic Surgery consult is the only way to know for sure.

Breast Implants only often sufficient
Aug 11, 2017
Breast implants by themselves are often sufficient to fill a small breast.  Lifting at the same time as filling with an implant decreases the room for an implant and adds cost, scar, and risk.  Sometimes you need a lift… most of the time you don’t.
