Reducing a bulbous tip and narrowing the nostrils

What you are asking for seems quite achievable based on the photos. You have a very nice nose to start with and it just could use a little refining. The small hump can be reduced, the tip refined and made more narrow, and the nostrils can also be made smaller. The nostrils are usually made smaller by removing a small wedge of tissue where your nostril meets your cheek and lip. The scar is very small and very hard to see. Bring your photos to your consult. They will be helpful. (Jeffrey E. Kyllo, MD, Seattle Plastic Surgeon)
