Do I need Chin Liposuction or Neck Lift?
Chin lipo or neck lift
Nov 12, 2021
It appears that you have a congenitally small chin and perhaps malocclusion issues. Certainly a neck lift with a chin implant will give you the most dramatic result but at 25 you might try the chin and neck lipo to see if this gives you what you want. Could always do the neck lift in the future. Sometimes less is Best!
Are chin implants recommended for young patients?
Chin implant in twenties
Nov 12, 2021
We do chin implants on many young patients for microgenia. Very frequently combined with rhinoplasty to help improve their profiles. I would not recommend either procedure for pre puberty patients. Chin implants if placed properly should last a lift time and not shift. I prefer solid silicon implants place through a submental incision. Many patient can also benefit with some submental liposuction which can easily be performed through the same incision. In my mind, Chin implant is perhaps the...
Can I get a chin implant with Novocaine only?
Chin Implant under Local
Nov 12, 2021
Yes. Especially is you are motivated to avoid anesthesia. We do this frequently. Once the mental nerves are blocked, it is easy to anesthesitize the chin and perform the surgery. I recommend a submental approach as opposed to intraoral. Better visulization to place implant is proper position. Also many patients need liposuction of the neck which can be done through the same incision.Also under local. Good luck!

Covid Test after Rhinoplasty

Nasal valve collapse: breathing difficulty and asymmetry. Can this be corrected?
Collapsed Nasal Valve
Nov 12, 2021
Cartilage grafting is the most traditional and time honored technique for correcting nasal valve collapse or insufficiency and is effective in improving the breathing in most cases. The depression in the nasal valve should improve as well but often need secondary improvement with injections. This is a common problem often overlooked by many rhinoplastic surgeons. Good luck.
Does a facelift limit the range of your facial expressions?
Facial expressions following facelifting.
Nov 5, 2021
In General, facelifting should not have any long term effect on facial expression. Of course, there is temporary effects due to swelling but these should resolve by 4-6 weeks.Many times the "sad " expression around the mouth can be improved with less aggressive procedures such as botox and fillers. Threadlifts can also be a consideration. I always suggest trying the least invasive procedure first before pulling our the "big guns" Hope this is helpful.Steve Hopping,MD, FACS
Facelift: is a submandibular gland reduction safe?
Submandibular gland removal?
Nov 5, 2021
The submandibular glands are one your major salivary glands. I do not like removing submandibular glands during face lift except in extreme circumstances. I prefer to tighten the overlying platysma muscles and "suspend" the glands thereby reducing their aesthetic prominence. Hope that helps.

What type of thread lift is best for early stages of jowls?
Tread lifting for early jowling.
Mar 23, 2020
In my opinion, early jowling as you describe is a great indication for thread lifting. I prefer the bidirectional novothreads (pdo) and have many happy patients with this technique. The beauty is that the procedure can be performed under local anesthesia with usually minimal bruising and a weekend recovery. Results last from 1-2 years although many patients return earlier for an "enhancement" of their results.
