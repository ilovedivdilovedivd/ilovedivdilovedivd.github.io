Would Juvederm Ultra or Ultra Plus be able to address my cheek volume loss?
Juvederm for cheek volume loss

I’ve had acne for more than 10 years. Can you suggest a suitable treatment?
Acne treatment
Jan 24, 2015
I recommend seeing a dermatologist who can first help to get your acne under control. Topical therapies and oral medications can be extremely helpful. Once the underlying acne is controlled, there are various modalities, such as peels, laser or dermal needling, to treat any residual scarring.
Should I be concerned about this mole on the sole of my foot?
Mole on the foot
Jan 24, 2015
If you have any concern at all, I recommend having the spot examined by a dermatologist. Routinely, dermatologists perform dermoscopy and serial imaging to assess for worrisome features or changes. A biopsy is often very helpful.
