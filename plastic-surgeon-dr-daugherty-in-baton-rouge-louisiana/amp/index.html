Birth control and breast augmentation?
Birth Control
Jun 10, 2019
There is no link between birth control pills and capsular contracture in breast implants. Birth control pills contain low dose hormones to alter your reproductive cycle. You should talk to your OB-Gyn about risks for cancer, especially if you have a family history of breast, ovarian or endometrial cancer or blood clots. There are multiple options for birth control, and not all contain hormones.

Do I have an infection? What should I do? Spitting stitches after breast lift.
Sutures may spit out of skin
Nov 14, 2018
Hi Jammin’ –
It is not uncommon for sutures to cause an inflammatory reaction and spit through a closed wound. While your wound is likely closed in multiple layers for the best possible scar, you should see your surgeon for evaluation of this. The sutures will likely be removed and then wound care with topical antibiotic ointment done until the small openings are healed over.
Best of luck!

Hi shezza13,
