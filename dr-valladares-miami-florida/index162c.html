Can you have CoolSculpting if you are on statins?
Statins and Coolsculpting
Sep 6, 2019
Coolsculpting is a safe and effective way to eliminate fat pockets in specific areas of the body. The cooling technology is designed to only target fat cells right below the skin. If you have high cholesterol or are taking statins, the Coolsculpting procedure would not affect this at all and you should still be able to achieve amazing results.
I had DVT in my leg twice is it safe for somebody who takes blood thinners regularly?
DVT and Coolsculpting
Sep 6, 2019
This is a very common question that we get asked at our practice. There is no contraindication to treating someone who has had previous DVTs. If you are currently on blood thinners, you may experience temporary bruising that may last longer that someone who is not on blood thinners; however the fat cells would still be affected and you would have beautiful results.
