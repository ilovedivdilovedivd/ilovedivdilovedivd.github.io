Plastic Surgery For Nose In Orlando FL

Rhinoplasty in Orlando, Florida is surgery to change the shape of your nose.

The main principles of rhinoplasty operation

During a closed rhinoplasty procedure, the doctor makes a small incision inside the nostrils to gain access to the bone and cartilages.

Unlike open rhinoplasty, the skin is not separated from the nose framework.

The physician achieves the desired shape of the nose by removing or adding bone, cartilage and tissue using miniature surgical instruments.

While some physicians prefer the closed technique, arguing that the short recovery period and the duration of the operation, both methods give excellent results.

During open rhinoplasty procedure, the surgeon makes a small incision to the columella and inside each nostril. Unlike the closed rhinoplasty, these incisions allow the doctor to separate out the correct anatomical components of the nose, which provides a complete visualization of the nose.

Thereafter, the surgeon can inspect the nose framework and implement accurate correction of its shape and structure. Using special tools and perfect technique of surgery, the doctor achieves the desired shape of the nose by removing or adding bone, cartilage and tissue.

At the beginning of the operation, the surgeon usually makes two incisions. A small incision is made to the columella – the tissue site that separates the nostrils.

Orlando Nose Job Before And After

Using the retractor to open the inside of the nose, the doctor makes a second incision, known as edge which extends from the incision in the columella to each nostril.

The surgeon carefully dissects the skin along the incision on the columella using surgical scissors to separate it from the framework. Then, during an operation takes place dissection of the anatomical nasal components. This will allow the doctor to carry out an accurate correction of the form of nose.

Nose job Surgical technique:

Changing the size and shape of the nose cartilage and bone can occur without external incision – closed access, or with a cut in the base of the nasal septum (columella) – open access.

Peculiarities of rhinoplasty recovery period

Orlando, Florida Most Beautiful Nose After Rhinoplasty

Few days after rhinoplasty will have to breathe only by mouth since the nose all this time will be filled with absorbent tampons. The shape of the nose from the outside helps keep the cast bandage, which is shaped like a butterfly. A bandage have to wear for 1-2 weeks. The face swells considerably after the operation.

Keep in mind that moderate swelling lasts up to six months in some individual cases. The first weeks after the plastic surgery you need to sleep with a head slightly raised, thereby swelling disappears faster.

You should also significantly limit the emotional and physical stress, in order to avoid nasalbleedings. You will have to to abandon the cold and hot meals on seven days.

You can not wear glasses one and a half months, as even the most lightweight and comfortable frame may lead to deformation of the nose, which is not yet fully healed.

Proper care of the nose in the first days after rhinoplasty

Orlando Rhinoplasty Correcting An Excessively Long, Short, Crooked Or Wide Nose

The first three days the patient is in Orlando clinic, for his own psychological comfort and to avoid any complications. After this time the tampons are removed, and the patient can go home.

Often after tampons will derive, to patient are inserted a soft protectors-plates made of polyurethane, which is absolutely not visible from the outside. They do not interfere with breathing and removed after three weeks.

The outer bandage should always be dry. During wearing can take a bath and a shower, but very carefully, not to get water on the bandage. After removing the bandage, you can begin to use makeup, trying not to break your nose.

Remember that procedures such as deep cleaning, peeling, can be carried out not earlier than 3-4 weeks after nose reshaping. Contraindicated sports for 3-4 months. The same goes for any physical activity.
