Will this go away or will I need a revision?
Swelling after surgery on the neck
Mar 15, 2019
Surgery in the neck area can create swelling that can take several months to totally resolve. Gravity tends to make swelling settle in this area, therefore, it can be one of the last areas for swelling to go away.  In some patients, the "final result" will not be apparent for 4-6 months.  Patience and your compression garment are your best keys to getting to your final result.   Best of luck!
