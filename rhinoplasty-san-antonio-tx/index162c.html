Nose Job San Antonio Texas

Rhinoplasty in San Antonio is a surgical intervention whose task consists in changing the size and shape of the external nose, as well as the restoration of nasal breathing function.

During the operation, the manipulation performed on the bone and cartilaginous part of the nose. Rhinoplasty is one of the most popular areas of aesthetic surgery.

It allows you to radically change the appearance for the better with minimal traces of intervention.

With the help of rhinoplasty can correct the hump, to narrow or shorten the tip of nose, align the nasal bridge, remove the asymmetry of the wings and the excess width of the base.

After nose beauty surgery, you will get a nose that be blend in with the other facial features and look completely natural.

The nose can be made more narrow, straight, short or long – the possibilities of aesthetic surgery in this area are enormous.

In many cases, people find it difficult to believe in the veracity of photos showing how plastic surgery on the nose transfigures the face.

Yet these impressive results are real. The nose is perhaps one of the most important parts of the face that can significantly transfigure face of the patient.

Tip Rhinoplasty: What is the Procedure?

Depending on the anatomical characteristics of the patient and the desired outcome in the course of this operation is performed the impact on the soft tissues, cartilages and columella. It allows you to change:

  1. distance from tip of nose to the face;
  2. thickness region above the tip;
  3. the angle between the tip and the upper lip;
  4. the position of the nasal septum.
