Nose Cosmetic Surgery In Manchester

Rhinoplasty in Manchester is a surgical procedure aimed at reshaping the nose. This operation is one of the most common today in plastic surgery.

Rhinoplasty is used, if necessary, to change the size or shape of the nose, the nostrils, or the angle between the tip of the nose and upper lip.

In addition rhinoplasty is performed if appropriate correction birth defects or injury to the nose, as well as for the fact that to eliminate chronic respiratory problems.

Principle of surgery

Manchester doctors possess methods both open and closed rhinoplasty. Both methods give excellent results and are used depending on the structure of the nose and the specifics of the operation itself.

During a closed rhinoplasty procedure, the doctor makes a small incision inside the nostrils to gain access to the bone and cartilages.

With the help of miniature surgical instruments, the physician achieves the desired shape of the nose by removing or adding bone, cartilage and tissue.

During open rhinoplasty procedure, the plastic surgeon makes small incisions inside each nostril. Unlike the closed rhinoplasty, these incisions allow the doctor to separate out the correct anatomical components of the nose, which provides a complete visualization of the nose.

Thereafter, the surgeon can inspect the nose framework and implement accurate correction of its shape and structure.

Changing the shape of the tip of the nose

In patients with a wide tip of the nose, so-called nose “potato”, the doctor may remove part of the alar cartilages. Depending on the structure of the nose and the necessary intervention, the surgeon uses sutures to tilt or pull cartilages of wings inside, to achieving the narrow tip of the nose.

Open rhinoplasty provides the greatest opportunity to tip rhinoplasty was performed more efficiently.

Correction of the nose hump

It is often patients have resorted to Manchester rhinoplasty with aquiline nose. Hump is composed mainly of bone and cartilage. The doctor uses the osteotome is a tool allowing make a nasal bones are more moving for its removal.

The plastic surgeon removes portions of cartilage and bone tissue using osteotome. The result may be a so-called open vault – it is an open space between the bones of the nose.

The surgeon polishes the edges of bones with a thin surgical scalpel, and performs an osteotomy to close an open vault, by repositioning of the bones.

Closing of incisions

Upon completion of the operation incisions are usually closed with absorbable sutures. The surgeon typically sets the tampons into the nostrils for respiratory tract which are removed the next day after surgery on nose. The outer side of the nose closed with sterile tape. If during the procedure were broken the nose bone will be applied plaster splints. The use of tampons and splint promotes the healing tissue and preservation the new form.

Recovery after rhinoplasty

As with any surgery, you will experience some soreness, bruising and swelling, especially in the upper part of the face and around your eyes. These symptoms disappear within the first days after nose beauty surgery.

Prior to removal of sutures, bandages and plaster may take from five to seven days. The main swelling will subside after a few weeks, the residual swelling may persist for several months.
