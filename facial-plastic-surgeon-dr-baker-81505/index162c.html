Time and patience!
Jun 7, 2017
Hi and thanks for the question! Yes, you still have a ways to go. Swelling and bruising after rhinoplasty is very common, and the recovery time varies from patient to patient as well as technique to technique. If undergoing open rhinoplasty it may be up to 12 months before the swelling subsides. Most of the major swelling is gone by about 6 weeks, but it takes time none the less. Remember, a happy healer is a healthy healer!
