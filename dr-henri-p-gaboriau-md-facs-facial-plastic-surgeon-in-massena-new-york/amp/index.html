How long does it take to get the final result of a submentoplasty?
Post op platysma-bands.
Mar 4, 2018
It may take up to 18 months for the skin to tighten over the muscle. When I do see post op bands, I usually inject them with a Neurotoxin (Dysport is my preferred one). The reason is that I want them to be “relaxed” so the skin retract over them. Unfortunately there are cases where a surgeon has to go back at 1 1/2 to re-tighten those bands. In fact my Surgical consent for Neck lift does have a paragraph addressing the issue of recurrence of bands.  Hope it helps. 
Bump on rhinoplasty nose. What could it be?
Post op bump
Mar 4, 2018
I would for sure talk to your surgeon. it could be what we call “Bone Callus” and may need to be rasped down. It is quite common. or, if He/She used cartilage graft, the graft could have moved and may need to be removed. Hope it helps.

Jan 25, 2018

Is it possible to change your eye shape and get eyes like Neymar with plastic surgery? (Pho
Changing Eye shape.
Dec 3, 2017
I would be very careful. In general it is difficult to change someone ‘s eye shape. In fact , as surgeons, when we do perform eyelid surgery we try to avoid any changes, except to “rejuvenate” the eyes. The main issue is that if the surgery does fail, it may not be possible to “go back” to your original eye shape, and you may become quite distressed. I would consult with Experts in the field of Occuloplastic surgery.
