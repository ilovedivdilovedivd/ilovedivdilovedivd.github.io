What is Rhinoplasty?

Rhinoplasty in Plano, TX commonly known as a nose job, is a plastic surgery procedure that allows you to change the size and shape of the nose.

Nose correction allows you to correct congenital or acquired defects of the nose, the effects of trauma (blows, cuts, animal bites), autoimmune lesions, cancer and a failed primary rhinoplasty.

The procedure can be carried out:

– straighten noses with a drooping tip;
– remove the bump on the nose;
– reduce or align the nose;
– hold the nose reconstruction;
– to shorten excessively long noses;
– deviated nasal septum or a sinus condition.

Rhinoplasty is the most popular surgery in aesthetic and plastic surgery in the United States and the world.
