How long before the lumpy unevenness goes away? I am two months post op from liposuction. (Photo
Lumpiness after Liposuction
Jan 28, 2018
It takes about 3 months for 70% of the result and up to one year for the final result. Lumpiness is very common but will diminish with time. Indentations and irregularities may be the result of skin laxity from the loss of subcutaneous fat. Swelling after exercise is expected in this timeframe. Follow up with your plastic surgeon for his or her advice.  Best
Why do I have tiny bumps after a chemical peel?
