Lebanon Nose Job Price

Rhinoplasty in Lebanon is a surgical procedure aimed at eliminating congenital and acquired during the life of nasal defects: functional (the normalization of the new breathing) and aesthetic, including a complete reconstruction of the nose in case of its absence.

This procedure, without exaggeration, is included in range of the most sought procedures in the plastic surgery.

Many people resort every day all over the world to rhinoplasty, wanting to return the appeal or to improve their appearance.

What are the indications for rhinoplasty?

There are many different nasal defects, due to which people resort to rhinoplasty in the Lebanese Republic.

Indications are usually subdivided into the aesthetic and functional.

The first aimed at correcting the shape and length of the nose, and the second aimed at solving the problem a difficulty breathing through the nose.

Rhinoplasty is performed in cases of nose following defects:

– excessive length;
– large hump;
– irregular shape of the nostrils and wings;
– excessively thin or thickened tip of the nose;
– massive bridge of the nose;
– saddle nose;
– shortness of nasal breathing.

The presence of contraindications to rhinoplasty in Beirut can have a negative impact on the result of the operation, both in during its implementation and in the recovery period.

Therefore, the identification of contraindications is a very important task during planning. Among the contraindications to nose reshape surgery are distinguished absolute and relative.

  7. oncological diseases;
  8. age – nose beauty surgery can not be carried out before the age of 18 (up to that time the bones and cartilage of the nose are in the process of formation);

  1. the presence of the active phase of herpes infection;

How do you prepare for rhinoplasty?

Still in the planning stage, the doctor and the patient need to have an idea of what the result will surgery. During the initial consultation will assessed the nose area in different projections.

It determines the symmetry of the nostrils, the magnitude of the nasolabial angle and an appearance of nasal septum.

Apart from all these parameters is determined the presence of disordered breathing in through the nose and their causes. It is also important the condition and quality of the skin.
