Can lip filler be moved in the lip afterwards?
Moving Lip Fillers into Lip Afterward
Oct 16, 2018
Hyaluronic acid (HA) based fillers are moveable in the lip after injection, especially within the first 48 hours after treatment. Over the course of 1-2 weeks the HA fillers absorb water and are less moldable. Best, Dr. Krathen

Chemical Peel and Pimples
Oct 16, 2018
Sometimes after a chemical peel you can experience inflammation around the hair follicle. Depending on the depth of the chemical peel, and other factors, other considerations one would need to think about after a peel is the possible development of a yeast infection or even bacterial / viral follicular inflammation. Telogen Effluvium and Minoxidil, how long does shedding usually last?
Hair Loss Treatment
Oct 16, 2018
Starting minoxidil therapy (in any type of hair loss condition) can lead to a month or two of increased shedding. Telogen effluvium usually lasts 6-12 months and the use of Minoxidil may shorten the time until resolution.
