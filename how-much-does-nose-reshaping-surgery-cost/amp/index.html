What does Rhinoplasty Price

As other physicians have stated, the rhinoplasty price can vary depending on numerous factors, including:

  • Location: Physicians located in large cities have higher rent and other expenses compared with physicians in smaller cities or rural areas. Therefore, they generally charge more for a cosmetic surgery.
  • Quote: When you phone in for a quote, some practices quote only the surgeon’s fee, while others quote everything: surgeon’s fee, anesthesia fee, operating room fees, etc. It’s wise to clarify the list of included items when you phone a plastic surgery office to ask about prices.
  • Complexity: A simple rhinoplasty takes less time than a revision rhinoplasty and, therefore, may cost less. While rhinoplasty price definitely a consideration, the biggest issue, of course, is the quality of the result.

Identifying the most skilled board certified facial plastic surgeon in your area, meeting that surgeon for a consultation, and viewing as many before/after photos as possible is the best approach. To your health & beauty. (Laurence M. Milgrim, MD, Teaneck Facial Plastic Surgeon)

The Cost Varies

As with many things you can purchase, nose job cost depends on many factors. The cost of the facility (where you will have your surgery), the cost of anesthesia, and the surgeon’s fee are the three main drivers of cost.

The facility charge depends on whether the physician owns the surgery center, and the size of the market you live in. Large and small markets tend to have higher facility costs than mid-sized markets.

The cost of anesthesia usually depends on how long the procedure takes. Finally, the surgeon’s fee often depends on how many rhinoplasties the physician has performed.

The time of year can also impact the surgeon’s fee. Ask the physician how many procedures she has performed. Rhinoplasties are extremely difficult, so you want to make sure that your physician is highly experienced. Review the physician’s before and after photos, and look to see whether the physician is experienced doing rhinoplasties on people of your ethnicity.

While it is wise to understand the factors that make up the total cost, you should never choose a physician based solely on cost. (Farhan Taghizadeh, MD, Albuquerque Facial Plastic Surgeon)
