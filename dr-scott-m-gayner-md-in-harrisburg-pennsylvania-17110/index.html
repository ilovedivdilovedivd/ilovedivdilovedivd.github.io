Chin implant versus Kybella
Apr 21, 2020

What can be done to get rid of fatty lumps and jowls?
Lumps and bumps
Sep 13, 2019
The fatty tissue in your neck is generally treated with liposuction, often in the office under local anesthesia. Jowls can be lifted with surgery, tightened with non invasive techniques like Ultherapy, or camouflaged with filler in the area in front of the jowl known as the pre-jowl sulcus.
Is a SMAS facelift at 42 too young?
Anatomy dictates candidacy, not age
Sep 13, 2019
It's not an age issue when we are discussing facelifts with patients. There are 40 year olds that are appropriate candidates, and 80 year olds that aren't. An individuals candidacy relates to how much laxity exists in the cheek and neck area. The more laxity, the more we can lift that tissue. Based on your photos, you have very little apparent laxity in these areas.

SMAS techniques
Sep 13, 2019
