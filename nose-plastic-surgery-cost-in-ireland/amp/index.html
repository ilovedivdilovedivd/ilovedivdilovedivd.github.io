Nose Plastic Surgery Cost In Ireland

Rhinoplasty or nose job in Ireland is an operation that can improve the function of the nose, and its external appearance.

With the help of rhinoplasty can reduce or increase the overall size of the nose, remove a bump, to change degree of the angle between upper lip and nose, change the shape of the tip of the nose, narrow the gap between the nostrils, reduce nostrils.

Also, the plastic surgeon can perform septoplasty, surgery aimed at eliminating the the problems with breathing.

Rhinoplasty candidates

Rhinoplasty can be done for physically healthy women and men who have reached the age of majority. Too young patients are often denied carrying out of operation because it is necessary to wait until the nose will be finally formed.

Parental consent is required for minors patients, the practice has shown that patients in a later age more consciously relate to rhinoplasty.

What the patient can expect from a first consultation?

During the consultation, the doctor will listen carefully to all the wishes of the patient with respect to the nose and conducts a preoperative evaluation of the nose, the overall balance of the face. The future result of the operation can be seen with the help of computer simulation.

Where is typically conducted rhinoplasty?

Rhinoplasty is often performed in clinic of plastic surgery.

What type of anesthesia is used for rhinoplasty?

Rhinoplasty is performed under general anesthesia or under local anesthesia with a combination of sedatives.

How is rhinoplasty performed?

There are two types of such operations, open and closed rhinoplasty. The surgeon makes an incision, the skin is removed and to give the nose the desired shape.

The incision is sutured and a dressing is applied.

Open Rhinoplasty In Ireland Is The Medical Term For Reshaping Of The Nose Or Corrective Surgery Of The Nose

How Long Does a Nose Job Last?

The duration of the operation depends on the tasks facing the surgeon and can take from 40 minutes to 3 hours.

How Visible Will Rhinoplasty Scars Be?

Surgical incisions always leave scars as a result, but with a closed rhinoplasty in Ireland, they are limited to the inside of the nose and quite unvisible. With open rhinoplasty scar is barely noticeable and eventually disappears completely.

How painful is it to have rhinoplasty?

Rhinoplasty is a surgical procedure, so some discomfort experienced by almost all patients. The unpleasant sensation of pain can eliminate using analgesics.

Can I drive the car immediately after rhinoplasty?

You should not get behind the wheel after the operation. It is much better if someone will drive you home.

How Long Does It Take to recovery after Rhinoplasty?

Discomfort and pain in the area of the nose can be observed within a few days after the operation, so the doctor prescribe antibiotics. In addition, the physician may prescribe antibiotics and other drugs.

Can I play sports after rhinoplasty?

Gym, sports activities you can resume after three weeks after rhinoplasty.

What Are Potential Rhinoplasty Side Effects?

Ireland rhinoplasty may lead to some complications, as with any surgery. The first is an adverse reaction to anesthesia, infection, vascular ruptures. The risks include the fact that it is not always possible to achieve the desired result after the first surgery is sometimes required reoperation.
