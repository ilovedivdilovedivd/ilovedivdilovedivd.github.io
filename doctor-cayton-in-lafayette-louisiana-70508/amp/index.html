Latisse and Styes
Jun 5, 2018
Latisse will not cause you to get a sty. It is a sterile solution. It can, however, cause darkening of the skin along the hair follicles of the eyelashes which is harmless. Many people wear eyeliner there anyway and don’t care and some don’t experience this effect at all.
