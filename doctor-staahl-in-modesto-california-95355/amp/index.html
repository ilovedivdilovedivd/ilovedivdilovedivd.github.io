Want a sharp nose, but I am scared that I'll start to get persistent rhinitis or sinusitis?
Rhinplasty
Dec 19, 2017
It sounds like you want the tip narrowed. this almost never causes rhinitis and certainly never causes sinusitis if done correctly. The natural sinus structures and nasal membranes are not damaged at all during rhinoplasty surgery so there should be no effect on the nasal function post procedure. I recommend scheduling a consultation with an otolaryngologist or ENT who does rhinoplasty.

Best of luck!
