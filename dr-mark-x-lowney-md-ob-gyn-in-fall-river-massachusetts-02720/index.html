I had a labiaplasty and I am 4 days post op but I still can't really sit down without feeling pain.
Post-op Pain/Swelling After Labiaplasty
Apr 16, 2018
Dear Kaylasm,
It may be normal to have asymmetric pain and swelling after labiaplasty. However, it is probably advisable to take photos of these areas and send them to your surgeon just to make sure everything is OK. As you may be aware, this area is very vascular and sometimes unusual pain and swelling may be a sign of a hematoma which would need to be evacuated to prevent infection.
Mark X. Lowney, MD FACOG

What are my best options for the sagging/droopy skin above my belly button?
Saggy skin options
Mar 30, 2018
Dear Aes295,
Unfortunately there are no good non surgical options for skin laxity in the upper or lower abdomen. There are new radiofrequency and ultrasound devices that claim they can tighten skin like Thermage and Vaser for example. However, I have never seen what I would consider impressive post treatment results. That is why I still feel the standard of care to get rid of skin laxity is surgical removal, ie. tummy tuck.
Mark X. Lowney, MD FACOG
