How Do You Massage Breasts After Breast Augmentation?
Breast Massages After Surgery
Sep 29, 2014
Different surgeons have a different attitude towards massaging breasts after surgery. In our practice we advise patients to start massaging their breasts a couple days after surgery for about a minute twice a day and this for as long as they can remember to do it. A hand is placed below the breast pushing the implant up and around in the pocket and then the other hand pushes the implant down and around after releasing the pressure of the other hand.

Breast Lift vs. Breast Implants
Aug 27, 2014

Breast Development

Rhinoplasty Recovery
Rhinoplasty Recovery
Aug 27, 2014
After Rhinoplasty, a splint is placed over the nose for one week. By then most of the swelling is down and even with the remaining swelling the nose will show its new shape. At that time most people can return to work looking presentable but have to refrain from physical activity for another two weeks. Most noses take between 6-12 months to settle down and acquire their final shape.
Is it true that there is a risk of infection with ‘Tip plasty’ without changing implant?
Tip Rhinoplasty With Presence Of Dorsal Implant
Aug 26, 2014
Not necessarily. Tip rhinoplasty can be performed without disturbing the dorsal implant if you have no concern about the bridge of your nose.Replacing the dorsal implant with an L shaped tip and dorsal implant is another possibility. If your body has already tolerated your dorsal implant, the possibility of infection after tip plasty is minimal.
