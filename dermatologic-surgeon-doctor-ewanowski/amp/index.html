To what extent does Retin-A actually transform the top layer of skin and clogged pores?
Retin-A
Mar 27, 2014
Skin is constantly renewing
itself, shedding dead skin and replacing it with new skin. Retin-A (retinoic
acid) works at the cellular level to increase cell turnover, thus exfoliating
the skin at a faster rate. This unclogs the pores, tightens the skin, and fades
dark spots. This is why retin-a is used for both acne and anti-aging.
