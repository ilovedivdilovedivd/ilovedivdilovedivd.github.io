The masseter muscles are very powerful muscles used to grind and chew food. Typically 30-50units of botox (25units each side) is used to weaken the muscle, and possible more to shrink the muscle. The effects of botox can be cumulative, thus you may need to have a second set of injections to see the optimum result. Good luck
