Purposes of carrying out of rhinoplasty

Rhinoplasty is a necessary in cases where the human has a acquired or congenital deformity of the nose.

Surgeons, on whose shoulders lie down a rhinoplasty is carried out on the bone and cartilage parts of the nose.

Most often, patients are treated in the hope of change, such as the deformation of the nose due to injury, a short or a long nose, hump, deviated septum, too wide nose and other.

Some patients need not only in rhinoplasty, but in the septoplasty is a procedure, that restores normal nasal breathing. Nasal septum deviation, enlarged turbinates and similar deformations can lead to discomfort.

The purpose of this operation is to obtain the desired shape, with the synchronous recovery of natural functions of the nose.

Types of rhinoplasty

To date, there are three types of rhinoplasty:

  1. an open;
  2. closed;
  3. non-surgical.

Open rhinoplasty: the nose can be restored after heavy injuries, congenital, and previously performed operations on the nose that need complex recovery. The disadvantage of this procedure is a visible scar on the columella (bridge between the nostrils) of the nose.

Perfect Nose Shape In Atlanta Can Vastly Improve The Self-confidence

Professionalism of specialists in Atlanta allows to perform a closed rhinoplasty. It is the most common kind of rhinoplasty, but also the most complex, because each manipulation is performed directly in the nasal cavity.

On the other hand, closed rhinoplasty is characterized by a shortened period of recovery and less risk of injury to tissue. Their healing takes place as possible operatively and, most importantly, does not leave visible scars.

Non-surgical rhinoplasty in Atlanta is perform in case of insignificant defects of the nose using a variety of injections administered directly under the skin.

This type of rhinoplasty is attractive because minimizes the inflammatory response and virtually eliminates the postoperative period. However, the correction in this case will be very effective in the short period of time and is not for everyone.
