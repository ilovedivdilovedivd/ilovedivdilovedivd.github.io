What is the worst part of Rhinoplasty/ Septoplasty regarding pain?
Post op rhinoplasty pain
Jun 10, 2014
It is unusual to have any significant discomfort following rhinoplasty surgery. Make sure to follow all of your surgeons instructions carefully both before and after the surgery.
Can I Wear Contacts After Rhino?
Rhinoplasty and Contact Lenses
Sep 30, 2013
Contact lenses are fine to wear immediately after your rhinoplasty. You are correct about glasses. I request that my patients not wear glasses for 6 weeks following their surgery.
I am 14 days after doing rhinoplasty. Skin on my noes is so dry and hard? Can I know why?
Post Rhinoplasty Skin Care
Sep 6, 2013
The skin is dry as a result of the nose being taped and casted for one week following surgery. Restart your normal skin care which should include a cleanser and a moisturizer. If ask my rhinoplasty patients to refrain from facials and microdermabrasion treatments for a period of 6 weeks following their surgery.
Rhinoplasty; Pressure Necrosis?
Silastic Implant Used for Rhinoplasty
Aug 28, 2013
Silastic (silicone) implants have been used in augmentation rhinoplasty for decades. It is critical during their placement that minimal pressure be placed on the overlying skin – especially in the tip. Excessive pressure can lead to skin breakdown and/or implant extrusion over time. Is This Normal Swelling and Bruising from Ear Cartilage Harvest 5 Days Post Op?
Cartilage and Rhinoplasty
Aug 27, 2013
You could expect to have some amount of discomfort and bruising after having the ear cartilage harvested for the rhinoplasty but extreme pain and extensive bruising is not normal. Go see your surgeon.
Will Anyone Be Able to Make my Nose Look Like my Morphed After-photos?
Rhinoplasty – Computer Imaging
Aug 22, 2013
The imaging that you performed looks very nice. Dorsal reduction, tip deprojection and rotation. From the photos, I can’t appreciate the tip narrowing, but this is also a commonly performed component of rhinoplasty surgery.
