Am I a candidate for a ThermiTight even though I had Eliquis for a previous blood clot and Atri
ThermiTight Candidacy With Blood Thinners
Oct 1, 2014
ThermiTight is a minimally invasive procedure that involves placing a probe underneath the skin to heat up the underlying tissue. This does require making a very small incision to insert the probe. My preference would be to stop the medication if your primary physician is comfortable with this, otherwise, you may expect more bruising during the recovery. So even while on blood thinners you are still a candidate for the procedure, you just may have more bruising while healing.
