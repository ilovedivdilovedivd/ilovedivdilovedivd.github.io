Why do I have "lump" on my forehead after having Dysport injections 10 hours ago?
Lumps on forehead after Dysport
Aug 17, 2017
Dysport (like Botox) is dissolved in bacteriastatic saline. It always leaves a little lump upon injection. The fluid is reabsorbed by the body within 24 hours. Your injector may have dissolved the neurotoxin in a large volume of saline which mean each injection would have more fluid to be absorbed. I bet the lumps are gone by now.
Clifton Smith, M.D.

Brown spots on my face, what can I do?
BroadBandLight (BBL) Treatment for Dark Spots
Jun 26, 2017
In my practice the most effective laser to treat sunspots on the face has been the Sciton broad band light (BBL). It does a wonderful job of consistently lightening brown spots while making the skin look younger. This laser treatment has minimal downtime. Most patients are slightly pink and puffy for 1-2 days and then they’re back to normal. It normally takes 2-3 treatments for full correction of brown spots.
Will drinking Alcohol after my Halo Fractional Laser Treatment affect the healing process?
Drinking Alcohol After Halo Treatment
Jun 16, 2017
Drinking alcohol immediately after having the halo resurfacing treatment should not negatively affect your overall outcome. Drinking alcohol can make you temporarily dehydrated and could possibly slow down healing time but should not affect your overall outcome. Best of luck with your results!
How many treatments are recommended for the Halo laser?
Recommend Amount of Halo Treatments
Jun 16, 2017
Good question. Most patients, especially young patients like you, have beautiful results with one halo resurfacing treatment. There are several patients that love the results so much that they choose to have a second treatment 6-12 months after their first. A evaluative consultation with your provider will be able to determine how many treatments would be needed based on your skin type. Best of luck!
