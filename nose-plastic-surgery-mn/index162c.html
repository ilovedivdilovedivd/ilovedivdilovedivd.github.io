You want to have a perfect nose? There is nothing impossible for a modern plastic surgery. Today is not a problem to correct the shape of the nose, remove a small hump or to correct the nasolabial angle.

Modern rhinoplasty in Minnesota can help solve all these problems. Modern rhinoplasty is able to correct defects such as:

– a long nose;
– large nostrils;
– expressive hump on the nose;
– curvature of the nose,
– deviated septum;
– thickened tip of the nose;
– drooping tip of the nose,
– saddle-shaped nose,
– nose deformity resulting from trauma.

There are no special restrictions for rhinoplasty in MN, but the operation is not desirable to carry out till the moment final formation of the face.

In girls, this process ends with in 18-20 years, for boys later. For those who postpones trip to the plastic surgeon for fear of the appearance of scars, we hasten to inform you that for this there is no reason.

Minnesota modern plastic surgery is increasingly use so-called closed rhinoplasty (except in cases of large-scale correction).

This method provides a small incisions in each nostril. Thus, scars are not remain on the outside of the nose, and in the scars eventually become almost invisible and their can be difficult to discern even for experienced specialist.

What is Involved in Rhinoplasty Surgery?

1. Preparation

At the preparatory stage, a specialist in the first place will evaluate your nose, discuss with you the possible options for correction of the shortcomings.

Also during the interview an experienced doctor must tell you about the factors that may affect the course of the nose operation, for example, the structure of the nasal bones and cartilage, the shape of the face, skin thickness and age.

In the course of further consultation being done a series of images, as well as X-rays of the paranasal sinuses. This is necessary for optimum plan of nose surgery.

What is required from the patient?

Rhinoplasty In Saint Paul MN Before And After

The patient should sure to inform your doctor if there was a surgery or nasal trauma, allergies or breathing problems.

It should also discuss issues related to smoking, and taking drugs. If your doctor deems it necessary, within 2 – 3 days before the nose reshape surgery you should take their prescribed medications.

2. Carrying out operations

The whole operation takes 1.5 to 2 hours, depending on the complexity of the correction. Also, the specifics of surgery depends on the defect.

During nose reshaping, local anesthesia is used in combination with local infiltration anesthesia.

Rhinoplasty with a pronounced a small hump nose or deformation in any part of the nose in the direction of the main axis provides the following professional action. At the beginning of the operation incision is made in the mucosa of the nose.

Then, the skin and the mucous membrane is peeled, opening access to the “hump”. The surgeon performs accurate sawing of the protruding portion of the nasal bones and fixes them.

The bandage is applied after the plastic surgery of nose, which prevents displacement of the cartilages and nasal bones. The bandage is removed after 8-10 days after rhinoplasty, simultaneously with the removal of stitches.

When correcting the tip of the nose, the surgeon removes part of the subcutaneous fat through a neat incisions in the nasal mucosa.

If necessary, the position of some cartilages in this area is also be corrected.

The postoperative period

After surgery to stop bleeding in your nose are entered cotton swabs for 1-5 days. A emplastic bandage is applied on the nose with the addition of plaster casts. It is removed after 7-10 days.

The first ligation being done on the next day after surgery. It is recommended physiotherapy for faster recovery. It is also better to refrain from wearing glasses for 2-4 weeks after nose job in Minnesota.

As practice shows, you will be able to return to everyday life after 5-7 days after nose beaty surgery. Preliminary results can be assessed after 1.5 months, the final – after 1 – 1.5 years.
