When to Stop Drinking Alcohol Prior to Surgery?
Alcohol and Rhinoplasty
Aug 4, 2015
After performing over 3500 Rhinoplasties I have found that stopping alcohol 5-7 days before and 7 days after is the safest for the day of surgery to minimize blood loss and speed up recovery afterwards.
Hit on the nose 4 weeks post rhino-septoplasty. Should I go to see a doctor?
Go have your surgeon take a look at your nose
Jul 12, 2015
Complete healing after rhinoplasty surgery will take several months including the swelling that fluctuates up to 1 year after the procedure. absolutely it is my recommendation that you go visit the surgeon who performed your surgery so he can examine if the trauma has had any sort of impact on the procedure. the suture may have to be removed if it is exposed and maybe some form of nasal tapping be of help to reduce some resulting swelling from the hit.
