Is It Possible That With Age, My Nose Is Growing Larger?
Nose Can Enlarge with Age
May 8, 2017
Patients frequently ask me if their nose will continue to grow as they get older.  The nose can appear to become bigger as patients age, however, it is unusual for the nose to actual grow after reaching adulthood. Rather, the support structures of the nose tend to loosen or weaken and the nose tip can droop, which makes the nose appear to look bigger. Additionally, changes in skin texture - and in particular skin thickening - can also make the nose look large as patients age.
