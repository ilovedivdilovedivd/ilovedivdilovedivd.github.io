Rhinoplasty Miami FL Price

Miami rhinoplasty is one of the most common plastic surgeries. The operation consists in changing the external contour by deleting, or moving bone and cartilage of the framework of the nose.

Usually, rhinoplasty is performed after 18 years of age when completed the formation of the skeleton. Operations are conducted by open and closed access, after which the scars are not remain.

The plastic surgery on nose is performed under local or general anesthesia. The duration of the nose job is approximately 1.5 hours.

The cast or thermoplastic splint is applied on the face for 7-10 days and tampons are installed in the nose to stop the bleeding, which are removed after 1-3 days.

Hospital stay is 3 to 10 days. Varying degrees of swelling and bruising around the eyes are always reduced by 90% within two weeks. Immediate results can be estimated after 3 months, and the final result – after 1 year, when the formation of the nose tissue will be completed.

Septoplasty

Septoplasty is performed on the nasal septum with the aim of correction, but with full preservation of cartilages and bone tissue. This type of surgery is used if necessary to combine a rhinoplasty and septoplasty for restoration of nasal breathing.

Secondary rhinoplasty is a separate procedure for its implementation is necessary not only eliminate the problems after the first intervention, and not create new ones. There are several types of rhinoplasty. There are two surgical access: closed and open.

Before And After Miami Nose Reshaping Surgery

Open rhinoplasty allows to carry out complex and profound changes in the shape of the nose. The surgeon makes an incision measuring approximately 5 mm at the lower part the skin of the nasal septum. It is through this incision occur all manipulations with your nose, removal of cartilage and bone fragments, their displacement, detachment of soft tissues and so on.

After an open rhinoplasty in Miami remains a small external scar, which is practically invisible after 2-3 weeks. Of course, this method has its advantages and plastic surgeon chooses this method during complex operations.

Closed rhinoplasty method allows to carry out some of the standard options for rhinoplasty. Incisions are made inside the nasal passages, in each nostril. It turns out that they are not visible from the outside. After healing the scar from the nose beauty surgery is not visible even at survey an expert.

Nose Plastic Is Done By Reshaping Nasal Skeleton

However, this method often does not allow to fully change the shape of the nose and has limited indications. It is difficult to change the shape of the cartilage blindly, which have a thickness of less than 1mm. The plastic surgeon must necessarily possess all methods of rhinoplasty. Choice of access is performed individually in each case.

Elimination of saddle nose deformation

Correction defects or deformities of the septum

Recovery after rhinoplasty

Miami Plastic Surgery Rhinoplasty For Man Before And After

After nose reshaping, on the patient’s nose is applied plastic retainer. Its should be worn for 10 days. Turundy are inserted into the sinuses for proper tissue healing and prevention of bleeding, which will be removed after 2 days after rhinoplasty.

The postoperative period does not bring discomfort to the patient, except to the need to breathe through the mouth (1-2 days). With extensive intervention is acceptable appearance of bruising around the eyes, which disappear with the removal of the plaster bandage.

A physiological edema decreases completely within a month, however, a pronounced puffiness will be less noticeable in a week.
