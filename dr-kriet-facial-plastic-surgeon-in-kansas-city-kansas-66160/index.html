Nostril reshaping. Can an alar rim graft and an alar base reduction be done at the same time?
Alar rim graft and alar base reduction
Apr 15, 2017
In answer to your question, both alar rim graft and alar base reduction can be performed during the same rhinoplasty. An alar rim graft can correct weaknesses of the alar rim or can help to support a deep soft tissue triangle. The alar base reduction should be conservative because once performed, the tissue cannot be put back.
Best wishes!
I am 10 days post septorhinoplasty and can’t open my mouth fully. How long until this resolves
Upper lip after rhinoplasty
Apr 12, 2017
It is very common for the upper lip to have decreased elevation with smiling after rhinoplasty. This is particularly true after working on the nasal tip. It relates to swelling in the area and generally starts to improve within a few weeks. I typically ask my patients to avoid elective dental appointments for 4-6 weeks postop for this reason. I would suggest that you ask your surgeon for their particular recommendations.
Best wishes!
Can people with myopia have rhinoplasty?
Myopia and rhinoplasty
Apr 12, 2017
There are no contraindications to rhinoplasty based on the degree of nearsightedness. Changing the dorsal profile does not worsen myopia or change the vision. Seek out an in-office consultation with an experienced rhinoplasty surgeon to discuss your concerns.
Best wishes!
Damage done to nose by basketball after rhinoplasty?
Damage to nose after hit by basketball
Apr 12, 2017
Sorry to hear about your injury. It is possible for an impact to the nose to cause either a fracture (break) or shifting of the nasal septum. Sometimes the blow will cause the septum to slide off of the crest of bone that it normally rests upon. I would recommend that you have an evaluation with your rhinoplasty surgeon or another board certified rhinoplasty specialist to determine the extent of injury.
Best wishes!
More confused than ever about whether stitches should be dissolvable or permanent?
Choice of suture material
Apr 11, 2017
The choice of suture material for manipulating cartilage during a rhinoplasty is highly variable depending on the surgeon and the techniques being used. Really, it is a non-issue since extrusion of permanent sutures is so rare. It is more important that you have trust in your surgeon’s ability and a good rapport than stress over the fine technical details of your operation.
Best wishes!

Medications for rhinoplasty
