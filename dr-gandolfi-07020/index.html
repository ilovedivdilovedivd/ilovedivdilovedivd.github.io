Am I a good candidate for a mini tummy tuck to remove extra skin on my lower belly?
full tummy tuck
Jun 1, 2022
Hi,for most plastic surgeons, the line between a mini tummy tuck and a full is whether or not a diastasis needs addressing. A mini tummy tuck removes excess skin below the belly button. A standard tummy tuck removes excess skin below belly button and addresses the diastasis to the level of the xiphoid. It looks like you have a diastasis and therefore a full tummy tuck would be more beneficial.

How much of my stretch marks will remain after I get a tummy tuck?
most above the belly button remain
Jun 7, 2021
Hi,
When a tummy tuck is performed, all the extra skin is removed. The exact amount depends on your individual body and the techniques used. Most times, all skin above the belly button is kept. Therefore, whatever stretch marks you have above your belly button will still be there, but they will be in a much lower position. For instance, the skin that is right now above your belly button will now be right above your vagina. I hope that helps!
