Dec 15, 2020
MysteriousWombat, Great name by the way. Yes it is possible to augment and/or reposition the columella although that is seldom the only thing done during a rhinoplasty. nose
Oct 2, 2020

Should I do a lip lift/corner lift or rhinoplasty first?
nose/lip lift
Jun 2, 2020
Thriving3471, you should not have a lip lift at the same time as a rhinoplasty. Too much swelling from the nose surgery will make the incision from the lip lift not heal well. Does not matter which one you do first, depends on your budget, what bothers you the most and expected healing time. See an experienced plastic surgeon that specializes in only faces. Make sure they have a lot of good photos. See the video and good luck!
