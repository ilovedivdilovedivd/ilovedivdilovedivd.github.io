Is IPL Effective on Light Colored Hair
Jan 31, 2018
IPL is attracted to pigment, like that of darker hair found on the face, arms legs and other areas. Blonde, white and gray hair doesn't have enough pigment to attract the light or heat of the laser. This makes traditional laser hair removal on lighter colored hair unreliable.
