Is it safe to use Botox on the neck area? Platysmal bands
A Beautiful Neck Lift
Aug 16, 2022
It is safe to inject botox in the platysmal bands in the neck. This relaxes the pulling on the neck and helps the muscle to lay down again. There is a risk that if injected into the wrong muscle group it could make the neck flexers weak and the muscles that control swallowing weak. It takes many injections and the amount varies as to how many neck bands that you are treating. Good luck!

Absolutely botox can help!

The lines that are formed around your eyes is from the contraction of the obicularis oculi muscle. This muscle is responsible for squeezing your eyes shut. If you paralyze this muscle in this area, you will be unable to make those creases. This will prevent static wrinkles from developing. This will help you to age gracefully. Good luck to you!
