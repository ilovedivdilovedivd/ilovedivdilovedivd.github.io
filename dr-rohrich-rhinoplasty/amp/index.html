Male rhinoplasty

Apr 30, 2019
You have realistic goals but the key is for you to seek a board certified plastic surgeon or otorhinolaryngologist with experience and expertise in African American rhinoplasty.  Typically the skin is thicker and the nose is broader.  Ask to see before and after photos of their own patients.
