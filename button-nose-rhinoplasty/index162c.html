Button nose rhinoplasty

Rhinoplasty for Button Nose

Button nose rhinoplasty surgery

Button nose rhinoplasty for your nose will make a visible difference in its shape but it is not realistic to expect that a button nose shape will result from the surgery. Your amount of nasal skin will not allow that to happen, thus your nose can not be made as small as you would like. You need to have computer imaging done with realistic results so you can determine if the changes that are possible are worth it to you. (Barry L. Eppley, MD, DMD, Indianapolis Plastic Surgeon)

Would a Rhinoplasty Be Worth It? Yes and No

Rhinoplasty is a quality of life issue. Is your nose big enough to make a rhinoplasty worth it?.

Surgery to correct button nose rhinoplasty

It depends. Can it be made smaller? Yes. But that’s not the most important question. The real issue is, ” is your nose bothering you enough that if it was smaller, would it improve your quality of life enough to make it worth it”. The bad news is that you are the only person that can answer this question. The good news is that there are some good ways to help you figure out the answer to this question.

The best next step would be to make an appointment with an experienced rhinoplasty surgeon who includes computer imaging as a part of the consultation. This way you will be able to see what actually can be done, which will go a long way towards helping you decide if a rhinoplasty will be worth it. (Michael R. Menachof, MD, Greenwood Village Facial Plastic Surgeon)
