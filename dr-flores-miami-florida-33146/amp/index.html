Are over the muscle implants for reconstruction post mastectomy in the skin that was radiated 9 years a
Over the muscle implants post mastectomy
May 7, 2018
It is not advisable to get implants over the muscle after having had radiation 9 years prior. Implants should never be put under damaged skin. By putting the implant under the muscle the implant is protected and can provide a collagen sling. The implant over the muscle would make that damaged skin even thinner severely increasing your chances of complications.
