Poland Nose Surgery

Rhinoplasty in Poland is a plastic surgery aimed at surgical correction of the size and shape of the nose.

Rhinoplasty allows the make the shape of the nose attractive, harmonious with face, in the correct proportions, reduce or enlarge the nose, giving it volume and create a beautiful nose tip.

Many people at least once in life think about the need for rhinoplasty. In fact, the shape of the nose plays a significant role in its appearance and life.

The nose is the center of the face. And, first of all, when we look at a person’s face, the first thing we notice is the shortcomings of the nose.

The shape of the nose affects all the other parts of the face such as the cheeks, lips, eyes. Rhinoplasty is a plastic surgery that helps correct the defects of the nose, acquired or congenital.

Through a complex operation, the surgeon can correct the shape of the nose tip of the nose, the nose wings.

The nose after rhinoplasty will have the form that the patient wants. Most often, people choose a small, straight nose and a thin. A notable feature is the tip of the nose. It is better to do a bit of a snub.

Unfortunately, with age, the soft tissues lose their former elasticity and the tip is lowered. But rhinoplasty will help to cope with this.

Rhinoplasty in Poland is performed with open or closed method. Closed technique is performed through the nostrils. With open rhinoplasty incision is made under the nose and raised tissue. So the surgeon to work much more convenient. The scar is almost invisible after rhinoplasty.

The nose curvature is possible to correct by means the extracorporeal septoplasty: nasal septum is removed and the doctor corrects it, and then, already prepare put in place.

Methods of rhinoplasty will be depends on the tasks facing the surgeon and the conditions. Surgeries are performed in our clinic under anesthesia.

Within 10 days of is usually used external fixation (plaster) bandages and intranasal tampons. Duration of hospital stay is usually 1-2 days. In the first days after the nose job there is a pronounced swelling, both outside and inside, which is manifested nasal congestion.

Contraindications to rhinoplasty

  1. Severe diseases of internal organs;
  2. diabetes;
  3. Coagulation failure;
  4. mental disorders;
  5. Inflammatory diseases and rhinitis;
  6. Age up to 16 years, because at this age nose still is in the process of formation of bone and cartilage tissue (with the exception of post-traumatic deformations and congenital defects.)
