Rhinoplasty Cost In United Kingdom

Rhinoplasty in United Kingdom is a facial cosmetic procedure, usually performed to enhance the appearance or reconstruct the nose.

Preparing for rhinoplasty surgery begins with a consultation. To facilitate understanding between the patient and the doctor there is computer simulation.

Taking into account the individual characteristics of the patient and his or her wishes, the doctor creates a computer model of the nose, on which he can be guided during the rhinoplasty.

Thus, with the help of computer simulation, the patient and the doctor come to a consensus before the rhinoplasty surgery. This is very important, because patients can not always explain what changes they expect.

At the same time, you should always understood that achieve identity, as on a model, is impossible due to the individual characteristics of the organism.

Improving the shape of the nose can be associated with the improvement of the respiratory function.

Plastic surgeons in clinics always strive not only to improve the shape of the nose, but also to restore nasal breathing, if necessary.

Sometimes, during the nose correction is necessary to correct the nasal septum (septoplasty) or perform resect of new turbinates (turbinotomy).

Thus aesthetic rhinoplasty is combined with functionality. Before the nose beauty surgery, the doctor evaluates the patient’s nasal breathing.

Besides breathing a doctor evaluates the patient’s skin. Thick and oily skin is more prone to scarring. Nose, created by the surgeon, is not always the same after plastic surgery of nose. Much depends on the skin type, age, individual features of healing and scarring.

UK rhinoplasty is performed from 18 years, but if there nose deformity due to injury, then before adulthood. The shape of the nose changes throughout life. So sometimes it needs rejuvenating rhinoplasty. Most often, its aim is to raise the tip of the nose.

In preparation for nose reshaping, the doctor collects all information about previous surgical interventions on the nose, the transferred upper respiratory tract infections, allergic reactions and problems with nasal breathing.

If a patient requests a correction after a primary rhinoplasty, the surgeon performs a thorough inspection to determine possibility of re-rhinoplasty.

Preparing for rhinoplasty from the patient includes:

  1. discontinuance of the use of all drugs containing salicylates or others, that affect blood clotting;
  2. discontinuance of the use of hormonal drugs for 10-14 days before nose job;
  3. you need to stop smoking for 2 weeks before the operation, and refrain from smoking for at least 2-3 weeks after rhinoplasty;

Women need to take into account the appointment of rhinoplasty not in a menstrual days.

Contraindications to rhinoplasty as for any surgery, are associated with diseases such as diabetes mellitus, diseases of the cardiovascular system, liver, kidneys, blood clotting, inflammation.

If the patient is taking any medication, he should inform the doctor. If no contraindications to nose plastic surgery, and the patient is completely healthy, then the day of the operation is appointed.

In the preoperative period, the patient passes all the necessary tests (blood tests, urine tests, glucose, ECG, fluorography of the chest), and draws up an agreement with the clinic.

Before rhinoplasty in United Kingdom, the patient consults with an otolaryngologist and anesthesiologist. Rhinoplasty is a surgery, therefore carried out under general or local anesthesia. The duration of the operation is an average of 1.5 – 3 hours (depending on complexity).

Plastic surgeons clinic mastered modern techniques of plastic surgery. To minimize trauma and reduce the postoperative period, our surgeons use micro tools in rhinoplasty. Using microtools increases time of the operation, but allows to achieve better accuracy.

After nose job in UK, plaster or emplastic bandage is applied on the face for 7-10 days, and to stop bleeding (or in order to fix nose skeleton in the right position) tampons or special silicone plate is inserted into the nose (“splint”), which removed after 1-3 days.
