Is Rhinoplasty covered by health insurance?
Rhinoplasty and insurance
Mar 10, 2015
Nasal surgery that improves breathing, such as a septoplasty, is generally covered by medical insurance. And if your nose has been misshapen by an accident or trauma, surgery to fix the nose can be covered. However, a purely cosmetic bump/hump on the nose that is not the result of trauma or injury and that is not affecting breathing, is generally not covered by insurance companies.Kind regards.
