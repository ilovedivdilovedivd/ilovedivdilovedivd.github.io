pointy tip?
Aug 16, 2017
It is difficult to give you precise recommendations on what would need to be done to improve the appearance of your nose with the angles you have presented on these photos. That said, a long nose is usually due to a lack of definition between the dorsum and the tip which can be created by reducing the height of the cartilage and bone of the dorsum and then manipulating the tip so that it is in proper proportion, projection, and rotation relative to the rest of your nose and face.

I’m super confused RE: septorhinoplasty infection prevention? I often suffer from impetigo, cystiti
infections
Aug 16, 2017
Infections after septorhinoplasty are extremely rare, but I think most surgeons will give antibiotics and do a standard skin prep before your surgery. A short course of antibiotics as a precaution are often given in the immediate post operative period. It is most likely an abundance of precaution, but better safe than sorry. The downside of doing these things are negligible and side effects from antibiotics are easy to resolve.
