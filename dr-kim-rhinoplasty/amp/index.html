During tip Rhinoplasty will there be less swelling?
Swelling after rhinoplasty
Feb 6, 2016
Hello,After rhinoplasty to refine the tip of the nose, there will be swelling. Patients of Hispanic, Asian, African-American heritage have tendency for thicker skin/soft tissue envelope. It is important to recognize patients with thick skin/soft tissue versus the thin skin/soft tissue prior to the surgery. Patients should be counseled prior to the surgery whether they would have longer period of post op swelling, and whether they will be followed closely after the surgery…
