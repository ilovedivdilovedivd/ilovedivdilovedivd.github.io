Rhinoplasty of the wings

This operation makes it possible to remove the asymmetry of the nostrils, to correct the length, width and thickness the nose wings.

Indications for this operation are:

– too wide or long the nose wings;
– the asymmetry of the nose wings;
– thickening of the skin in the area of the wing cartilage and nostrils;

Operation on the wings of the nose may seem relatively easy, since it does not affect the functional departments of the nose. However, the result has to be impeccable from an aesthetic point of view, so requires experience and skill of the surgeon.

Defects in the nose wings area may be congenital or acquired as a result of burns, neoplastic diseases in the facial area, as well as plastic surgery unsuccessful in the past. Sometimes the need for this operation occurs after rhinoplasty the tip. Correction is performed by cutting the tissues in different ways depending on the nature of the existing defects.

Postoperative scars in the nasolabial folds area and creases the nose wings will becomes completely unnoticeable after a certain time. If you need to restore the wings of the nose after an injury, the operation is considerably complicated.

In this case it is necessary to use surrounding tissue or to transplant patient’s own cartilage, for example from the nasal septum or the auricle.

How to prepare for the surgery?

What should you pay attention? The success of the nose reshaping depends on the preparatory period. It is recommended to stop the solarium, prolonged sun bathing and taking medications blood thinning for a few weeks before surgery. Such as aspirin, Aspekard, for example.

If you smoke, then during the preparation period is desirable to reduce cigarette consumption to a minimum. It is better to stop smoking. It is best to abstain from alcohol. It can provoke the swelling and bleeding.
