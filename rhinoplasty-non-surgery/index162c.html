Rhinoplasty non surgery as an alternative

It seems you’ve already done your research and realize that placing filler into your nose makes it bigger.

Based on the photo, it seems your nose is already somewhat big for your face. If you feel a smoothing of the profile with filler at the radix will be worth the increase in overall size, then it is reasonable.

A hyaluronic acid filler would be advised – it is safer and will go away. You should seek two or three opinions.

Other views of your nose, and an examination might indicate that a nasal filler is not a good idea.

You might also decide after consultation that a rhinoplasty is the best way to go. (Douglas J. Mackenzie, MD, Santa Barbara Plastic Surgeon)

Rhinoplasty or non-surgical rhinoplasty

In your case, I would recommend against a nonsurgical rhinoplasty. You are an excellent candidate for rhinoplasty to decrease your dorsal hump, refine and deproject your tip. (Josh Surowitz, MD, Raleigh-Durham Facial Plastic Surgeon)

Rhinoplasty Candidate

You appear to be an excellent candidate for Rhinoplasty Surgery. Your nose is too large for your face. Taking the hump down with a few other tweeks would give you a pretty feminine nose.

You would be wasting your money with fillers. (Robert E. Zaworski, MD, Atlanta Plastic Surgeon)
