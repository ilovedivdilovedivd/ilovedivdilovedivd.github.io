Doctors

Olga Bachilo, MD, FACS

Name: Olga Bachilo, MD, FACS
Statement: Board-certified plastic surgeon Dr. Olga Bachilo founded Glamour Plastic Surgery and Medspa in Bellaire, Texas, to help each of her patients feel confident and as glamorous as a celebrity. “I want to empower women to achieve their personal best by helping them feel more comfortable with their appearance,” she says. “This newly gained confidence will aid them in the workplace as well as their personal life.”

During every cosmetic and reconstructive surgery she performs, Dr. Bachilo utilizes her lifelong love for science and her discerning artistic eye to deliver a beautiful, natural-looking result.

Dr. Bachilo’s talent with a scalpel isn’t the only reason patients book her for plastic surgery in Houston, Texas. She’s also known for being incredibly warm and engaged with every patient. “It’s my pleasure to hold their hand throughout the entire process—from the moment we meet to the moment they are ready to reveal their transformation to the world,” she says.

Her patients have said that she makes them feel heard, respected, and informed, which is always her goal. “I want to make sure they understand their options, have realistic expectations, and above all, are comfortable with their choice and excited about the procedure,” she says.

Dr. Bachilo was born in Minsk, Belarus, and moved to the United States when her father, an accomplished chemist, began work at the Texas Medical Center. This inspired Dr. Bachilo to pursue a bachelor’s degree in bioengineering and biochemistry from Rice University. She then received her medical degree from Texas A&M Health Science Center College of Medicine, where she discovered her love for her craft. “While doing my rotations, nothing attracted me more than plastic surgery,” she recalls. “I loved that feeling of instant gratification and the immediate transformation patients would see, even before the final results post-healing.”

She completed a six-year plastic surgery residency at the University of Texas Medical Branch in Galveston, rotating through multiple highly respected hospitals and private cosmetic surgery practices across Texas before opening her own practice and medspa in 2017. She is board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery.

In her spare time, Dr. Bachilo uses her surgical skills to give back to the community and has traveled internationally on mission trips to perform cleft lip and palate surgeries on children in need. At home in Houston, she spends time with her husband, young daughter, and dog (a Shiba Inu named Kito!), and she enjoys cooking and crafting.
