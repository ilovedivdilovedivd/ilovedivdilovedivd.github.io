Is There Any Way to Get Rhinoplasty Covered by Insurance?
Rhinoplasty and Insurance Coverage
Nov 20, 2018
Cosmetic rhinoplasty is not covered by insurance. However, if there are functional issues such as difficulty breathing through your nose as a result of a deviated septum or nasal valve collapse, then those portions of the procedure may be covered by insurance. Depending on your insurance, you would then only be responsible to pay for the cosmetic portion of the procedure, including the anesthesia and facility time.

Can I have a lip lift 8 weeks after a closed rhinoplasty?
Lip lift after closed rhinoplasty
Aug 27, 2018
Every surgeon has their own preferences but I do not see any contraindication to having a lip lift 8 weeks after closed rhinoplasty. The incisions and areas of dissection for a closed rhinoplasty are separate from those for a lip lift. You may have prolonged swelling given the close proximity but that is not a definite. I recommend you discuss your plans with your surgeon(s) to make sure everyone is comfortable with your plan.
