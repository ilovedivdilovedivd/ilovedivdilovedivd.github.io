When can double bubble be detected?
By Dr. Alex Kim
Jan 8, 2019
Double bubble can be detected as early as 3-4 months after surgery. Typically, once swelling of the breast goes down, you will start seeing indentation above of the bottom of the breast. Eventually, as the implant drops further, indentation becomes more prominent. Once established, you will need to have surgical correction. Best of luck!
