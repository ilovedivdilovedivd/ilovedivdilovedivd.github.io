Who needs rhinoplasty?

Indication for rhinoplasty in Louisiana, or a surgical change in the shape of the nose, is the desire of the patient. However, there are absolute indications for such operations.

It is congenital and acquired deformities. For congenital deformities are pathologies such as cleft lip or cleft palate. Acquired deformities or defects are mainly a consequence of trauma.

Contraindications for this operation. It is a chronic disease such as diabetes, heart disease, liver, bronchial asthma and drug allergy.
