Can I go through with a breast augmentation if I have colonized MRSA? Worth the risk?
Breast Augmentation in the setting of MRSA exposure
Aug 17, 2023
You can definitely have a breast augmentation with a history of MRSA colonization. I use a chlorhexidine scrub in the OR to prep the skin, and use Clindamycin for the preoperative IV antibiotic and oral dose after surgery. No matter the history, there is always a very small risk on implant infection with a breast augmentation, but these precautions can reduce the risk.
