San Francisco Nose Surgery

Rhinoplasty in San Francisco is a nose reshaping and is one of the most common operations performed today in plastic surgery. Often, the structure or size of the nose is not proportionate to other facial features.

Rhinoplasty is aimed at changing its shape can correct inharmonious appearance by changing the size or shape of the nose, nostril size, or the angle between the tip of the nose and upper lip.

In addition, rhinoplasty is performed to correct congenital defects or nose injuries, as well as for the elimination of chronic respiratory problems.

It is important to understand that undergo this procedure, in contrast to the same otoplasty, patients have reached fifteen years of age or older can perform this operation, because this operation is only possible if the nose was fully and finally formed.

Before we consider the principle of rhinoplasty, you need to become familiar with its anatomical structure. The framework of the nose consists bone and cartilage tissue. The upper part of the nose is supported by bone. On the bridge of the nose may be a a small hump, which is formed of bone and cartilage components.

The lower part of the nose is supported by various cartilaginous plates that lie on either side of the center, determining the shape and type of the tip of the nose.
