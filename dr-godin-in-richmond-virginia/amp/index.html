How long must I wait after double jaw surgery to get Rhinoplasty?
How long to wait
Apr 28, 2016
The short answer is to wait until all swelling goes down and your oral surgeon clears you for more surgery. The most conservative approach is to wait a full year and then consider rhinoplasty. Subtle changes can occur during the first year after a big surgery and you want everything in your favor when you undertake a rhinoplasty. You want everything to go right so that it is your first and last nasal procedure. Best of luck to you.
