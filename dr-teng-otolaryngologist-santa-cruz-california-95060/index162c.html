When can I wear my spectacles/glasses once I’ve had my operation?
Depends on the procedure
Sep 21, 2017
Restrictions on wearing glasses typically vary depending on the procedures performed during your surgery. If the nasal bones were broken, you may need to wait until they are healed before you can wear glasses. Consult with your surgeon regarding post-op care and restrictions.
Subclinical Acne after Rhinoplasty? Has anyone else suffered through this?
Acne can occur after rhinoplasty
Sep 21, 2017
Acne can occasionally occur after rhinoplasty from an adhesive tape reaction and/or the nasal splint allowing oil to accumulate in the skin. This is temporary and will heal with time. Gentle facial cleansers may help facilitate the recovery.
