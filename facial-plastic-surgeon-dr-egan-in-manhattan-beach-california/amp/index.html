Spot near eye turned into hole

Without a picture, it is hard to accurately explain what might be going on but any lesion may heal with a depression in the skin, especially acne spots. It is best to have it looked at by a Facial Plastic Surgeon or Plastic Surgeon who can better explain your options for making it less noticeable.
My Husband Cut Behind His Ear, the Cut is About 3 Cm Long Does It Require Stitches?
Does cut need stitches?
Jan 25, 2013
A cut of that length should be examined by a doctor. If it is deep enough that the skin is separated, then stitches will most likely be required. It is important to keep the wound clean and covered in antibiotic ointment to prevent infection. Infection could spread to other places and prevent the wound from healing properly.
Latisse Effectiveness After Years of Use?
Latisse stopped working
Jan 25, 2013
There are no studies that demonstrate that Latisse stops working over time. Many patients have been using it since it came out with no decrease in the thickness or length of their lashes. Our lashes thin and become shorter with age but any dramatic change should be checked out by a doctor.
Why Did I Get an Indentation on Forehead After Botox?
Dent in Forehead after Botox
Jan 25, 2013
The dent in your forehead is not likely to be due to the Botox itself but rather due to the effect of Botox on the underlying muscles. Botox paralyzes the muscle, smoothing it out which may uncover underlying irregularities of the bone of the forehead. It should lessen as the Botox wears off.
I Went to Try on Wedding Dresses Two Weeks After Rhinoplasty. Can I Continue Doing So?
Wedding dress shopping after rhinoplasty
Jul 19, 2012
Wedding dress shopping is not a problem after rhinoplasty surgery but do take care not to overexert yourself soon after surgery. I understand your desire and need to find a dress and that waiting to shop will only increase your stress levels. Be careful with pulling dresses on over your head as your nose will be swollen and sore after surgery. Best of luck!
Botox After Browlift?
Botox for eyebrow lift
Jul 19, 2012
If it has been 2-3 months since your surgery, you are likely seeing your final result. It should not be a problem to do Botox around your crow's feet to get a lift of your lateral brow. It is always best to check first with your surgeon. It is recommended to use 10U per side in the crow's feet area.

Severe Headaches, Swollen Lip Since Botox 4 Days Ago. How Long Before These Decrease?
Swelling after Botox
Aug 25, 2011
It is not expected to have significant swelling after Botox, especially of the eyes or lip. If you had filler put in your upper lip, the swelling can last 3-4 days. Headaches are also not uncommon after Botox but should only last 1-2 days. I am concerned that you might be having an allergic reaction of some type and encourage you to make an appointment to discuss this with your surgeon as soon as possible.
Ulthera As an Alternative to Tradition Surgery?
Ulthera for neck improvement
Aug 25, 2011
Ulthera is a non-surgical alternative for improving the appearance of the neck but is not a replacement for standard surgical techniques. Surgery removes excess skin creating a permanent tightening that can be more dramatic than that with Ulthera. Ulthera has much less downtime than surgery but also has more subtle results so it is best to discuss this with a surgeon experienced with both.
Very Unhappy With Rib Cartilage Harvest Scar- Options?
Rib cartilage scar
Aug 25, 2011
There are many options for revising the scar and improving the appearance so there is no reason to be upset about it. Talk to your surgeon about surgically revising the scar to minimize the depression. Redness/purpleness of the scar will fade over time and can be improved with lasers. Hypertrophied scars can also be improved with lasers or steroid injections.
