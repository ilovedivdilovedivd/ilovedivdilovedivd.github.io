What kind of downtime can I except for dental implants? How long before I can eat and will there be
Implant downtime
Sep 12, 2019
Downtime typically depends on the number of implants being placed and the additional procedures performed, if any, such as bone grafting. Although everyone’s experience can be different downtime for a single dental implant is typically less than the extraction downtime. Typically, most are low key for a day and able to return to normal activities and a modified diet the next day.
Hope this is of help.

Will Hyaluronidase injection to dissolve Juvederm affect natural volume in my lips?
Decreasing volume
Sep 10, 2019
Hyaluronidase is difficult to control in regard to reducing volume evenly after filler placement. It will not effect the natural lip fullness. I’d consider waiting until the effects have self resolved in 9-12 months or remove the filler entirely.
