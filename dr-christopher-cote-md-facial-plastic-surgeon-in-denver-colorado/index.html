My nose is slanted and split at the tip? What can I do about it?
Changing nose
Jun 5, 2015
There can be some changes to the nose that occur over time. The skin changes, gravity has its effects and the nose can droop. Also, the tissues can thin, including the skin and fat over the nose. Many of these things can be addressed and starting with a thorough consultation with an experiences rhinoplasty surgeon will be your best first step.
