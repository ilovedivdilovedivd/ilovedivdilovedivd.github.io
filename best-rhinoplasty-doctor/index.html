Cost of services and guarantees

You Can Also Ask To Speak With Previous Patients To Hear About Their Experience

Not always the highest price guarantees the highest quality. Cost of the operation depends largely on the popularity of the plastic surgeon and the clinic’s prestige. You should not always to rely on the prestige of the clinic, because the experience of plastic surgeons who work in there, may vary considerably.

Services of the most prestigious clinics usually are more expensive (anesthesia, hospital stay, dressings, inspections), though the quality of services is not much different from the slightly less well-known plastic surgery clinics.

In the field of plastic surgery, none of the plastic surgeons can not give a 100% guarantee on the result of the nose job. There is always at least a minimal risk of the slightest, natural complications.

The reaction of the human body for surgical intervention can not be predicted at 100%. If the doctor assures you absolute guarantee this is a reason to doubt his qualifications and adequate approach to the forthcoming operation.
