Scheduled for a rhinoplasty and septoplasty in 3 weeks. General anesthesia concern.
General anesthesia is safer than driving
May 10, 2017
The death rate from general anesthesia is 1:500,000 and that includes 90 year olds with heart disease. The death rate from driving every day for a year is 1:10,000. Choose a board certified plastic surgeon and a board certified anesthesiologist and an accredited facility and, if you're over 60, have a stress test. With all of these things, the chance of a problem is very low.
