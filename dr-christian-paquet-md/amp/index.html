Fillers
Apr 26, 2018
Both dermal fillers are hyaluronic acid based, which is natural and safe to use. Their difference relates to their cross-linking which results in a different consistency for the filler. Volbella is generally used more for very subtle enhancement rather than more voluminous changes.
