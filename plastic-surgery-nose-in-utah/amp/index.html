Plastic Surgery Nose In Utah

Rhinoplasty in Utah is a surgery to change the shape of the nose and carry out at approximately in 18 years of age and older.

Unfavorable conditions for the operation created with age-related changes, in case of deterioration of skin elasticity.

Surgical intervention is considered necessary, after from the trauma suffered, which may be accompanied by a violation of nasal breathing (septum bending).

Nose surgery is performed in a single step – the restoration of breathing and changing the shape of the nose.

It should be noted that in the case of injury, at an early stage healing (first 10 days) without surgery, outpatient basis, the original location a nasal septum will recover (with lateral displacement, distortion).

Ability to restore impaired breathing nose and in other cases, changes in the shape, will determined during the inspection.

The main indication for rhinoplasty is the patient’s desire to make changes in shape – long nose, removal of a small bump , the broad base of the nasal bridge, a large tip. It is important to save and not to disrupt a nose breathing during the nose operation.

Nose plastic surgery is performed through the nostrils with closed access without external incisions.

The doctor may use an external incision at a specific form of the nose, which is almost imperceptible. A full rhinoplasty be affected all structural parts of the external nose (bone, cartilage, soft tissue). Closed access requires less time and lasts approximately 40 minutes.

Partial rhinoplasty limited to the treatment of certain structural parts of the external nose and can not affect the bone tissue. All these manipulations are performed under intravenous anesthesia or under local anesthesia.

The recovery period after rhinoplasty (nose job)

Recovery period is painless. Some discomfort persists to 1-3 days prior to the removal of tampons, which closed a nasal breathing.

Superimposed on the nose plaster cast is removed periodically for skin care. In general, you need to wear a bandage 7-10 days.

During this period decreases and the primary, most pronounced postoperative swelling.

Swelling may come and go and worsen in the morning during the first year following your rhinoplasty surgery. The final shape of the nose takes in the period from 2 to 6 months.

The recovery process may be delayed due to the individual characteristics of healing wound.

During the postoperative period, you must comply with all restrictions and temporary minor individual recommendations surgeon.
