Rhinoplasty means changing the shape of the nose.

How is rhinoplasty performed in California?

The goal of rhinoplasty is to correct the shape of the nose. This intervention can be performed under local anesthesia, but more often under general anesthesia.

Choosing the type of anesthesia for rhinoplasty depends on the patient’s preferences and the surgeon’s preference, and also on the presence of contraindications to a particular type of anesthesia and an allergy to anesthetics.

Rhinoplasty is performed via of incisions in the nasal cavity, that is, on the nose will not be any incisions and consequently lack of scars. Sometimes, incisions can be made in the columella area – skin fold between the nostrils.

The essence of rhinoplasty boils down to is that the plastic surgeon separates the soft tissues of the nose from the cartilaginous and bony structures, and only then shall corrects the shape of the nose cartilage and bone.

Sometimes it is necessary to implant a piece of cartilage tissue taken from other parts of the patient body to increase the strength of the structures of the nose, or for forming a beautiful nose tip, and to improve the respiratory function of the nose.

Surgeon must to resort to synthetic materials, in the case where the new nose impossible to form from the existing tissue.

The risk of synthetic implants for rhinoplasty is a migration or extrusion of the implant when it is displaced in the nasal tissues, which leads to a change in its shape.

As a natural material for rhinoplasty can be used cartilage of the nasal septum, the ear or rib cartilage. Usually rhinoplasty is performed on an outpatient basis, but sometimes it has to be carried out in a hospital.

Rhinoplasty takes an average of one – two hours. Today, plastic surgery is used two types of rhinoplasty open and closed rhinoplasty.

Closed rhinoplasty

Closed rhinoplasty in CA is performed via incisions inside the nasal cavity.

Thus the skin is separated from the nasal cartilage and nasal bones, which allows the surgeon to create the desired shape of the nose, remove excess cartilage, bone, or vice versa, to increase volume by implants.

To remove the protruding bone structures is used a special chisel. Incisions are sutured after changing the shape of the nose cartilage and bone.

Open rhinoplasty

This type rhinoplasty required if a large volume of nose reshape surgery. The incision is made in the folds of skin between the nostrils – columella. This incision allows more freedom for the manipulation of the nasal cavity with nasal cartilages and bones.

Nonsurgical Rhinoplasty Using Dermal Fillers

Sometimes the patient does not want to change the whole shape of the nose, and only slightly correct the it, for example, to smooth the tip of the nose, to restore its symmetry, and so on. In this case, the fillers may be used.

The risk of complications after rhinoplasty

  1. Infection of the wound and its abscess,
  2. Nose bleed,
  3. Violation of nasal breathing,
