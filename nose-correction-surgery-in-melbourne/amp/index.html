Nose Reshaping Melbourne

Rhinoplasty in Melbourne is a surgery which purpose is aesthetic nose correction.

This type of operation is in plastic surgery is the most difficult, as it requires not only the aesthetic results, but also to ensure the proper functioning of the human nose.

The main task of the surgeon during the rhinoplasty is to give your face a harmonious shape of the nose, while preserving individual facial features and all of its functional features.

Rhinoplasty is prescribed by a doctor both for medical reasons, in case of defect or injury as well as for aesthetic reasons, if the shape of the nose is not satisfactory.

Minimum age limit to perform rhinoplasty

Operations are generally carried out from 18 to 45 years. After 45 years, patients want correct the nose and age-related changes for a rejuvenating effect.

There are exceptions that involve surgery for children, if they got a serious injury, or they have post-traumatic deformity of the nose with impaired breathing through the nose.

Preparation for nose job

A few weeks before rhinoplasty is not recommended to attend bath, sauna and solarium, and take blood-thinning medications. It is advisable to refrain from smoking and drinking alcohol.

For 5-7 days before Melbourne nose reshaping, it is desirable not to use cosmetics for the face.

When rhinoplasty is needed?

Rhinoplasty is needed for the following indications:

  1. desire to change the size of the nose
  2. the presence of defects, such as large nostrils, small hump, long wings of the nose
  3. nose reshaping due to injury
  4. respiratory failure

How is nose surgery in clinic?

In hospitals, the solution to every problem is carried out individually. During the consultation the doctor will conduct a detailed examination and will discuss with you the expected effect on the basis of simulation. Next, you will be assigned a convenient day for the nose plastic surgery.

Melbourne rhinoplasty takes from 30 minutes to 2 hours, and post-operative period at the clinic will be 1-2 days. Superimposed fixing bandage are worn 3-10 days depending on the volume of nose job.

The period of recovery after nose reshaping takes up to 10 days, during which the tissue swelling may appear, which will disappear after a few days. The final effect after nose surgery is formed within 10-12 months.

In the first days after plastic surgery of the nose it is recommended to sleep only on your back with a raised headrest. It is also recommended to limit yourself in very cold / very hot foods, and avoid the physical and emotional stress to prevent nosebleeds.
