Nose bridge doesn’t look right – Any advise or tips?
shape of nose bridge after 2 nose jobs
Jan 3, 2019
Hi.  Your upper bridge is slightly too low and your lower bridge is slightly too high right above your tip creating a mild “polly beak” effect.  The answer is to raise the upper bridge and lower the lower bridge but seeing as you have had 2 rhinoplasties you may want to just settle for a non-surgical rhinoplasty to elevate the upper bridge.  But that does pose a small risk of infection to your implant.  
Had rhinoplasty 3 years ago. Tip is thick. Could a laser help?
Bulbous tip after rhinoplasty
Dec 3, 2018
HI.  No.  Lasers have no place in changing the shape of the nose.  You either have remaining round bulbous cartilages, or thick layers of scar tissue deep under the skin, or thick oily skin that did not allow effective “shrink wrapping” of your skin after your rhinoplasty or a combination of these three.  The proper correction is a revision rhinoplasty not lasers
How can I define and enhance my jawline and reduce my double chin?
realistic expectations
Nov 26, 2018
