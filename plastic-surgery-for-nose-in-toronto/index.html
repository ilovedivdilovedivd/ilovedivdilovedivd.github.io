Plastic Surgery For Nose In Toronto

Rhinoplasty (nose surgery) in Toronto – one of the most difficult plastic surgeries of aesthetic surgery of the face.

When performing a rhinoplasty surgeon is faced with a variety of anatomy, contours and proportions, requiring a coherent and interconnected actions of the surgeon, taking into account the anatomical and functional needs of each individual patient.

The goal of rhinoplasty is the elimination of aesthetic defects, creating a harmonious appearance, as well as the recovery of the respiratory function of the external nose.

There are two methods of rhinoplasty: closed and open.

Closed rhinoplasty

Closed rhinoplasty (plastic of nose) is carried out through the incisions performed in the natural holes (nostrils).

After cut the tissues are exfoliated, thereby providing surgical access required to perform the main phase of the operation.

Scars do not remain on the visible areas of of nose after this operation

Open rhinoplasty

Open nose job procedure requires a small scar on the skin bridge that separates the nostrils. The rest of the cuts are carried out inside the nose and do not leave a trail.

Open rhinoplasty allows get a good overview and access to all bone and cartilage structures of nose, which makes it indispensable for interventions of large volume, as well as in cases of repeated reconstructive surgeries.

Rhinoplasty On Tip Of Nose Is A Very Challenging And Demanding Surgery In ON, Canada

Indications for Toronto rhinoplasty (plastic of nose) is:

  1. a broad back and (or) the tip of the nose
  2. hump on the bridge of the nose
  3. excessive length of the nose
  4. saddle or beaky nose shape
  5. congenital and post-traumatic deformations of nose
  6. deviated septum
  7. wide nostrils
  8. violation of the respiratory function.

Features of surgery

Rhinoplasty (nose surgery) is indicated in patients after 18 years, and since at this age completely ends the formation of osteochondral nose skeleton.

Plastic Surgery Of Nose In Toronto Is Mainly Carried Out Under General Anaesthesia

Minor corrections of nose can be done under local anesthesia. Operations greater volume are performed under general anesthesia. The duration of nose reshaping, depending on the complexity, can range from 45 minutes to 4 hours.

After plastic surgery for nose in Toronto, Canada in the nasal passages are set the special swabs to 2-3 days, and therefore breathing through the nose will be hampered until their removal. On the back of the nose is applied gypsum or plastic bandage for 7-10 days.

Some patients may experience nasal congestion for several weeks associated with edema of the nasal mucosa. Black circles and bruises disappear in 1-2 weeks. Edema of nose tip may persist for up to one month.

The final result of the operation is evaluated 6-12 months. In the postoperative period it is necessary to refrain from intense exercise, sauna and baths for 2-3 months. The wearing of glasses is prohibited up to 6 weeks.

It is necessary to be careful in the care of the skin of the nose, because in the postoperative period it is very sensitive to external irritants. In addition, should strictly adhere to all the recommendations of the operating surgeon.
