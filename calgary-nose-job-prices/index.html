Calgary Nose Job Prices

The nose is the central body of the face and plays an important role in creating a harmonious appearance.

The aim is to eliminate aesthetic defects of the nose, and, if necessary, restore the nasal respiratory function.

Aesthetic rhinoplasty in Calgary helps eliminates external defects of nose, such as: hump, a disproportionately large nose, saddle nose, deformed nose as a result of injury or previously an unsuccessful rhinoplasty, wide or narrow nose, asymmetry of the nose, wide the tip of a nose, etc.

Functional rhinoplasty (septoplasty) eliminates the defects of the nose, which lead to disruption of the respiratory function, and complications associated with them. Most often, this intervention is performed on the nasal septum.

The best candidates for aesthetic rhinoplasty are those who wish to improve their appearance and are not looking for perfection.

Calgary rhinoplastyis usually carried out after the complete formation of the facial skeleton and the skeleton of the nose.

For women it is after age 16 years, for men – after 18 years. Functional septoplasty is performed in the case of indications for its implementation, and at an earlier age.

Our specialist will hold contact you detailed consultation in order to choose the type of volume and operations that will help to improve the nose aesthetically and harmonized with the individual characteristics of your face.

Skin type, the ethnic background, and age are important factors that will be considered during the consultation with your surgeon prior to rhinoplasty.

A plastic surgeon is board certified in Plastic Aesthetic and Reconstructive Surgery, a specialist in diseases of the ear, nose and throat.

Rhinoplasty is performed under general or local anesthesia through a closed or open access. When closed access all the incisions are made inside the nose.

With open rhinoplasty the external incision is performed at the narrowest point between the nostrils. The duration of nose job in Calgary clinics is on average 1.5-2 hours. With functional surgical stage may be delayed by 1 hour.

After surgery, to maintain the newly formed nasal septum, in order to prevent complications and more comfortable course in the postoperative period, splints are inserted intranasal, that are in the nasal passages to 14 days and nasal tampons for 1-2 days.
