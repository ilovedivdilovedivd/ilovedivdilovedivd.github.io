Position: Nurse Practitioner
Can answer: 1

Biography: Daniella Gery is a board-certified Adult Nurse Practitioner with over 10 years of experience as a Nurse Practitioner and 4 + years as an aesthetic master injector. She helped start Anew Medspa Beachwood in 2014 and decided to give the medspa an evolutionary makeover and name it Trouvé. She earned her Bachelor of Science in Nursing from Case Western Reserve University in 2006 and her Master of Science in Nursing from University of Michigan in 2008. She is a faculty member for the American Academy of Facial Esthetics (AAFE) and has taught hundreds of healthcare professionals on the art of injecting botulinum toxins, dermal fillers, Kybella, Sculptra, PDO threads, and PRP. She is also certified in Coolsculpting, IPL (Intense Pulse Light), Nd: YAG laser, and radiofrequency body contouring devices.
