Does having thick skin mean I can NEVER have a refined nose tip?
Thick Skin and Rhinoplasty
Mar 29, 2017
Naturally thick skin tissue covering the nasal framework can play a frustrating role in limiting the more subtle refinements possible. But some very nice changes can still be achieved, and there are some special techniques (eg. cartilage grafts) that are often used to help with this situation. Unfortunately, the skin cannot be thinned - a layer of scar tissue would result that could be as thick (or even thicker!) than was originally present.
