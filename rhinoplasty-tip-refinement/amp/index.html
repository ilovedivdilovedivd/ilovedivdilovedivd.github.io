Rhinoplasty candidate

Rhinoplasty surgery tip refinement

You are a great rhinoplasty candidate since you are young, have great skin, and can expect a significant improvement from rhinoplasty.

Tip refinement and narrowing can be achieved by an experienced rhinoplasty surgeon. The goal is to create an elegant and defined tip with natural results.

Cartilage conservation techniques (where you shape cartilage without excising it) can be used that will create a narrow tip without weakening your tip cartilages. This will decrease the chance of an unnatural pinchy tip or weak cartilages that may create problems with your breathing or nasal tip support over time. Tip refinement is best achieved in the hands of an experienced rhinoplasty surgeon who will create a pretty and natural looking nose with long lasting results. (Sanaz Harirchian, MD, Houston Facial Plastic Surgeon)

Rhinoplasty can help a bulbous tip

Rhinoplasty tip refinement picture

Corrective nasal surgery can indeed help you achieve your goal. In the photos you provided, it appears your septum deflects off midline, causing a deviation of the nose and “uneven” nostrils.

After realigning the septum, conservative tip cartilage excision and suturing techniques can narrow your tip nicely. It may take over a year to see your final result due to thick skin, so be patient! (Jeffrey Joseph, MD, FACS, Lafayette Facial Plastic Surgeon)
