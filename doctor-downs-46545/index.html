Can I use Latisse after my microbladed eyebrows have healed?
Latisse
Sep 25, 2017
Hi Risa0011,
Latisse® is a prescription treatment for hypotrichosis (inadequate or not enough lashes) to grow eyelashes longer, fuller, darker. If you are looking to use Latisse for your eyelashes, then yes you can use the product after your Microblading is finished. However, Latisse is not made to help grow your eyebrows. It is only FDA-approved treatment to grow eyelashes.
