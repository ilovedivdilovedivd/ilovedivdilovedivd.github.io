When can I ride my motorcycle?
Riding a motorcycle after a tummy tuck
Oct 22, 2014
I generally tell my patients to wait 8 weeks post surgery before performing any strenuous activities. This is primarily due to the fact that any pulling or stretching can increase the size of the scar.
Why do breasts feel like they are getting pulled when doing push ups?
Why do my breasts feel like they are being pulled when doing push ups?
Oct 22, 2014
Breast implants are often placed submuscularly, that is under the pectoralis major muscle. This muscle extends from the medial chest wall to the humerus or upper arm bone. When the muscle is contracted, the implant may move or feel as though it is being pulled.
When can I get back to working out after my Tummy Tuck without muscle repair or lipo?
Activity after tummy tuck

I recommend patients wait 8 weeks following surgery. Any type of stretching or pulling on the scar may widen it permanently.

No results after arm liposuction
