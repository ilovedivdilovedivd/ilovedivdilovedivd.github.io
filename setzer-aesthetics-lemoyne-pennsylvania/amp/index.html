Scott Setzer

Name: Scott Setzer
Position: Business Owner
Years experience: 22
Biography: Dr. W. Scott Setzer, M.D. is a Board Certified Family Physician and graduate of the University of Virginia School of Medicine. Seeking to spend more quality time with his patients, Dr. Setzer realized his dream of opening his own private practice in October, 2011. The practice philosophy allows much more time with each patient, thus Dr. Setzer is able to focus on his patients’ lifestyle choices and provide and integrative, holistic approach to overall wellness. Living healthier means living with more vigor, purpose, and confidence. This has evolved into an interest in skin health and aesthetic procedures. Dr. Setzer has been performing skin procedures since 1997. He has since trained extensively in the art of aesthetics and launched Setzer Aesthetics as a complement of the medical practice in 2012.

When not managing a busy medical practice, Dr. Setzer enjoys traveling with his wife and two sons. He also enjoys personal fitness and is an avid fly fisherman.
