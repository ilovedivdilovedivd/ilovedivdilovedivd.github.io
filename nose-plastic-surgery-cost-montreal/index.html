How Rhinoplasty Surgery is perfomed?

Rhinoplasty is a very painful procedure, and in most cases requires general anesthesia.

The operation on nose can be performed in one of two types:

Plastic Surgery Of Nose In Montreal, Quebec Is A Very Safe Procedure, And Complications Are Uncommon

1. Open rhinoplasty involves the application of incisions in the external folds of the nose and across the columella. Thereafter, the surgeon pulls up the soft tissue of the nose and to gets access to the surgical area.

This type of rhinoplasty is used for the most serious changes in the shape and size of the nose, as well as to correct the majority of birth defects.

2. Closed rhinoplasty Montreal is used for more of delicate goals. In this case, all incisions are made on the inside of the nose.

Then, the outer skin layer is separated from the bone and cartilages, which allows for any necessary action.

In general, closed method is much more popular among patients of Montreal clinics, than the open method, and its capacity is more than enough to eliminate the typical drawbacks.
