When to Stop Drinking Alcohol Prior to Surgery?
When to Stop Drinking Alcohol Prior to Surgery?
Dec 21, 2022
I recommend that you stop drinking for 24-48 hours prior to your surgery. This is particularly important with red wine as it may have some anticoagulant properties. It is also important to drink plenty of fluids prior to surgery to hydrate yourself as you will not be able to eat or drink for 12 or more hours. You may resume consumption several days after your procedure, but should avoid mixing any alcohol with medications that will be prescribed for you such as pain medication and antibiotics.
