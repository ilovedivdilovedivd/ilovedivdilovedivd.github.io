boney nose
Oct 23, 2019
Your nose is nothing unusual. the “bone poking” are the edges of your nasal bones that can be easily corrected with limited rhinoplasty. Usually by just rasping the area under general or sedation anesthesia the problem can be corrected with a short recovery period.

closed rhinopasty

Open or closed rhinoplasty depends on the surgeons experience and comfort level. If you do not want open rhinoplasty you should be able to find a plastic surgeon who is comfortable with closed procedure and one that can obtain the result you are looking for.

By Persian rhinoplasty I believe you mean a refined narrow nose with slight dorsal curvature. All these are possible. Discuss your thoughts in detail with your surgeon. A 3D imaging will also help you and your surgeon to plan the procedure according to your wishes.
What should i do with my nose? More and more I am noticing that it actually looks very mas

With the limited photos it is hard to make a recommendation, but overall your nose appears to be too large for your face. Reducing the tip projection and length of the nose would help. This will require a full rhinoplasty which can be done open or closed. 3D imaging can help identify the issue and show possible result of surgery.
What approach is best for a deviated septum?
Deviated septum
Oct 16, 2019
Deviated septum can be corrected by itself or in combination with Rhinoplasty. Septoplasty is usually performed as a closed procedure if it is done by itself. Septoplasty can be performed in conjunction with rhinoplasty, Open or closed rhinoplasty is based on the surgeon’s experience and comfort with the procedure and severity of the correction needed.

tip rhinoplasty
Oct 16, 2019
Limited tip rhinoplasty can be done without touching the rest of the nose. However the result will be limited. Based on the photos you provided you may require more than just a tip revision due to the amount of projection of the tip. 3D imaging can help you see possible outcome of the procedure.

rhinoplasty technique

Your nose is not straight and there are tip asymmetries. All these can be corrected with closed or open rhinoplasty. The technique will depend on the surgeon and your preference. Could I have a closed rhino or will a nose filler look good with my nose?
Surgery vs. liquid rhinoplasty
Sep 4, 2019
Liquid rhinoplasty is a good alternative when surgery is not an option or when a limited change is needed. The photos you provided would benefit more from reduction rhinoplasty to improve the dorsal hump and tip projection. 3D imaging can help decide what procedure is best for you.
Are these results achievable through alarplasty?
Alar reduction
Sep 4, 2019
Alar reduction may be performed alone or at same time as rhinoplasty. Using 3D imaging you can get a good idea of possible result from Alar reduction. However there may be visible scar on the groove depending on the procedure and amount of reduction required.
