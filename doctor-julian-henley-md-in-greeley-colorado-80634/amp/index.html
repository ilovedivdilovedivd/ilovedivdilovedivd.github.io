Is there any safe alternatives to general anesthesia for a Rhinoplasty?
Rhinoplasty is an operation of refinement
Aug 22, 2014
A good rhinoplasty is an operation of subtle refinement that should never look like a nose that was operated upon. Most of such operations can be performed under local awake anesthesia for those patients that desire such option. Modern general anesthesia is relatively safe and should not be feared. Either option is available to most patients. Local anesthesia with sedation often has less postoperative bruising and less postoperative nausea.
