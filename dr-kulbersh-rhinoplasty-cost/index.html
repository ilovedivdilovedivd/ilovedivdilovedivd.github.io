Open vs. closed rhinoplasty
Dec 18, 2020
Thank you for your question. The difference between open and closed rhinoplasty is a single small incision under the columella. This incision typically heals very well, making the scar virtually undetectable. Open rhinoplasty allows for more access to the internal structure, which is sometimes preferred depending on the amount of correction needed.
