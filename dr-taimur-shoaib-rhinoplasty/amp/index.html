Am I a candidate for a rhino? I have thick skin.
thick skin is not a contraindication to rhinoplasty
Nov 18, 2018
If you have thick skin and are considering a rhinoplasty, then you have to set your expectations at a level where you accept less of a result than you might want. There are two things to consider –  what you want, and you can have. “what you can have” is determined by your body, often. If the two align up, then you should consider surgery more seriously.
