Is it a normal to see my abdomen getting a little big compared to recent weeks?
Abdomen enlarging after tummy tuck
Sep 25, 2018
There are a variety of causes for a patient’s lower abdomen to enlarge following a tummy tuck. These include swelling of the soft tissues from fluid retention, a fluid collection or “seroma” of the abdominal wall, pregnancy changes, and others. Seromas are very common following tummy tucks and can be drained in the office to remove any extra fluid. It is best for a patient to return to her surgeon for another post-op visit to evaluate the swelling.

Recovery from rhinoplasty

After rhinoplasty, the post-operative pain experienced by patients is typically managed with mild pain medication.
