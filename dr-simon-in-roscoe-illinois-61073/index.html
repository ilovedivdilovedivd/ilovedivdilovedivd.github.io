Why does my nose keep getting bigger instead of shrinking after rhinoplasty?
Pollybeak deformity
Feb 2, 2017
It is difficult diagnosis with certainty without examining the nose, but you appear to be developing supratip fullness. This has been callously named “pollybeak deformity,” and is well described postsurgical phenomenon usually secondary to excessive scar formation in the supratip. I would suggest you seek evaluation by your surgeon. At 2 months a simple treatment with steroid injection can stop and reverse the process.

Thanks for your question. The slow revelation of your result is due to multiple revisions and not the type of grafting material. Patience will be required and hopefully your surgeon did your last rhinoplasty.
