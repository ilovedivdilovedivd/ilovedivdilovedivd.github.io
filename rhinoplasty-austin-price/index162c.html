Rhinoplasty Austin Price

Rhinoplasty in Austin is plastic surgery, which allows change the shape of the nose. This operation helps a person to get rid of the inferiority complex, which provoked problems with appearance.

It also helps to improve nasal breathing, and thus significantly improves the quality of life.

The main indication for surgery is the fact that a person does not satisfied with the shape of the nose, which may be congenital or acquired due to any illness or injury.

A plastic surgery allows to give the nose another shape, create or remove a small hump, change the tip of the nose, increase or decrease the nose (as well as its separate parts), to eliminate the asymmetry, and correct the whether national or racial characteristics.

Besides using this operation can be leveled the nasal septum, thus significantly improving respiration quality.

Approximately 14 days prior to surgery should stop taking drugs that affect blood clotting (cough medicines, aspirin and others). You need to eat only a light foods in small amounts 24 hours prior nose reshaping.

On the day of surgery is better not to drink and eat. Sometimes the surgeon recommends to take medication for several days before the nasal surgery, which strengthens blood vessels.

The menstrual period or the common cold is unfavorable for the surgery. The procedure need set aside for some time.

Rhinoplasty is divided into open and closed, depending on the method of operating. With open access the doctor makes a small incision over the nose, slightly occasionally lifting flap of skin and performs the desired correction. The main advantage of this method is the fact that the specialist sees well all that makes.

With closed access the correction is performed through incisions inside the nostrils. This method allows the patient to quickly return to normal life and to restore the appearance.

But at the same time it increases the risk during the nose operation. The method of surgical approach the surgeon chooses after a full examination of the patient.

Possible complications of nose job

The first thing that may disappoint the patient is discrepancy of the result to the patient’s expectations. Keep in mind that 100% predict the outcome of the rhinoplasty in Austin is impossible. Do not forget that even if you are absolutely not satisfied with the result, return to former shape of the nose is impossible except that the to do another operation.

But, again, to predict its result no one can. That is why you need to weigh the pros and cons before to take a decisive step. And, of course, trust your face you can only to experienced specialists and professionals.

Other complications which are possible after the operation can be called less severe. Sometimes internal rough scars remain after the correction of the nose, which is removed with the help of a special operation, thereby completely restoring breathing.

Contraindications, precluding the possibility of carrying out the procedure

All contraindications for this operation can be divided into the following groups – when there is no reason to change anything, and when really you can not do the nose beauty surgery.

Diseases for which rhinoplasty is absolutely contraindicated:

  2. Diseases of any internal organs.
  3. Diseases of the cardiovascular system.
  4. Oncological diseases.
  5. Infectious diseases.
  6. Coagulation disorders.

People who are under 18 years old should not resort to nose job in Austin, because the cartilages and bones of the nose are in the growth stage, and it is likely that the shape of the nose is still to change.
