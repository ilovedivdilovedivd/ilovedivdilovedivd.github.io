Why hasn't the skin around my eyes peeled after a chemical peel yet?
Lower eyelid skin after a chemical peel
Apr 7, 2014
After a full face chemical peel, it is not unusual for the skin in different areas of the face to peel at different times. When the skin begins to slough off, can also vary with the type and depth of the peel. I typically inform patients that most areas should begin peeling by day 4 after the procedure. The most imprortant thing to do is to keep the treated skin moist and covered with ointment thus providing the optimal healing environment.
How long does it usually take for bruising under eyes after rhinoplasty to go away?
Bruising after rhinoplasty
Apr 7, 2014
I typically cousel rhinoplasty patients that the bruising can take up to 2 weeks to resolve. Cool compresses that were used for the first 3 days after surgery can be switched to warm compresses after postop day 7. This increases blood flow to the area and can "wash" away the bruisng a day or so more quickly. With that said, I would recommend being evaulated by your surgeon and asking him/her is this practice would be appropriate for you.
Hypertrophic Scar Still Red 5 Weeks After CO2 Laser Resurfacing. Will the Redness Go Away?
Skin redness after laser resurfacing to slowly resolve
May 3, 2011
I routinely tell my patients to expect skin redness for up to 3 months after laser or chemical peel resurfacing. 

The are steroid creams and other topical medications that your doctor can prescribe to help speed up the resolution of the redness.

True inflammation and raising of the scar is not always expected at 5 weeks out and should be brought to the attention of your doctor.

Is the Position of Earlobes Changed After Face and Neck Lift?
Ear lobe changes after facelift
Apr 25, 2011
Ear lobe position can change after a facelift.  If re-piercing the lobe does not help with earring symmetry then a small in-office procedure can help reposition them.
