Can I sleep on my side after rhinoplasty? 3 weeks post op.
Side sleeper
Apr 7, 2017
Usually by three weeks, it is OK to resume your normal sleeping condition. However each rhinoplasty is unique. There may be certain maneuvers your surgeon did for which he or she wishes you to be extremely careful of any potential trauma. Sleeping on your side, you may be at a little more risk of burying your nose in your pillow which could alter the fragile postoperative nose. this is likely a question best answered by your surgeon.
