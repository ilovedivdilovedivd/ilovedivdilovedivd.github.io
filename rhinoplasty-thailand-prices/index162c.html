Rhinoplasty In Thailand

Rhinoplasty in Thailand is a plastic surgery to change the shape and size of the nose, improve nasal breathing after injury or congenital disease.

Rhinoplasty is performed for medicinal and cosmetic purposes. Rhinoplasty can be performed after 18 years, when the face shape and bone structure of the nose is already been formed.

The surgery is performed under general anesthesia. The time spent in the hospital is 1-3 days.

The techniques of Siam rhinoplasty

There are closed (the incisions are made inside) and in more severe cases, open methods of rhinoplasty (a small incision across the nose). After surgery in the nasal passages are put tampons for 3-5 days.

During this period, you must breathe through the mouth. Nose reshape surgery ends with the imposition of a plaster bandage, which is removed at 8-10 days.

Postoperative swelling persists for 2-3 weeks. Preliminary results of the nose beauty surgery can be estimated after 1-2 months. The final result of carried out operations can be seen in 9-12 months.

Rhinoplasty is able to fulfill the dream many people – to give a perfect nose.

Indications for rhinoplasty:

The operation to change the shape of the nose may be performed for aesthetic purposes, the indication in this case is the patient’s desire to correct an undesirable shape of the nose.

Before And After Korean Nose Surgery In Bangkok

Most often, patients want correct the following shortcomings:
  1. a pronounced hump on the nose;
  2. too thin or thick, beaked tip of the nose;
  3. saddle nose;
  4. excessively long nose;
  5. expanded nostrils.

Also, in certain cases, surgery is performed, and for medical reasons:

  1. complete or partial absence of the nose (congenital or due to trauma);
  2. nasal septum deviation;
  3. difficulty breathing because of violations of the nose structure.

Duration of nose job: 1-2 hours.

With Rhinoplasty For Man In Thailand You Can Reshape The Bridge And Nasal Tip

Recovery period: 1 month.

There are several types of Thai rhinoplasty. Most of the operations carried out by the closed method, with no visible incisions on the outside.

If necessary, used an open rhinoplasty, small incisions are made in the most inconspicuous places.

There is also method of non-surgical correction of nasal defects, but they are not widely used, because of the fragility and lack of reliability of the results. Depending on the complexity of plastic surgery of nose (what is the volume of the work of a plastic surgeon) is used local or general anesthesia.

The patient can go home immediately or the next day after nasal surgery. About 10 days need to wear special bandage that promote tissue healing and prevents accidental damage.

Male Nose Surgery Is Consistently Among The Most Frequent Plastic Surgeries In Bangkok Thai

Since rhinoplasty is a surgical operation, the complications may occur: swelling, bruising, pain, bleeding. You may need to breathe through the mouth for some time.

The first few weeks should be avoided strenuous exercise, hot baths, active facial expressions, try not to fly on airplanes, do not wear glasses in a heavy frame and do not abuse salty, hot and cold food.

But all these inconveniences will soon pass away, but after a few months it will be possible to evaluate the preliminary result of the nose job in Thailand (a final – only a year later).
