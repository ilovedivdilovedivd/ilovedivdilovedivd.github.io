CO2 LASER– makeup remover when healing
Jul 11, 2014
The CO2 laser can require many days of recovery. One trick that I like for make-up remover is coconut oil or argan oil. The oils will usually take the eye-makeup off with ease and will hydrate the skin without clogging. I would not recommend using makeup on the fresh, healing skin. You may consider a tinted, physical-blocking sunscreen over your moisturizer, and you may remove it by washing with a gentle cleanser and warm water.

Pixel laser and what to expect

Pulse Dye Laser for Scars?
PDL treatment for scars
Nov 29, 2013
The PDL laser is effective in reducing erythema (redness) of scars. It may take a few treatments before the desired outcome is achieved, sometimes more than three. Increasing the energy levels with each treatment, as tolerated, may help to achieve maximum benefit.
