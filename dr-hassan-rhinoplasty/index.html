Rhinoplasty Recovery Time and Downtime?
Post rhinoplasty recovery process
Feb 25, 2020
Following a rhinoplasty I normally advise my patients that packs will be removed the morning after surgery. Sutures will be due out by day 7. The dorsal rhinoplasty splint can be removed after 2 weeks. No heavy swimming/exercise for about 6 weeks and no blowing the nose for around 6 weeks. return to work at 2 weeks.
When are all visible signs of a healing rhinoplasty gone from the public eye?
When will I start looking normal and be able to return to work after a rhinoplasty
Feb 25, 2020
This is another important consideration for any patient considering surgery. Following a rhinoplasty I generally say most bruising will have settled by 2-3 weeks. Swelling can take many months to resolve but around 80% should have subsided by 6weeks post although this is time can be doubled in patients with this skin.
