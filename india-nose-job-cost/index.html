1. The hump on the bridge of the nose; many people are embarrassed, besides sometimes it indicates the presence of Caucasian nationality.

Types of rhinoplasty

Types of rhinoplasty are selected depending on the purpose of the operation. The first type of rhinoplasty is Septoplasty. It is carried out, in case of damage to the nasal septum and difficulty breathing.

Doctor surgically removes jagged cartilage, at the expense of the nose aligned, and breathing becomes easier. This means that the surgeon does not make external incisions and does not violate the nose support and its blood supply.
