Now plastic surgery is very popular, more and more people are resorting to it, who want to improve their appearance. There are operations that are most popular, among them is rhinoplasty.

Earlier rhinoplasty did only stars, but now it has become quite affordable and, therefore, even ordinary people can resort to it.

Rhinoplasty in Canada is a surgical procedure aimed at reshaping the nose. The operation is carried out not only for aesthetic purposes but also for medical reasons, for example, if a deviated septum, prevents a person to breathe normally.

But most people decide make a rhinoplasty is to simply improve their appearance and make the nose more perfect. Previously, there was a high probability that the result of the nose reshaping can not conform to desire the patient, now the probability is reduced to almost zero thanks to advanced technology.

You can be sure that the nose as a result will be exactly the way you want. And the newest methods of computer simulation allows even, so to speak, try on the a new nose on yourself and understand how it is suitable for a particular person. It may seem that this operation is very easy, but it is not ,so important to choose a really good surgeon for this procedure.

Since this is operation, it has certain contraindications. For example, rhinoplasty can not be done to people who have not reached the age of 17 years, it is not just an age limitation.

This contraindication related to the fact that at this age the nasal cartilages are finally formed. It is also forbidden to do this operation for those who suffer from diabetes and people who have problems with blood clotting.

The advantages include the fact that the person gets the desired shape of the nose and, accordingly, it becomes aesthetically satisfied with their appearance.

Cons can occur when a specialist was chosen incorrectly. The illiterate surgeon who has a lack of experience carrying out of nose job, can actually cause harm not only to the patient’s physical appearance, but also his health.

Complications include the reduction of sensitivity of the nose, increases of bleeding, infectious diseases etc.
