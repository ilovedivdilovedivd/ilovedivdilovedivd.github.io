Could consuming alcohol within 24 hours of lip injections dissolve filler & not achieve full results?
alcohol won’t dissolve your filler
Jul 26, 2023
The good news is drinking alcohol will not dissolve lip filler so you can enjoy your event, however I usually don’t recommend attending an important event right after lip filler in case there is bruising or excessive swelling. Lips typically look more filled/swollen right after they are injected. Avoid kissing on the lips for 48-72 hrs after filler also. Icing the lips gently 10-15 minutes at a time can help reduce swelling as can arnica gel (available without a prescription).
Can I do the SculpSure treatment for my belly when I have my period?
Yes, but I recommend waiting.
Jan 17, 2021
You “can” do Sculpsure on your belly when you are on your period, however it may be more uncomfortable during your period, so I would recommend waiting till after. Many women compare the feeling of Sculpsure to menstrual cramps, so having menstrual cramps on top of menstrual cramps is not ideal.
Can SculpSure cause Paradoxical Adipose Hyperplasia?
Sculpsure/Warmsculpting does not cause PAH
Jan 17, 2021
Paradoxical Adipose Hyperplasia is a rare phenomenon that can occur with Coolsculpting/Cryolipolysis. One of the benefits of Sculpsure is that there is no such risk for this.
