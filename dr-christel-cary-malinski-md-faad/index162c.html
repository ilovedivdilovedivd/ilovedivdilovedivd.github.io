Can I still move my forehead after Botox?
Natural result
Jul 27, 2017
Many of my patients are opting for the natural result that you are speaking of. My patients want to move their fireheads while still softening their wrinkles. This is very possible with Botox. Just make sure your injector is board certified and understands the result you are looking for.
6 days after Botox – didn’t stop facial sweating
Hyperhidrosis
Jul 26, 2017
Botox is a good treatment for hyperhidrosis but it doesn’t completely stop excessive sweating(hyperhidrosis). I think you should give it at least another week for results. You may want to visit your Dermatologist to discuss other medical treatments for hyperhidrosis. In my practice, I seldom use Botox alone to treat this condition.
Could jaw Botox help me?
Botox injections to masseter muscle
Jul 20, 2017
It is difficult to evaluate your masseter from the picture. Masseter muscle is over the angle of the jaw and can make a face seem wider. Botox can make the muscle less noticeable and thus slim the jaw. Botox and filler can also be injected to a chin to improve symmetry.
Follow-up Question: Blue bruise and lip hanging 10 months after Juvederm and Botox? Is
Botox and filler blues
Jul 20, 2017
A bruise does not last 10 months. You are likely noticing the Tyndall effect from too much filler placed to superficially in the skin. That may also be what is distorting your smile. You should ask to have the filler dissolved or have it dissolved by a board certified dermatologist.
Will botox around the mouth interfere with eating? Or kissing?
Gummy smile
Jul 7, 2017
The appropriate amount and placement of Botox will temporarily correct a gummy smile. Properly placed Botox will not interfere with eating or kissing. I often use Botox to correct a gummy smile with great results.
