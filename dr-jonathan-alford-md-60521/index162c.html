Dr. Alford has exceptional educational experience and unique surgical training. Dr. Alford attended Louisiana State University in his hometown of Baton Rouge, Louisiana. He then completed a dual degree program at St. George’s University earning both a Master’s Degree in Public Health and his Doctorate of Medicine. Throughout his residency Dr. Alford served on numerous departmental and hospital administrative committees as a resident representative. In his final year of residency, he served as Chief Resident.

During this fellowship, Dr. Alford completed over 1,000 cosmetic treatments.

Dr. Alford specializes in cosmetic procedures. He’s had extensive training with VASER hi-definition liposuction, as well as Renuvion non-invasive skin tightening. Dr. Alford enjoys working with moms to help them look and feel as confident and comfortable with their bodies as they did before having children.

Dr. Alford appreciates the importance of being a life-long learner and takes every opportunity available to both teach and learn. He is an active member of the American Academy of Cosmetic Surgery, the American College of Surgeons, and the American College of Obstetrics and Gynecology.

Dr. Alford lives in Royal Oak with his wife, Catherine. They have two children, boy-girl twins, and a rescue dog named Julep. In his free time, Dr. Alford enjoys reading, discovering new restaurants with his wife, traveling with his family, spending time outdoors, and cooking at home with friends and family.
