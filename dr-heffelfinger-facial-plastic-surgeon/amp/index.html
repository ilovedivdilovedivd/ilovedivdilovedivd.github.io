So I'm 4 Weeks Post-op. Is my Nose Strong Enough to Return to Normalcy?
How long to "baby" your nose after rhinoplasty...
Feb 12, 2011
At 4 weeks, things are pretty stable.  Popping zits, exfoliating, and washing your nose are fine to do.  If it itches on the inside, take some Vaseline, put it on a Q-tip, and "scratch".  The itching is probably due to dryness/crusting and will improve with this treatment.  I would be careful with contact sports for another month.  Be careful with sun exposure to the columellar incision for the next year.
