Rhinoplasty Turkey Prices

Rhinoplasty in Turkey is one of the difficult sections in the plastic surgery, because the physician has many tasks, such as restoring forms, maintaining of symmetry as well as he need to provide the proper functioning of the nose.

This operation became completely safe and predictable with the help of modern equipment and experienced doctors.

A lot of people are dissatisfied with the shape of their nose and there are often congenital and acquired deformations of the nose, and they must be corrected.

Indications for rhinoplasty:

1) Congenital deformations of the nose or resulting from injury.
2) The problem with nasal septum, curvatures, which violate breathing and cause snoring.
3) Various visible defects of the nose, such as large nostrils, hump, length, a very sharp or thickened the tip.
4) Unsuccessful previous nose surgery.

Peculiarities and types of rhinoplasty.

The first is to consult a doctor and a full medical examination. The surgeon determines the type and method of rhinoplasty if there are no contraindications.

You can not do the operation if: the skin around the nose is inflamed, a disease of the internal organs, blood, cancer, diabetes, or a virus infection in the body.

People with chronic diseases of the heart or liver, as well as those who have mental health problems will be identified.

The recovery period after reconstructive rhinoplasty is between 6 and 12 months. A good and experienced doctor will always choose the type of rhinoplasty for his patient, which suits him.

Methods for rhinoplasty

There are two types of rhinoplasty closed and open. In closed rhinoplasty all incisions are made inside the nose only, while the surgeon performs his work almost to the touch.

The advantages of a closed rhinoplasty including rapid healing, small edema and short period of recovery, but also in such cases there may be complications in the form of bleeding and infection.

Open method is needed when the surgeon has to work with the cartilage and bone of the nose, it helps increase access to see and evaluate the condition of the treated area.

Open method is used during the reoperation, as well as when is required high accuracy and symmetry. The surgeon selects the method according to the purpose. You need to trust the specialist, then the best results will be achieved.
