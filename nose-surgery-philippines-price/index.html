Nose Surgery In Philippines Price

Rhinoplasty in Philippines – is a separate area of plastic surgery to correct congenital or acquired defects of the nose.

With rhinoplasty you can solve the problem of nasal breathing, correct the external effects of trauma, as well as carry out the correction of appearance for aesthetic reasons.

For many people who experience dissatisfaction with their own form of the nose, rhinoplasty can be a salvation.

However, it is quite expensive and complicated plastic surgery, which requires an enormous professionalism of the surgeon.

Let’s try to figure out what is the specificity of these surgeries?

Whether to rhinoplasty or not?

Rhinoplasty can be recommended by a doctor when detected a deviated septum, there are congenital or post-traumatic deformation of the nose, there is a violation of nasal breathing.

Aesthetic indications for rhinoplasty are:

– saddle-shaped nose;
– hump on the bridge of the nose;
– the hooked tip of the nose;
– too pointed or thickened tip of the nose;
– excessive length of the nose;
– the asymmetry of the nose;
– dilated nostrils.

The presence of nasal defects is determined by the surgeon with special measurements, the results of which are compared with the average parameters.

Asian Rhinoplasty In Phillipines Before And After

Rhinoplasty is carried out only after psychological counseling, often because the patient’s problems are not associated with any real flaws of the nose, and on the self-perception disorders.

Rhinoplasty for aesthetic indications is done an adult only, because the body must be fully formed at the time of surgery.

Contraindications for rhinoplasty are: diseases of the cardiovascular system, liver and kidneys; diabetes; mental diseases and cancer; a bleeding disorder.

Also not recommended to do the nose reshaping for people older than 40 years, for which the ability to regenerate tissue is significantly reduced.

Types of rhinoplasty

There are many types of Philippines rhinoplasty, each of which is designed to solve a particular problem and can affect different types of tissue.

Rhinoplasty nostrils. Designed to solve the problem of large nostrils. After the nose reshape surgery may remain inconspicuous scars at the base of the nose. The operation is carried out both in aesthetic indications and in order to reduce potential swelling of the nose after another surgery in this area.

Rhinoplasty nose tip. The most difficult kind of rhinoplasty that can dramatically change the appearance of the patient, but requiring from the surgeon great experience. Fixes hooked, upturned, shifting the axis and other external nasal defects.

Columella correction. Columella is the lower part of the bridge between the nostrils.

It may be too wide or too narrow. You can fix this by its excision or partial healing of cartilage.

Correction of the nose form. Giving the correct form the bridge of nose – smoothing of a small hump, the axis alignment. Affects the bone and cartilage.

Contour nose surgery. Non-surgical method for the correction of small external nasal defects due to the injection of hyaluronic acid.

Septorhinoplasty. The alignment of the nasal septum to relieve nasal respiration, solving the problem of snoring.

Turbinectomy (including laser). Partial or complete removal of hypertrophied nasal mucosa when nasal breathing problems.

Methods of rhinoplasty

Nose Job Procedure Prices In Cebu City Philippines Can Improve The Appearance And Proportion Of Your Nose

Currently, there are two methods of surgical intervention to correct the form of nose: open and closed.

Open nose job involves incisions under the skin of the nasal septum with a depth of 5 mm for the purpose of further manipulation. It is used in complex operations that require a complete visual control over all stages of rhinoplasty. It allows you to easily peeled off soft tissue, delete and move cartilage.

Closed technique uses the possibility of surgical procedures through incisions in the nostrils, which are subsequently not visible.

Recovery features

Rhinoplasty with a laser, injections of hyaluronic acid and fillers reduces recovery period to a minimum . Surgical methods leave quite a severe swelling of the nose and around the eyes, which subside in 10-20 days.

It may require the dressing of a plaster cast for up to ten days. Tampons in the nose is left up to three days, so that the patient has to breathe through his mouth.

The progress of recovery depends significantly on the patient’s age and individual characteristics of the organism. After the swabs are removed and removed the plaster, it will be necessary to periodically visit your doctor for the control of healing.

Not recommended: exercise, swimming pool and wearing glasses for the first few weeks after nasal surgery. You should avoid too hot or cold food, so as not to provoke nosebleeds. When planning nose rhinoplasty, it is best to take leave from work for at least for half a month.

Possible complications

In cosmetic surgery, rhinoplasty is considered one of the most difficult areas. In order to surgery was performed successfully, previously is carried out a thorough examination and computer modeling of the future shape of the nose.
