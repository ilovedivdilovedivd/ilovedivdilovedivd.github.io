Cost Of Rhinoplasty In Orange County CA

Rhinoplasty (nose surgery, nose shape correction) in Orange County is the most ancient of all aesthetic surgeries. The history of rhinoplasty has more than 3000 years, and the correction of nose shape and restoring its function is one of the most difficult sections of plastic surgery.

In addition to its aesthetic features, the nose is the start of the respiratory system. The air is heated, humidified, cleaned out and disinfected when passes through the nasal passages.

In addition, the nose contributes not only to air filtration, but also participates in the process of smelling, ventilation and purification of accessory sinuses, and plays a significant role in the functioning of the lacrimal system.

It becomes clear that rhinoplasty is aimed at changing or correction of the external nose shape, as well as its internal elements, which is of great importance not only in the process of respiration.

Possible complications

Any surgery carries some risk. Rhinoplasty is no exception in this respect. Complications are rare when the nose operation is performed by a qualified surgeon.

However, due to specific features of the anatomical structure, healing of wounds, general reaction of the organism to the surgery there is a possibility of complications, including infection, nosebleed, or a reaction to medications that injected during anesthesia.

The patient can significantly reduce the risk of surgical intervention, if carefully follow the recommendations of the surgeon, both before and after nose job.

Before And After Nose Surgery In Orange County

On average, 3 cases out of 10 may required additional surgical intervention to achieve the desired result and correcting minor residual deformations.

Planning for the plastic surgery on nose

The basis of success of the rhinoplasty procedure is a mutual understanding between the patient and the surgeon. At the first consultation the surgeon should listen to the expectations of the patient and discuss with them all the possible options for changing the shape of the nose.

The surgeon selects a suitable technique for nose job, as well as evaluates the factors that may affect the result of the nose reshape surgery. This method of selection will be determined by the following parameters:

Orange County Rhinoplasty Creates A More Elegant, Refined Shape To The Nose And Face

  1. The structure of the nasal bones and the nasal cartilages;
  2. Thickness and condition of the skin;
  3. Type of person;
  4. Age;
  5. The patient’s wishes.

Computer simulations is conducted by the surgeon before the nose reshape surgery in Orange County. The purpose of this research is to simulate the shape of the nose with special computer programs.

The surgeon selects the type of nose plastic surgery and methods, taking into account the expected result. Also, the doctor must carefully collect anamnesis.

Preparation for nose beauty surgery

Orange County Nose Surgery To Correct Bulbous Or Boxy Nose Tip

The surgeon should explain to the patient how to prepare for surgery, including the principles of rational food intake and liquid, smoking, and intake of various drugs.

The operation is better to postpone for some time if the patient has recently suffered a disease of the upper respiratory tract or any infection skin lesion. You must stop taking all drugs containing salicylates preferably 10 days prior to the planned plastic nose surgery. The most commonly used drug is a aspirin.

Information for women

Women taking contraceptives or other hormonal preparations (e.g. estrogen replacement therapy) are more prone to bleeding. It is advisable to stop taking hormonal drugs for 10-14 days before surgery, as well as the rhinoplasty is not recommended during menstrual periods.

Anesthesia

Nasal Plastic Surgery Orange County For A Thin, Proportional Nose

When performing a rhinoplasty in Orange County can be used different types of anesthesia, which are determined by the volume of operations, the wishes of the patient and the surgeon’s preference.

It is preferred to perform the operation under general anesthesia, especially for intervention on the bone structure of the nose. In this case, the patient will be spared from unnecessary emotional problems during the surgery.
