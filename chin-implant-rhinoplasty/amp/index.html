Chin implant will help balance your profile

You are correct, a chin implant will help balance your profile. I often explain to patients that making their chin larger automatically makes the nose look smaller. Your chin should meet a line drawn straight down from your lower lip. Your photo shows that you have an “underprojected” chin. Surgery could be done at the same time – a rhinoplasty and chin implant, which would make your profile more “harmonious” and give you the result you want.

Most cosmetic surgeons have “morphing” software and can show you your potential postoperative results. Otoplasty surgery brings the ears closer to the head. You did not provide a photograph that shows if you need this done. However, if you are looking to decrease the vertical length of your ears, I would advise against it. (Theda C. Kontis, MD, Baltimore Facial Plastic Surgeon)
