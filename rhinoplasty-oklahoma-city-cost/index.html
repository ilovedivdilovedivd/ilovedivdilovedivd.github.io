How is rhinoplasty performed?

Method of plastic surgery for nose depends on the problems faced by surgeon and the conditions. There are two methods of carrying out this operation: with an external incision in the skin of the nasal septum ( “open” rhinoplasty) and using only intranasal incisions (“closed” method). Both methods allow you to achieve good results. The nose job in OKC is performed under local anesthesia or general anesthesia.
