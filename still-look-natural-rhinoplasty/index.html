Natural Look after Rhinoplasty (The Cosmetic Consultation.)

You can achieve many things with a Rhinoplasty (or any other cosmetic procedure, for that matter). Therefore, the most important thing you can do before a cosmetic consultation is to decide what you like and what you do not like and changes you would like to make. At the consultation, you can discuss this with your Plastic Surgeon and decide what YOU will have done. If the person you see does not discuss your desires and how to meet them, see someone else. (Robert T. Buchanan, MD, Highlands Plastic Surgeon)

That is why you need at least 3 in person evaluations with boarded surgeons in your area. Discuss your concerns in detail to your satisfaction. Called informed consent. Understand the answers so there are no post operative surprises. (Darryl J. Blinski, MD, Miami Plastic Surgeon)

Natural results are the goal of a great rhinoplasty

Your consultation will be as individual as you and your surgeon each are. Every doctor approaches the consultation in a customized manner and thus, you must look for the surgeon you believe will fit who you are and what you want. Natural results can be achieved. The best way to tell if you will be happy with your surgeon is to look at their before and after gallery. If you like what you see there, then you will most likely be satisfied with the aesthetic results that come with the surgical process.

There are no guarantees, but experience, reputation, and previous results go a long way toward stacking the cards in your favor. (Jay Calvert, MD, Beverly Hills Plastic Surgeon)
