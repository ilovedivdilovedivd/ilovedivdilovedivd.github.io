San Diego Nose Job Prices

Rhinoplasty in San Diego is a popular nose reshaping surgery often referred to as a “nose job” and it is one of the most popular cosmetic procedures performed worldwide and US.

Genetics, national features of appearance and acquired deformities affect on the shape of the nose. Age also affects the appearance of the nose form.

With years the thickening in the the nose bridge area becomes more prominent, and the nose tip slightly sags (this is a consequence of tissue ptosis). As a result, the nose as though becomes larger. Often, for a full face rejuvenation is needed rhinoplasty.

Furthermore, correction is performed for reconstruction after injury, and to eliminate the asymmetry and deformations, congenital defects, and also to fix broken nasal septum and restoration of the nasal respiration.

Rhinoplasty can be successfully applied at:
– disproportionately – excessive length or width of the nose;
– presence of hump, which spoils the appearance of the nose;
– wide tip of the nose or nostrils;
– any deformations – congenital or acquired as a result of injuries;
– fractures of the nasal septum, violation of nasal breathing;
– age-related changes – sagging of the tip of the nose, the expansion nasal bridge.

Preparing for rhinoplasty

Preparing for rhinoplasty begins with an initial consultation of the surgeon, on which the patient receives all the information about the surgery, possibilities of correction of the nose, the risks and results of the procedure.

San Diego Rhinoplasty is a surgical procedure, so you can not do without a medical examination. The surgeon will appoint examination by an otolaryngologist. It will need to hand over tests, to make sure, that patient has not contraindications to rhinoplasty – infectious processes, systemic disorders, pregnancy, exacerbation of chronic diseases and other risky conditions.

The patient has the ability to immediately see a projected results that gives rhinoplasty. Computer simulation is carried out by photo using a special software. Efficiency of the procedure depends on the structure of the nose, and condition skin and the cartilage, type face and age of the patient’s.

To find the most harmonious shape of the nose always need taken into account the so-called profile angle: the angle between the line forehead-chin and nasal bridge.

Nose job

Rhinoplasty in San Diego CA involves the use of surgical approach in two ways. The first method (closed rhinoplasty) does not envisage incisions on the surface. All manipulations are performed through the nostrils. Most often, this method is used for correcting the width of the nose and its tip.

Nose Job In San Diego, California Before And After

The second method (open rhinoplasty) – an incision made to the columella. It facilitates the surgeon’s work and helps to achieve better results. With the help of a rhinoplasty can be carried out all kinds of nose correction – narrowing of the nasal bridge, shortening the nose, a decrease in width nose, the elimination of a small hump, asymmetry and consequences of fractures, the creation of an entirely new harmonious shape.

Rhinoplasty is performed mainly under general anesthesia. The operation lasts about three hours. Rhinoplasty is not only a change in shape of the nose, but also the treatment of nasal breathing.

If the inspection be found out that the patient has a broken nasal septum, the doctor is easy to eliminate this defect during the nose reshaping.

Recovery after rhinoplasty

With Open Nose Surgery You Can Get A Natural-looking Nose

Rhinoplasty is a complex operation that requires a long recovery. The first few days, the patient should wear a hard fixing form – splint and bandage.

  1. Hospital stay – at least for two days.
  2. Removal of sutures – the fifth-seventh day after rhinoplasty.
  3. Removing fixing bandages – one and a half weeks after the procedure.
  4. Rhinoplasty has the side effects of a “bruised”, which disappear after two weeks.
  5. A small, barely perceptible swelling may persist longer, sometimes up to several months. Because the final result of the operation can be evaluate only 4-6 months after rhinoplasty.

Rhinoplasty San Diego will require rejection of serious physical activities (work, sports) in the first six weeks after the procedure. Also contraindicated stay in the sun, in the bathhouse and sauna, and the wearing of glasses.
