Rhinoplasty Recovery Time and Downtime?
Returning to public after rhinoplasty.
Nov 5, 2017
I remove nasal splints around day 5 after rhinoplasty. Most patients return to work or school 7-9 days after surgery. Exercises and wearing glasses can be resumed 4 weeks after surgery.
Rhinoplasty Recovery
Recovery after rhinoplasty
Nov 5, 2017
We give the following instructions to our patients after rhinoplasty. These instructions may be modified for each patient depending on what was performed during surgery.
First day after surgery: Keep you upper back elevated 30-40 degrees. Apply cold compress (cloth soaked in cold water or crushed ice in a glove placed over a cloth) during the first 24 hours. Facial swelling tends to get worse during the first 3 days after surgery and improves by…
How Much Does Rhinoplasty Cost?
It is a wide range
Nov 5, 2017
The price varies geographically and depends on surgery complexity, duration, and surgeon’s practice. Revision rhinoplasty tends to be more expensive. Although the price is an important factor in selecting a surgeon, it is more important to ensure that your surgeon specializes in rhinoplasty, able to perform both cosmetic and functional aspects of rhinoplasty, performs this surgery frequently, and has numerous before and after photos.
Is it possible to get as excellent a result with closed rhinoplasty as open?
I prefer closed rhinoplasty
Sep 24, 2017
Yes, closed rhinoplasty can produce excellent results depending on closed rhinoplasty techniques used surgeon’s experience. Over 90% of my practice is dedicated to closed rhinoplasty.
