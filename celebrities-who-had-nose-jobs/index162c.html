How To Get A Celebrity Nose Job

There is nothing special about a celebrity rhinoplasty other than the fact that they are famous and most people know about it. In fact, if you look at celebrities, some have good nose jobs and others have bad ones. Here are some tips on how to get your own good celebrity rhinoplasty. Find an experienced rhinoplasty surgeon. Look at a lot of before and after photos and ask to see side and front views.

The hardest view to improve is the front, so beware if only the side view is always shown. The simplest plan is not always the best. Talk to several surgeons and ask how they will fix your nose.

There are many techniques to improve each aspect of the nose and every millimeter counts. If the plan sounds too simple the results are not going to be spectacular. You get what you pay for. Although price is not the ultimate factor (the most expensive is not always the best) you can rest assured that a cheap nose job will be exactly that.

You are paying for experience and for someone to customize a natural rhinoplasty for your face. (Adam Hamawy, MD, Princeton Plastic Surgeon)
