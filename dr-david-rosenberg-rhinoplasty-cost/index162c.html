What surgery is available to fix asymmetrical nose? What is the price range?
Considering Rhinoplasty
Jul 17, 2019
Rhinoplasty can correct nasal asymmetry. This may involve cartilage or bone reduction. Consult with an experienced, board certified facial plastic surgeon to see if you are a good candidate. Costs may vary. 
Am I a better candidate for a non-surgical or surgical rhinoplasty?
Considering Rhinoplasty
Jul 1, 2019
Rhinoplasty can correct a dorsal hump and maintain proportions of the nose. Liquid nose jobs are limited in that they can only camouflage defects and can be quite risky as far as skin necrosis. 
Is it possible to narrow the Alar (wings) and the upper parts since I have a wide tip that’s covered b
Considering Rhinoplasty
Jul 1, 2019
It is certainly  possible to narrow the base of the nose with alar reduction. The scars generally are well-hidden. Consult with an experienced facial plastic surgeon specializing in rhinoplasty. 
Is it possible to get a nose job without much change in front profile?
Considering Rhinoplasty
Jun 28, 2019
You can absolutely have rhinoplasty just to correct the profile (side view), however sometimes minor adjustments are needed to other areas to keep the nose in balance. An endonasal approach can bypass your tip and allow the surgeon to reduce your bridge alone. 
How would my nose need to be reshaped in order to suit my face?
Considering Rhinoplasty
Jun 27, 2019
You look like a great candidate for rhinoplasty with hump reduction and tip refinement. Your tip may need support or even a tip graft to correct the proportion to your bridge, and that will correct the feminine supra-tip break you are looking for. 
Rhinoplasty then lip lift, or lip lift then rhinoplasty?
Rhinoplasty and lip lift
Jun 19, 2019
Rhinoplasty and lip lift can be performed at the same time if no significant change is being made to the base of the nose, however if forced to choose I would suggest performing the rhinoplasty first and then reassessing the lip.
