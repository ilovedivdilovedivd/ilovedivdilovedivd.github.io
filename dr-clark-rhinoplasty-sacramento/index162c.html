What Is The Proper Way For Me To Clean The Dried Mucus Out Of My Nose 3 Weeks Post-Op?
cleaning nostrils
Oct 29, 2018
Hello,
Since I do closed rhinoplasty, it is important to gently clean inside the nostrils where the suture lines are located. We recommend using saline nasal spray in each nostril a few times a day, followed by a q-tip dipped in hydrogen peroxide and gently applied inside each nostril. You can also apply bacitracin ointment inside your nostrils as well. Check with your surgeon’s office for their specific instructions as they may vary.
