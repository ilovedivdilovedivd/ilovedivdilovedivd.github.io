Hair transplant
Feb 13, 2017
Hair transplant will not cure baldness, it only works to restore lost hair.  You will continue to lose more hair.  It is important that you begin a medical therapy such as Minoxidil, Finasteride, Low Level Laser Therapy or PRP to ensure that you stop the progression of hair loss.
