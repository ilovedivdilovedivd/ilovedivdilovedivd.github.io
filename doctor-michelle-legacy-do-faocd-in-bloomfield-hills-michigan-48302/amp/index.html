I’m a 38 years old thyroid patient weighing 78 kgs (171 lbs). How to keep my cheeks from loo
Nonsurgical Cheek Augmentation
Jan 28, 2015
A great way to prevent fat pads in the cheek area from falling is to use soft tissue fillers in the area over time. Juvederm Voluma is FDA approved for cheek augmentation and has been shown to last 2 years. Another option for long term results is Sculptra. It is placed deep, under the fat pads, to add volume to an aging face. This too lasts 2 years or longer and leads to a very natural-looking rejuvenation.

Keep in mind that this is off label and should be done carefully as it can weaken the ability to form a tight seal with your lips.
