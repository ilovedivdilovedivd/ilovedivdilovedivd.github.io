What age I can resort to rhinoplasty?

Usually nose job from aesthetic purposes is performed at the age of 18-40 years, but sometimes age does not matter and is not a contraindication.

It is not recommended to do a rhinoplasty up to 18 years old, as during this period are formed by bony structures of the facial skeleton and the nose have not yet fully formed.

Types of rhinoplasty in Dallas TX

Nasal Tip Rhinoplasty In Dallas, Texas With Dr. C. Spencer Cochran

Open rhinoplasty. The incision is performed from the exterior of of the nose, then the skin is separated from the bone and the surgeon performs the necessary correction of form. The wound is closed with a cosmetic seam. On the nose is superimposed a plaster cast, and tampons are inserted into the nostrils.

Closed rhinoplasty. The incision is made only on the inner side of the nose, so that scars are absolutely imperceptible in the future. Wounds sutured using bioresorbable thread.

Non-surgical rhinoplasty. The nose and its shape are changing by injecting hyaluronic acid or implantation of adipose tissue (lipofilling) taken from a patient from other body parts.
