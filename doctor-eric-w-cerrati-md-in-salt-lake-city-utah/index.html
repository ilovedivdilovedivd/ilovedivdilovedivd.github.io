Is there any way to keep the fillers in your lip looking at its best for a longer period of time?
Lip Filler
Apr 26, 2019
Lip augmentation with Volbella (also Juvederm and Restylane) should last close to 1 year. This is obviously an average length of time and some people tend to be fast metabolizers. Most patients like the initial swelling that occurs during the first week. The slight decrease in size that occurs after about 1 week is normal and is not an indication of the filler not lasting. If you want a greater augmentation, additional filler can be placed at any time.
Best of luck.
