Sacramento Nose Operation

Rhinoplasty In Sacramento, California is the most common operation in aesthetic surgery, which consists in change of form nose.

The choice of surgical approach depends on the type of deformation of the external nose, as well as the preference of the surgeon.

There are two options: the first – open rhinoplasty the incision is carried out in the area of the skin of the nasal septum, the second – closed rhinoplasty – an incision made from the side a nasal mucosa.

Undoubtedly, the most favorable is a closed method from an aesthetic point of view, which leaves no visible scarring on the face.

However, it requires a certain experience of the surgeon, since the operation is carried out almost blindly.

Rhinoplasty takes an average of one – two hours. The volume of the operation depends on the initial form of nose, as well as the chosen method of rhinoplasty.

The first stage of any rhinoplasty is an incision of the vestibule mucosa of the nose. Next, the surgeon proceeds to the correction of the end of the nose.

In some cases, you may need to changes the width of the base of the nose and nostrils height. The final contour of the nasal bridge can be formed using own (removed in the correction of the tip of the nose) or preserved cartilage.

Deformation of the nose of various origins, usually accompanied by a change in position of the nasal septum. Therefore, interference play an important role in the restoration of nasal breathing function and are one of the tasks in the performance of any rhinoplasty.

Before And After Surgery On Nose Sacramento, California Pic

A forming bandage is superimposed to maintain the modeled form of nose. In the nasal cavity through each nostril administered gauze pads that create internal support tissues of the nose.

It should be remembered that the nose created on the operating table in the future is undergoing changes, which the surgeon can not predict with mathematical precision.

This process will depend on the individual characteristics of the healing and thickness skin, a tendency to scarring, and many other factors.

Usually after the rhinoplasty does not appear of painful sensations, however slight malaise, and headache may occur which are reduced to a minimum after taking the pain medication prescribed by your doctor.

Sacramento, CA Nose Surgery Before And After

Retaining bandage is removed 5-6 days after rhinoplasty in Sacramento, turundy are retrieved after 1-3 days. The stitches are removed 1-2 days after plastic surgery on nose, and then the edges of cut are fixed with special glue for optimum healing and the formation of imperceptible scars.

Gluings are removed approximately after 5-7 days. In closed rhinoplasty (where incisions are made in the nasal cavity) stitches are absorbed on their own.

The return to habitual life – revoery period

California Surgery For Nose Can Narrow The Span Of The Nostrils

In the early days of swelling and bruising in the periorbital area may be increased, but since the 3-4 days they will be gradually reduced and soon will disappear without a trace. After 1-2 weeks the nose will get a perfectly acceptable appearance. However, the nose is be finally formed after 6-12 months after nose reshaping.

Should refrain from physical activity (including various sports) within 2 to 3 months after nasal surgery. Be especially careful in the care of the skin around the nose, as it is area will be sensitive to various injury after surgery.

It is advisable to abandon the mechanical cleaning of the face, and not to visit bath or sauna for several months. You can not wear glasses after removing the bandage within 6-7 weeks, possible to use contact lenses if necessary.

Healing is a gradual process. The patient will see reshaping nose directly after the operation, but the final result will be much later. Every day, your nose will look better and better. After a week or two, the patient will return to normal life but with some limitations.

The patient should be visited the doctor at the scheduled time, so that he could monitor the healing process. The patient should inform your surgeon if there are any unusual sensations in any period after nose plastic surgery.
