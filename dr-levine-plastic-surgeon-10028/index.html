After a rhinoplasty, are the muscles in the nose still able to function and flare your nostrils?
You can "flare" your nostrils after rhinoplasty
Jan 29, 2020
The nasal muscles should be able to function normally after a rhinoplasty. Patient may notice that they have decreased function in the immediate period after surgery as swelling limits this motion.
