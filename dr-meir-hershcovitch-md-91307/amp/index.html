Under eye circles after 1 month post op rhinoplasty? Any remedies?
hemoglobin deposits
Sep 26, 2016
Dear LLL111,

This type of problem occurs when red blood cells settle in your skin from bruising. The hemoglobin (protein that carries oxygen) from your red blood cells can create dark circles. That protein is eaten up by one of your white blood cells (called macrophages). These will then slowly remove the hemoglobin debris from the area. But you are correct, this can take months. Congestion after Septo/Rhinoplasty, is this normal?
Nasal Congestion
Sep 13, 2016
Hi,Congestion and rhinorrhea (runny nose) after a septorhinoplasty is not uncommon. However to be having this problem 4 months out is quite unusual. Especially unusual if you didn't have nasal congestion or allergies before the operation. Typically this procedure will improve your nasal breathing and runny nose. I would consider having it examined and scoped to ensure nothing serious is going on. Hope this helps!--Dr. Hershcovitch
