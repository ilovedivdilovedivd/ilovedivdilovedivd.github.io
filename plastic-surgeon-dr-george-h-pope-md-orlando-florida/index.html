SculpSure and hernias
Mar 22, 2017
An untreated ventral or abdominal wall hernia is a contraindication for SculpSure treatment, meaning someone with that condition cannot have SculpSure treatment in that area. But if the hernia has been repaired, SculpSure treatment is OK.
