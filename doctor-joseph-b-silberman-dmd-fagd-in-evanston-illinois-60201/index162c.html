I hate my Veneers, can I get them changed? Will it be harmful for my teeth?
Veneers
Apr 21, 2020
Your veneers aren’t really that bad. My initial thought was they were too square and aggressive appearing but your before photo shows that your original teeth were also square. Though they might appear slightly large, your overall smile would look better if you had done 2 more veneers, 1 on each side on the teeth behind your canines. This way, your smile would be broader and brighter. Otherwise, you would need all of them remade and you may not really need to do that. I hope this helps!

If you’re getting a bridge on the right side is it necessary to do it on the left side?
Bridge
Apr 5, 2020
The bridge on your right  side should match how your teeth appear on your left side assuming your mouth is consistent on both sides  From a color matching standpoint, both sides should match. You definitely do not need to do anything to the teeth on the opposite side to make both sides match. I hope this helps!
Hello there, are my canine teeth suitable for recontouring?
Prominent Canine
Mar 29, 2020
Your issue isn’t that your canines are big, it’s that the teeth in between your canines and very front teeth ( lateral incisors) are set back and that gives an illusion of prominent canines. Ideally, you should do Invisalign to move your teeth into better positions but you could also have bonding placed on the lateral incisors to build them out and your canines won’t look as big  Recontouring  them won’t be your answer. I hope this helps!
Can my two front teeth be fixed with composite bonding?
Bonding
Mar 15, 2020
You have triangularly shaped front teeth and rounded edges. In your situation, I would smooth the edges of the front teeth to make them appropriate, followed by just bonding the sides of the 2 front ones to close up the black triangle appearance. You do not need to bond the edges of your front teeth. I think you would be very happy with that process. I hope this helps!
