Eyelid Surgery Recovery
Eyelid Surgery Recovery Time
Dec 14, 2017
Following eyelid surgery there is very little pain. It is common to see swelling and bruising for a week to 10 days following the surgery. All the sutures are removed 6 days post-operative. After 7 days, cover-up make-up can be applied to conceal the bruising. Most of my patients return to work between a week and 10 days following surgery. It is always wise to choose a well-trained, experienced, and board certified plastic surgeon.
