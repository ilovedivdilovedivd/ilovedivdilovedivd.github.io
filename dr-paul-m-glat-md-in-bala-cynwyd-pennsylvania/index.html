What are the chances of developing keloids with a hair transplant on a 40-year-old with history of kel
Keloids Are Unpredictable
Dec 1, 2021
Keloids are unpredictable but extremely rare in hair transplant surgery, especially during an FUE procedure. There is no way to tell if one wound will keloid while another will not. The patch test would not guarantee that you wouldn't develop a keloid from the larger procedure. Your risk is certainly increased and you would have to decide if this risk were worth taking to achieve your personal goals from the procedure.
