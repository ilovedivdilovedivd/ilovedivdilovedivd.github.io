Why do I still feel heavy around the tip 3 years post rhinoplasty?
Feeling heavy around the nasal tip Pushing tip to breath properly
Dec 14, 2020
The enclosed photograph clearly shows the asymmetry of the tip of your nose which can occur after a rhinoplasty
You should return to the original surgeon if possible to discuss options likely a secondary rhinoplasty
Otherwise seek out an experienced surgeon who will examine your nose perhaps do nasal function studies to determine your problem and can make suggestions as to the repair of your nasal tip.
I’ve been told that rhinoplasty on thick skin is very difficult. Could you advise
Rhinoplasty and thick skin
Dec 29, 2019
Many people requesting rhinoplasty have thick skin.The thickness may be a racial characteristic or secondary to overactive sebaceous glands.If the skin is thick we can sometimes thin it with oral or topical agents before and after the rhinoplasty.At surgery a fatty layer under the tip is thinned when present.Post operative care of the nose with thin skin may require prolonged taping or steroid injections.Thick skin doesn’t preclude you from a rhinoplasty it just has to be managed!
Should I get a genioplasty and/or rhinoplasty? r
Genioplasty and or Rhinoplaty
Dec 22, 2019
Doing the combination genioplasty and rhinoplasty is the best combination to improve your profile and readjust the bony harmony of your face.Being Asian its important you communicate carefully as to your aesthetic ideals.Computer imaging will help.You may have had orthodontics and your occlusion needs to be assessed.An experienced surgeon will take you through these steps so you can decide what is best for you.
