Is it the best idea to have secondary Asian rhinoplasty with the previous doctor?
revision of Asian rib rhinoplasty
Jan 13, 2020
The number of surgeons experienced in Asian rib rhinoplasty is fairly small. Revision rhinoplasty fall into two categories: major and minor. Minor revisions are generally best handled by the primary surgeons – slight asymmetry, slight irregularity, etc. If a major revision is needed, which is basically the need to redo the entire rhinoplasty, then you need to figure out what led to the problem.
