Swollen and Big Nose Tip After 3 Months of Rhinoplasty?
Swelling after Rhinoplasty
Nov 13, 2018
It really takes 12 months to see the effects of rhinoplasty.There is usually a great deal of swelling that need to resolve. In addition the skin needs to shrink to the size of the newly created underlying framework.I tell my patients that to see the full effect in the tip of the nose, it takes at least 9-12 months. For patients with thicker skin and soft tissues, this may take 18-24 months.Hope this helps.Best,Dr Sajjadian
