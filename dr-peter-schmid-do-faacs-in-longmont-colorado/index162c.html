Will Latisse Change Eye Color?
Latisse should not affect the color of your eyes.
Jan 10, 2011
Thank you for the inquiry. To my knowledge, there haven't been any cases reported in which Latisse affected the color of the iris.
How Long Does Latisse Last? Are Results Permanent?
Latisse will continue to work while you are using the product.
Jan 10, 2011
Latisse works during the growth cycle of the lashes to make them longer and darker. You should continue to see modest improvements after 16 weeks if you continue usage. However, once you discontinue Latisse, your lashes will not continue in this manner.
Maximum Length / Limit with Latisse?
Latisse works in growth cycle of the lashes
Jan 10, 2011
Excellent question. You may experience ongoing growth after 16 weeks, you should also see that the lashes are fuller and darker.
Best Tips for Rejuvenating Skin to Reverse Signs of Aging
Protection and prevention is the first step to improving the skin.
Dec 30, 2010
If you are looking only for at-home treatments, the first stop is the sun care aisle. Everyday apply sunscreen, even in winter! Next is find a good skin care line. Depending upon your skin type, I recommend a variety of lines including Obagi Nuderm and Obagi CRx, Epionce, SkinCeuticals and Retin A.

You really need to speak to a qualified skin care consultant/aesthetician or your physician for the best recommendation. But remember, topicals will offer limited results for wrinkles, etc.

What is the Best Topical Facial Filler?
No topical on the market will provide results you desire.
Dec 30, 2010
Topicals simply don't penetrate the skin deep enough to stimulate the collagen that would be necessary to "plump" the skin. You will need to look to other treatments for real results. Juvederm, Restylane and other Hyaluronic Acid (HA) fillers would be the least downtime and expense for you. Fat transfer is another option.

Will Most Surgeons Draw on Your Body Before Liposuction?

Dec 16, 2010
Whenever I perform body liposuction, I mark my patients prior to anesthesia in order to create my surgical plan, answer any last minute questions and confirm the plan with the patient. I also take the "before" photographs at this time. All of this helps to clarify communication with the patient. 
Why Can't I Apply Latisse to Lower Lid or Eyebrows?
Off label usage of Latisse has been working
Dec 16, 2010
Latisse is indicated and FDA-approved for the eyelashes on the upper eyelids. However, many people have been using it "off-label" on the eyebrows with some results. Just note that once you stop using the product, the results will eventually stop as well.
After 16 Weeks of Latisse Will the Maintain Process Continue to Grow Your Lashes?
You'll need to continue to use Latisse to maintain results.
Dec 16, 2010
Thanks for your inquiry. Because Latisse works on the lashes that are in the "growth" phase, discontinuing it will eventually reverse any results you see. Long term usage is needed for long term results.
Is Fraxel Laser Safe for People with Jaw Reconstruction?
Fractional laser treatments should be fine.
Dec 16, 2010
Your jaw reconstruction is much deeper than any of the fractional lasers (Matrix, Fraxel, etc.) will penetrate and therefore should be fine to get the treatment.
How Long Before Botox Results Can Be Seen?
Botox usually begins working at 3-5 days post-injection.
Dec 16, 2010
Thank you for your inquiry. Botox usually begins working approximately 3-5 days after the injection. Some considerations are how many units you receive (I usually do not recommend less than 30 units). It should last 3-4 months.
Natural Look Lip Augmentation?

With age the lips do tend to loose the plumpness look of youth. The fillers we use today, Juvederm and Restylane for example, are great for giving a more youthful appearance to the lips without looking overdone. Just tell to your facial plastic surgeon your goals for the procedure.

Dec 12, 2010
Thanks for your question. Both Juvederm and Restylane are used in the lips with excellent results. The most important thing is the skill of the injector, so be sure you find an experiences facial plastic surgeon or plastic surgeon for your procedure.
