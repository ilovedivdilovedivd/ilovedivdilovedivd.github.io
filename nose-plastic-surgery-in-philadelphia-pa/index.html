Rhinoplasty is the second most popular plastic surgery in the world. Such statistics lead international health organizations. Rhinoplasty in Philadelphia is done by men, women and even for children for medical reasons.

Rhinoplasty is a very popular among celebrities. Some film and pop stars does not conceal that the the nose made the correction. For example, actress and social activist, UN Ambassador Angelina Jolie and singer, Queen of the French chanson Patricia Kaas.

Rhinoplasty helps not only to improve the appearance, but also to solve problems with nasal breathing, while at the same time relieve the brain from oxygen starvation, promote restful sleep and improve memory. This is the data of numerous investigations.

The plastic surgeon makes a few dozen operations on the nose of a year. Extensive experience allows to solve even unusual situation with the correction of the nose.

What problems can rhinoplasty solve?

Too long or short nose, the consequences of injury or fracture, hump, drooped tip of the nose, a wide nasal bridge – all of these drawbacks is easy to eliminate with nose job. In addition, rhinoplasty is indicated in patients with nasal breathing problems.

On the recommendation of the surgeon Rhinoplasty can be performed in parallel with septoplasty (septum alignment) for the resumption of the respiratory function of the nose.

The consequences of one or multiple fractures, deformation of external tissues of and the shape of the nose also eliminates the plastic nose (rhinoplasty).

Rhinoplasty in Philadelphia allows simultaneously perform and other plastic surgeries such as lip augmentation and chin, giving the visual effect of reducing the nose and improving facial proportions.

Rhinoplasty: how to prepare for surgery?

At the first meeting with a plastic surgeon the patient must explain the problem in detail. Only an accurate and complete description of all its complaints gives a guarantee of getting maximum desired result.

Rhinoplasty, like any surgery will require a full medical examination. The plastic surgeon must carefully conduct a survey of the quality of the skin and the structure of the patient’s nose. It is mandatory should be made photographs and computer model of the nose, the patient could clearly see what changes are planned, and what to expect after the rhinoplasty.

At the final stage of preparation is conducted radiography and consultation with an otolaryngologist.

In case of serious problems associated with the curvature of the nasal septum, the patient is assigned a complex rhinoplasty, which in addition to a plastic surgeon takes part an otolaryngologist. Rhinoplasty is performed on patients 18 years and older.
