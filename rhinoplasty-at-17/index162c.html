When is the right age for rhinoplasty?

When to undergo a rhinoplasty operation is determined by several factors. Physical maturity is important, and most people are ready by the age of 16-17.

Girls do mature a bit sooner, and may have reached full facial growth by 15. Emotional maturity is another important factor.

All surgical candidates must have a full awareness of the changes to be made, and accept that there are limitations to what a surgeon can achieve.

With realistic expectations and clear goals shared by both patient and surgeon, happy results can occur. (Ira D. Papel, MD, Baltimore Facial Plastic Surgeon)

Rhinoplasty requires maturity of mind and body.

Cosmetic surgery requires emotional maturity and developmental/physical maturity. Different people achieve both of these at different ages.
