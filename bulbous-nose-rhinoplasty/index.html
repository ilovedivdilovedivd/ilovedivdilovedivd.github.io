How does look a bulbous nose?

The nose is round with a thick tip called “bulbous”. It gives to the image of kindness and naivety. Many women are not satisfied with the massive shape of the nose, and they resort to all kinds of techniques and cosmetic procedures to correct the facial features, that nature gave us.

The massive nose is caused by a complex internal structure – branched cartilages on the tip and an abundance of soft tissue. As we age the nose may become wider due to loss of elasticity of the ligaments.

Visually to correct a defect of the nose is quite difficult, in contrast to the eyes and lips that can be corrected with the help of makeup.

Therefore, people from around the world have resorted to plastic surgery and non-surgical procedures that allow to cope with the defect and feel truly beautiful.
