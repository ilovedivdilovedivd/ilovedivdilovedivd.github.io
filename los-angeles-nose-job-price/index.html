Los Angeles Nose Job Price

Rhinoplasty in Los Angeles is one of the most difficult sections of plastic surgery consists in change of outer contour of the nose by smoothing, deleting or moving the bones and cartilage that form its basis.

With the help of rhinoplasty is possible to reduce or increase the size of the nose, change its shape, as well as to correct congenital defects and breathing problems (respirogenesis).

When planning your surgery, it is desirable to have a photo of computer simulations during the consultation.

At the stage of consultation, the surgeon will evaluate the structure of your nose and will discuss with you the options, explain the factors that can affect the surgery and its outcome.

These factors include the structure of the nasal bones and cartilage, the shape of the face, skin thickness and age. If previously you had any surgery or trauma of the nose, if you have any allergies or breathing difficulties, be sure to report it.

Rhinoplasty is carried out only in a hospital under general anesthesia in conjunction with local infiltration anesthesia. Nose job lasts 1,5-2 hours, although more complex procedures may take and more time. Nose reshaping consists in the separation of nose leather from cartilage and bone, which will provide a desired shape. After that, the skin is restored.

Also, Los Angeles rhinoplasty is performed from inside – incision is made inside the nostrils. In severe cases, open method is preferable when a small incision is made across the nose. Minimum stay in hospital is usually 1-3 days depending on the volume of surgical intervention.

In the postoperative period, to stop bleeding after nose beauty surgery, nasal tampons are inserted, which are removed after 2-5 days during which the patient is breathing through the mouth. In many cases, special plates (protectors) from soft polyurethane are inserted into the nasal passages to retain the nasal septum in the right position.

Protectors are not visible, they are removed 3 weeks after plastic surgery on nose. After nose job on the face superimposed emplastic (sometimes with the addition of plaster strips) bandage which is removed after 8-10 days. The first bandaging is done on the next day after plastic surgery for nose.

Always observed swelling and skin discoloration (bruising) around the eyes, which pass up to 90% in two weeks. Completely swelling of tissues passes over a longer period (up to 6 months).

Time when the patient can return to their daily activities, is determined by its state of health. Usually this is possible within 5-7 days after nose operation. In many cases, it is strongly recommended to refrain from wearing glasses for 2-4 weeks after nose job, since it can significantly impair the result.

Closed rhinoplasty in Los Angeles CA is a plastic surgery aimed at reshaping (remodeling) of the nose, which is produced without a visible scar on the skin. Access to the necessary structures of the nose is performed through an incision inside the nose.

The complexity of closed rhinoplasty is that the entire operation must be carried out through the nostrils with limited visibility.

By performing closed rhinoplasty, the surgeon can easily control the outcome, because the skin has not been displaced, compared to an open rhinoplasty.

Nostril Surgery May Performed In Combination With Other Procedures Designed To Address The Functionality Of The Nose

The main requirement for the closed rhinoplasty is the understanding of the complex anatomy of the nose and the ability to “see through the skin” of the nose for planning of the necessary steps.

During the consultation the plastic surgeon examines the nose in several parameters: the breath, the nasal septum, the length of the nose, the width and the asymmetry of the nasal bone, the width and contour of the nostrils, and others.

As the result of examination, the surgeon determines which options you want to change and what to leave to avoid an imbalance between the “new perfect nose” and other facial features.

Very frequently asked question: “Is closed rhinoplasty better than the open?” On this issue there is no clear “Yes” and affirmative “no”. Both rhinoplasty techniques are modern and successfully used in plastic surgery. Although both in open and in closed rhinoplasty may be undesirable results.

The debate about which method of Los Angeles rhinoplasty is preferable – open or closed – many plastic surgeons have come to the conclusion that if the task of primary rhinoplasty is correct minor defects then the surgeon can use the closed access.

If nose reshape surgery is planned with the use of cartilage grafts or re-rhinoplasty, the open access will be used more efficiently.
