Is 40 Too Young for a Facelift?
Surgery for sags and bags!
Nov 2, 2018
A facelift or mini-facelift helps reposition sagging tissue. If you don't have sagging you would not see improvement with a facelift. However, a facelift will not restore a loss of volume. Facial fillers or sometimes fat transfer may help when hollow areas develop.
Best Age for First Facelift?
Varies!
Nov 2, 2018
A facelift may help when sagging is not relieved by rest. Permanent facial sagging may occur as early as the 30s, but more usually in one's 40s, 50s, or 60s. Everyone's genetics are different and surgery is still the best way to reset the hands of time!
