Restylane can swell for 48 hours, it can also be shaped or dissolved
Jun 6, 2011
Hyaluronic acid fillers including Restylane cause swelling for about 48 hours, and it is hard to judge the treatment during this time period. Bruising under the skin can also make the treatment seem more prominent, and this can last two weeks. After two weeks, if the fullness is still too much, your physician can massage the Restylane to shape it under the skin.
