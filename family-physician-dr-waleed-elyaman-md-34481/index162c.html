Laser hair removal on beard regret. Can I get a transplant?
Hair removal .
Dec 26, 2017
So in general, most patients who have laser hair removal eventually do regrow hair. Its usually a couple of years down the line. One option you can consider is Platelet rich plasma or PRP. It involves drawing your own blood, spinning it down, and harvesting the platelet rich plasma. Then it will be injected/microneedled into your skin. This same technique is used for hair growth on the head. Not a guarantee, but it should help.
