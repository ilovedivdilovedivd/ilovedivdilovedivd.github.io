Wider implants are not the answer

Wider implants are not the answer. To be sure I really need to view you laying down to see how your chest shape is. Also side views are important. How Long for Swelling to Go Down After Mini Abdominoplasty?
Recovery time after tummy tuck or mini tuck
Jun 21, 2012
You can expect swelling up to 6 months after a tumm yck procedure. If you have seroma, then you may require longer recovery period. Seroma is not necessary a complication of tummy tuck, but rather an unanticipated but not unusual event. As long as it is drained properly, you should be fine. Daily massaging of the scare area will expedite the reduction of swelling. Be patient and trust your surgeon.
How Long Should I Wait to Buy New Bras After Breast Implants?
Shopping for bra after breast augmentation
Jun 20, 2012
Every one reacts differently. As a rule, 4-6 weeks should be enough time. I do tell my breast augmentation patients to just buy a few bras at week 4-6 and get more after a few more weeks just in case if the breast size changed again.

And finally, don’t forget to donate your old bras to the needy. Enjoy your new breasts in good health.

1 Month Post Breast Implants, and The Crease Is Uneven and Not as Full as I’d Like?
Breast Implant shape and size after surgery
Jun 20, 2012
One month post op is fairly a short time to give you final results. You will have some swelling up to 6 months following the surgery, although most swelling should be gone by months 4 or 5. Also your implants are still settling and are probably sitting high. There is good chance that the implants will fall into proper place, so have some faith and give it some time. In my practice, the most common complaint after a breast augmentation surgery is “I wish I had gone bigger”.
Seriously Considering the Brazilian Butt Augmentation, What is the Price Range for this Procedure?
Plastic Surgery Cost
Jun 20, 2012
The price depends on how much time is needed in operating room to perform this procedure. I also assume it varies depending on what state or area of country you are located. The time is dictated by how much fat is needed to be removed for fat grafting, how many areas of liposuction as donor site, and then flipping you on the operating room table, processing and purifying the fat and transferring to the buttock area for augmentation. The price in Southern California is $8500 to $12,000.

How Do I Sleep After Mommy Makeover?
Mommy Makeover Recovery
Jun 20, 2012
Use a lot of pillows on your back. Also sleeping on the sides and hugging pillows will help you. Percocet should not be used to help you sleep. It will reduce the pain but will not induce sleep and it will make you constipated, which will cause additional problems. Lot of walking will increase blood flow and circulation and will expedite the recovery. Think about the final results in order to encourage yourself.
