How long will PRP for hair loss take to see the results?
About 3 to 6 months
Nov 16, 2018
Generally, it takes about 3 to 6 months to see results. The key is the diagnosis (the type of hair loss you have). There are > 10 types of hair loss and some respond faster than others. Either way, we find that patients who undergo PRP do better than those that do not.

Is Trusculpt the Same as Coolsculpt?
I prefer truSculpt 3D over CoolScuplt
Oct 26, 2018
TruSculpt 3D uses radiofrequency to heat fat cells while CoolSculpt freezes fat cells. To help put the difference in perspective: we use truSculpt 3D to repair the dimpling/rippling caused by CoolSculpt. truSculpt 3D also tightens skin, a benefit not seen with CoolSculpt.
Trusculpt for Fat and Cellulite?
truSculpt 3D is effective
Oct 26, 2018
TruSculpt 3D uses radiofrequence to kill fat cells. Unlike other bodysculpting devices, we do not see dimplling or worsening of cellulite with truSculpt 3D in our practice. Patients typically see results in 8 weeks, typically after 1 treatment.
