Could I have a closed rhino or will a nose filler look good with my nose?
Liquid versus surgical Rhinoplasty
Jul 15, 2019
Liquid or ‘non-surgical rhinoplasty’ involves using filler to smooth out and build up the nose, making a bump disappear on the bridge or have the nose look narrower from the front. The best candidates are those with flat or small noses that need additional height in their bridge. When the nose has a very tall profile, surgical Rhinoplasty is a much better option, since it makes the nose smaller, not bigger.
