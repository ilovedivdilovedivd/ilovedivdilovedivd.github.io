Is it possible to change the shape of my nostrils?
Rhinoplasty age
Apr 22, 2015
It would be best to wait until you are about 18 to consider
rhinoplasty to ensure you are fully grown. Rhinoplasty and alar reduction
surgery can help change the shape of your nostrils.
Can somebody tell me if my nose looks like this from swelling?
swelling post rhinoplasty
Apr 22, 2015
You are still in the very early stages of settling down.
Swelling can often distort the nose and can change every day for the first few
months. Unfortunately you just need to be patient and wait for the swelling to
subside before you will see the final results of your rhinoplasty.

It is best not to exercise during the first few weeks as this
can make your nose swell. Best to wait for your check up with your surgeon
before you start exercising. Feeling depressed can be common for the first few
