What is better for flat cheekbones: Radiesse or Sculptra? Can they be combined?
Cheek Augmentation
Jan 22, 2020
Thanks for your question.  Both are fillers, but very different.  They can be combined.  Radiesse is an actuall filler material that will immediately lift tissues and give a 3D effect.  Sculptra is an injectable "biomodulator" meaning it does not fill immediately but stimulates your skin and soft tissues collagen to increase and then lift the tissues, it can take weeks to see an improvement.  Your provider should be able to instruct and give recommentations. Hopefully, this information helps!
