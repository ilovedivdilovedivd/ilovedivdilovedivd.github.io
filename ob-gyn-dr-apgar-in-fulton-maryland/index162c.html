What is the most effective treatment for underarm sweating hyperhidrosis? Infini or Miradry
Miradry
Oct 7, 2019
Miradry seems to be the gold standard as most patients experience significant reduction in underarm sweating, hair, and odor. The treatment is very well tolerated whereas, Infini can be quite painful and not render the same results as Miradry. For severe cases, a second treatment may be necessary for additional reduction.
