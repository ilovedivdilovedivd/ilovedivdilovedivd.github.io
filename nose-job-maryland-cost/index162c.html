It becomes clear that rhinoplasty is aimed at changing or correction of the external nose shape, as well as its internal elements, which is of great importance not only in the process of respiration.

Changing the shape of the nose is possible by increasing or decreasing its size, removing hump, correcting shape and position of the tip of the nose and the width of the nose, as well as the angle between the tip of the nose and upper lip, narrowing the nostrils.

The new nose shape is created using of these manipulations according to the aesthetic requirements of the patient.

Rhinoplasty in Maryland allows you to correct the deformation of the nose caused by traumatic injuries, unsuccessful surgical interventions or due to age-related changes.

Each operation in the area of the nose is unique, as does not exist two identical forms of the nose, and two identical operation can not be performed.

Therefore, we will describe only the basic principles of intervention in the area of the nose. You should have a better idea about the forthcoming operation, without delving the technical details.

The choice of surgical approach depends on the type of available external deformation of the nose, as well as the preference of the surgeon. There are two options: the first – the open – when the incision is carried out in the area of the skin of the nasal septum, the second – closed – cut extends from the side nasal mucosa.

There is no doubt that the closed method is the most favorable from an aesthetic point of view, and does not leave visible scars on his face. However, it requires some experience of the surgeon, since the operation is carried out almost blindly.

Practically any deformation can be eliminated by using a closed access (which was awarded the title of endonasal access in specialized literature). Only in rare cases it is necessary to use external incisions at presence of specific indications.

Also, the manipulation on change the position the nose wings is the exception (often necessary to reduce the width of the nostrils), in this case, can not do without incisions at the base of the nose wings. However, since incisions are located in the natural crease of the skin, they are practically invisible at the correct postoperative period.

Rhinoplasty in Maryland takes an average of one – two hours. The volume of the operation depends on the original shape of your nose, as well as method of surgery. The first stage of any rhinoplasty the incision is performed of the mucosa of the nose. The skin is peeled from the underlying bone and cartilage structures through the incision.

Then, using special tools (chisel or rasp) is removed hump nose, after this the bone basis of the nose is a truncated pyramid. Thus, the bridge of the nose becomes more subtle and elegant, and the height of the nose is reduced by the amount remote hump.

Next, the surgeon proceeds to the correction of the tip of the nose. Is carried out a partial excision alar cartilage or thinning them evenly over the entire surface. The intervention on the soft tissues of the nose end department allow us to make it more subtle, and reduce or significantly shorten.

The angle between the nose and upper lip is changed after the intervention in the cartilage of the nasal septum (wall, separating the nasal passages). In some cases, may be required change the width of the base of the nose and the height of nostrils.

This procedure involves removing a strip of skin at the base of the nose wings with their moving to a new position if necessary. In this case, the incision is in a natural skin crease at the base of the nose wing on each side.

Deformation of the nose of various origins, usually accompanied by a change in position of the nasal septum. Therefore, intervention on the internal structure play an important role in the restoration of nasal breathing function and are one of the problems when performing of any rhinoplasty.

After the nose job a plaster bandage is applied to maintain the shape of the nose in order to prevent displacement of bone and cartilages and reduce swelling.

Usually after the nose beauty surgery there are no painful sensations, but the general weakness, fever up to 37,2- 37.4 may be present in the first days after the intervention. Headache is also possible in the first two days because of the tampon in the nasal passages.

All these unpleasant sensations are reduced to a minimum after taking pain medication prescribed by your doctor. In the early days of swelling and bruising may increase in the periorbital area, but we should not be afraid of starting with 3-4 days, they will be gradually reduced and soon disappear without a trace.

It is advisable to comply with bed rest for the first two days, the doctor may also recommend to keep your head in the raised position during this time, since it is possible slight bleeding from the nasal passages. You should not neglect these recommendations.
