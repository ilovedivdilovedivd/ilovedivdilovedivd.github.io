Liquid rhinoplasty
Jun 26, 2019
Hello and thanks for the question.  Liquid rhinoplasty is definitely not indicated for all patients.  It is generally used in patients who have a weak nasal bridge that needs augmenting.  There are risks associated with the procedure. I recommended seeing a board certified rhinoplasty surgeon to discuss if you would be a candidate. Good luck.
