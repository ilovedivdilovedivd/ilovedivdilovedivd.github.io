How Much Does a Tummy Tuck Cost?
Average cost of a Tummy Tuck
Aug 23, 2017
The average price of a tummy tuck (abdominoplasty) will be determined by three factors:
1. The Surgeon's Fee
2. The Anesthesiologist's Fee
3. The Facility Fee
Depending on geography, these fees will vary. My practice, which is located in Syracuse, NY, charges $7900 for an average standard abdominoplasty. This includes all 3 fees listed above, since we have our own AAAASF operating suite in the office and collect the anesthesiologist fee and pay him directly.
