Robert N. Young, MD, FACS

Name: Robert N. Young, MD, FACS
Statement: Dr. Robert N. Young is a board-certified plastic surgeon in San Antonio, Texas, who has been in practice for three decades. He founded DrYoungForever, an AAAASF-accredited outpatient Plastic Surgery Center, as well as the YoungForever MedSpa in Shavano Park.

Dr. Young specializes in breast enhancement procedures, mommy makeovers, and skin tightening treatments. "I take my work of creating beauty very seriously,” he says.

After completing his undergraduate degree at the United States Military Academy, West Point in New York, he obtained his medical degree from Tulane University School of Medicine. From there, Dr. Young completed his residency in general surgery at Brooke Army Medical Center in San Antonio, Texas and then a fellowship in plastic surgery at Beaumont Army Medical Center.

Dr. Young returned to Fort Sam Houston, where he served as a plastic surgeon and consultant to the burn unit at the Brooke Army Medical Center. There, he served as the chief of plastic surgery and performed various major reconstructive procedures. In 1990, he retired from the army and opened his own private plastic surgery center.

Since then, Dr. Young has established himself as one of the most recognized plastic surgeons in San Antonio, Texas. Having performed more than 10,000 breast enhancement procedures, he’s also earned the nickname of “San Antonio’s breast doctor.”

Dr. Young is board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and has received membership into the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery (ASAPS), as well as the American Society of Plastic Surgeons (ASPS). He is also a fellow of the American College of Surgeons (FACS).

How long does it take to see results from CoolSculpting? Will I need more than 1 treatment?
Is more than 1 CoolSculpting visit needed?
Mar 14, 2018
In most cases, yes, more than a single visit of CoolSculpting is needed. While many get noticeable results that they're very happy with after their first treatment, it is recommended that you have at least 2 cycles. Many start seeing changes after a few weeks, but the bulk of the fat loss will occur after 2 to 3 months. An exam and consultation with an experienced CoolSculpting technician will give you a better idea of what you could expect with CoolSculpting.
