I had a rhinoplasty done 18 months ago and want to pierce my septum. Do you think this could
septal piercing
Dec 26, 2016
The septum has three layer-cartilage with mucosal lining on each side. With septal deviation repair, cartilage is removed from the center of the septum, leaving mucosal lining without much support. The safety of the piercing depends on the amount of septal cartilage left in the caudal strut. I would consult with your surgeon to get the best answer. Good luck!
Am I handicapped in anyway by having an L strut compared to full septum?
Septal L-strut
Dec 20, 2016
Dear elizabeth1993,

L-struts are very common with septal deviation repair with or without rhinoplasty. A 1cm L-strut is standard to maintain support of the nose. However, some patients need a larger strut if the cartilage is weak, or can get away with a smaller strut if the cartilage is very strong.

Cold weather will not affect your septum, whether you have an L-strut or not. No need to worry.
