Should I consume fatty foods with Accutane pill even though fatty food like peanut butter and avoca
Fatty foods and Accutane
Mar 13, 2017
Accutane does get absorbed better with fatty foods, but if fatty foods make you break out more take it with a normal meal. It should still work well for you. You could try a brand of Accutane called Absorica which the company reports good absorption regardless of the food with which the medication is taken.
How long should I wait to start accutane when I've been taking doxycycline?
A few days
Mar 13, 2017
Doxycycline does not stay in the body long after your last tablet is taken (2 days or so). Therefore, you may want to stop Doxycycline just a few days before starting Accutane. Good luck with your therapy.
