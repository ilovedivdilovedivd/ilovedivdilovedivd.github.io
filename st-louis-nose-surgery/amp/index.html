St Louis Nose Surgery

Many people often have a desire to change or improve the shape of the nose. As a rule, patients concerned about a big nose shape, the presence of a small hump, a deviated septum.

These features may be the result of injury, congenital defect or a heredity. Also, when changing the shape of the nose after an injury are often violated and its functions.

Difficulty in breathing is often leads to snoring, weakened immunity, and to frequent colds. Correction of congenital and acquired defects of the nose is engaged in such a direction of aesthetic surgery, like rhinoplasty.

Saint Louis plastic surgeons offer popular productive solutions, which, along with improved appearance, allow to return the full functionality of the respiratory system.

Rhinoplasty is recommended to eliminate the following congenital or acquired aesthetic defects:

1. correction of the form of the nasal bridge;
2. reduction in the size of the nostrils, or the correction of the nose;
3. correction forms the tip of the nose;
4. reduction in the width of the nasal bridge;
5. correction of all the other defects of the nose and nasal septum, including deviation and deformation that arose after the injury.

Removing of the nasal hump is one of the most popular operations in St Louis clinics. Such interference is shown only to adult patients. During the procedure, excess bone and cartilages is removed to the extent necessary for the formation of correct, accurate profile.

The ideal shape of the nose can be achieved only by reducing the length of the nasal septum.
