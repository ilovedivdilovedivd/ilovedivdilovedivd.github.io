Malar Bags are Anatomically Different than Lower Eyelid Bags
Jun 10, 2016
Most lower eyelid surgery is done when membrane holding the fatty tissue around the eyeball gets weak and allows the fat to bulge. Eyelid surgery itself causes some swelling and this can make the malar bag area look more swollen until the healing is complete.
