In your opinion/experience, what is the most difficult technique or maneuver during a septorhino?
In your opinion/experience, what is the most difficult technique or maneuver during a septorhino?
Jun 24, 2019
There are many challenging techniques and maneuvers required during rhinoplasty surgery. There are too many to list here.  A surgeon should have multiple tools in their toolbox to achieve the goals of the surgery.  In general, revisions on noses that have been severely compromised in form and function require multiple challenging techniques such as those requiring rib cartilage for example.
