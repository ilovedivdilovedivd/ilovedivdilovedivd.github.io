Can Botox to masseter and DAO (downturned mouth muscle) both be done at once?
Botox to masseter and DAO on same day?
Nov 20, 2018
The jawline/masseter and DAO areas are separate muscle groups and perform separate functions on the face. They can certainly be treated with Botox on the same day without any problems. I have treated the masseter and other areas on the face in one sitting without any problems in the past. Botox is an excellent product to treat both areas.
Can you have Botox in your chin area?
Botox in chin area?
Nov 15, 2018
Yes. Botox can be injected into the chin to relax the mentalis muscle which results in dimpling of the skin over the chin. Some people refer to this as a pebbly or golfball chin, but this can be suppressed by two small injections of up to 5 units/injection.

Lump after filler injection. Is this normal?
Under eye lumpiness after filler?
Oct 16, 2018
It is not unusual to have some visible lumpiness under the eyes after filler is used in the area due to the thinness of the suborbital skin. Usually, this resolves within several weeks but you should contact your injector if it does not resolve in that timeframe. If the lumpiness persists as a concern, an enzyme injection can dissolve the residual unsightly filler although this is not commonly necessary.
Is it safe to get a Botox after a flu shot that contains a very small trace of Neomycin?
Botox after a Flu shot?
Oct 16, 2018
Yes, it is completely safe to have Botox after a flu shot. The amount of neomycin in a flu shot is not enough to affect Botox in any case. Plus, Botox is injected locally in a very distant location. Don’t worry and I am glad you protected yourself from the flu with your timely vaccination.
