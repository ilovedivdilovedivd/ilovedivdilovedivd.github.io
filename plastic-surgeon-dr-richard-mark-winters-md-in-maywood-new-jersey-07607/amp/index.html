Recommendations on post-surgical issue from bacteria?
dormant infection vs. scar tissue….
Jun 19, 2022
Hard to tell whether you have residual dormant infection or just scarring… bacteria can remain colonized on the cartilage because of its poor blood supply and antibiotic penetration… this makes matters tricky…

I would likely try to fix this in 2 stages… the first a wide debridement and cultures to both make an accurate diagnosis and help clear any residual infection…. depending upon the results of that, I would treat you appropriately before definitive grafting, etc.

good luck…
