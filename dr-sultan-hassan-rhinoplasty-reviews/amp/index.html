Is my rhinoplasty officially botched? Will it collapse?
Rhinoplasty 1 month post-op
Apr 4, 2018
Having a rhinoplasty is a big deal in the sense of emotional and monetary investment let alone the physical healing. 1 month post-op is very early and I would allow some time for more healing. Seeing pre-op pictures as well as the operative note would help guide specific comments regarding potential future improvement. In the meantime, maintain close follow up with your surgeon and voice your concerns.
I wish you the best!
Babar Sultan MD
What is the difference between an open reduction and open rhinoplasty?
Osteotomy vs. Rhinoplasty
Mar 29, 2018
An open reduction is where the nasal bones are broken in a controlled fashion to correct bony deviation or cause narrowing. An open reduction can be a component of a open rhinoplasty. A rhinoplasty or nose job can address other issues as well such as the tip or projection.

All the best!
Babar Sultan MD
