Benefit and risk factor of using a tissue expander and latissimus flap on radiation breast.

Mar 22, 2017
Radiation treatment often causes permanent changes to the skin and soft tissues of the breast. The implants generally require healthy tissue coverage. Surgeons may recommend flaps (example latissimus dorsi flap) to offer new healthy tissue coverage over the implant. This requires a physical exam by a plastic surgeon to determine the best option for you.
