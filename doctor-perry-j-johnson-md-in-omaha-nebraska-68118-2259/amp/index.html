Does the nose swell after having columellar strut inserted, without any removal of cartilage?
columellar strut
Sep 23, 2015
The nose will swell a little from insertion of a columellar strut. I assume the insertion would be through a small incision on the columella. If the columellar strut is inserted by "opening the nose up" via a standard open rhinoplasty approach then the swelling will be much greater and for up to a year.

Can I get anything without scars for Breast augmentation?
Breast lift?
May 28, 2014
Although it is difficult to offer a definitive opinion based solely on pictures, I think it is very likely that you would require a breast lift for the best result. It is way too early to make an accurate assessment of your nose. Swelling, distortion and asymmetry are not uncommon during the early postoperative period.
