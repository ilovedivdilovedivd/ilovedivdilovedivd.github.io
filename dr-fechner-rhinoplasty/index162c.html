Does nasal valve collapse mean my nose has collapsed?
Nasal Valve Collapse
Apr 8, 2019
This is a very common issue for patients. It may be due to trauma, surgery or simply weakness of the cartilages of your nose. Appropriate treatment usually involves rhinoplasty surgery: The support of the nose will be improved using grafts. These grafts are made out of your cartilage and fashioned into thin plates or strips to improve the function of your nose.
