Mexico Nose Surgery

Many patients often have a desire to make your nose less noticeable. The most frequent causes of discontent: the nose is too big, with a small hump or crooked.

This may be the result of injury, congenital defect or hereditary. Most people want to improve it shape: reduce, add expressiveness, make a snub nose or any other suggestions for improvement.

Nose job in Mexico can solve many of the problems with the nose. Rhinoplasty can help reduce its size, correct the form the tip of the nose, to correct the shape of the nasal bridge or to narrow nostrils, give your face an aesthetic completeness.

Rhinoplasty is a rather popular procedure, so allow to solve the most different aesthetic problems of your nose. The most important thing is a correct choice of a plastic surgeon.

Only an experienced expert will be able to properly assess the current situation and help you make the right decision.

Plastic surgeon must to understand the anatomy of the face at the appropriate level, to have a great experience in the area of rhinoplasty, and, of course, a great aesthetic sense.

After all, as a result of the operation will change as your nose, and your face as a whole. The optimum age for rhinoplasty is 20-35 years. The important point is that by the time the bone should be finally formed.

Rhinoplasty technique is based in the fact that a plastic surgeon does not make of external incisions, working through the nostrils.

To reduce the size of the nose alar cartilages are removed that support the wings of the nose, while the septal cartilage and bone tissue was removed from the back of the nose is only partly.

Before After Rhinoplasty For Large Nostrils In Tijuana Mexico at Cosmed Clinic

If the nostrils are quite broad, rhinoplasty is performed with the removal of a small portion of the wings. When correcting the shape back of the nose are removed protruding portion of the nasal bones. If in the process of rhinoplasty Mexico City is necessary to correct the the back of the nose possible to use even silicone implants.

Each case is individual, and the surgeon makes a decision based the patient’s wishes, as well as from actually existing opportunities.

The result of rhinoplasty is not long to wait, and after a couple of weeks looking in the mirror, you will be able to say: “Now everything is as it should.”

On the site you can see photos of before and after rhinoplasty in Mexico, to read patients reviews and ask any question.
