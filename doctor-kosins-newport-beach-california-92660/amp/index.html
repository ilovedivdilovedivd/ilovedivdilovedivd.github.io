No
Jan 28, 2017
You need to wait at least 1 year for the tissues to soften.  If you reoperate on the tip at 6 months it will not go well.  See an experienced rhinoplasty specialist and you will always get the same answer.  In some patients I wait even longer than a year.Good luck,Aaron Kosins, MD
