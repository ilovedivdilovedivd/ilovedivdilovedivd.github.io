Can the length of my nostrils be reduced?
Nostrils changes in rhinoplasty
Oct 12, 2020
Thank you for the great question. Nostril shape can be changed in a variety of ways during rhinoplasty. This is accomplished by changing the shape and position of the tip cartilages, and/or modification of the nostril rim skin/fibrous tissues. Unfortunately, there is not a way to change where the nostril connects to the nasal passage. This is determined by the skeletal anatomy below and is only modified in cases of congenital stenosis; even then, repair is challenging and sometimes not…
Is it possible to derotate the tip of the nose?
Derotation of the nasal tip
Aug 25, 2020
Thank you for the question. This is a complex issue and should be discussed with your oral surgeon to determine the best timing for a rhinoplasty. It is possible to move the tip of the nose downward using an open rhinoplasty technique with structural grafting. Please visit with a couple rhinoplasty experts before deciding on the best path forward for you. Take care.
