Septoplasty in 2010, drooping tip, hanging columella?
Drooping nasal tip
Nov 27, 2013
It is somewhat difficult to assess the potential success of revision rhinoplasty without fully evaluating you. Revision rhinoplasty is usually an excellent technique for correcting the drooping tip. This issue occurs when the tip loses its support, most often with age or prior surgery. The correction usually involves using a cartilage graft between the existing cartilage to give it support and to reposition it.
