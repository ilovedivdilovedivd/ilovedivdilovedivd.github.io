Can liposuction and tummy tuck be done at the same time?
Liposuction with tummy tuck
Nov 11, 2019
Liposuction is commonly performed with a tummy tuck. I have been using the lipoabdominoplasty technique exclusively for the past 15 years. This preserves the major blood supply to the abdominal wall, which improves the circulation, making complications less likely. It results in better contour and avoids the need for liposuction at another time. If a traditional tummy tuck is performed without preservation of the blood vessels from the muscle, liposuction could increase complications. 
Do you think I’ll need a tummy tuck too with my BBL?
For this patient, BBL offers no improvement.
Oct 17, 2019
A BBL will not offer any improvement to your abdomen. A tummy tuck will remove loose skin and can remove fat as long as it is not deep and under the muscle.  You would need to be examined. The picture is not adequate.
What is the right order to have surgeries, time space between each, and which can be done together
Surgery at the same time
Oct 3, 2019
You could have all the surgeries at one time and you do not need multiple recovery periods. The cost would be much lower doing all at the same time. I frequently perform breast surgery, tummy tucks and liposuction at the same time. I am not a fan of BBL. Nationally there are significant complications. I have seen poor results from the technique involving the buttocks and deformity from where the fat was taken.
Can my stomach be fixed?
Your stomach can be improved
Oct 3, 2019
The main thing I notice from your pictures is marked asymmetry of the breast implants, which is much more obvious than a bulge in your abdomen. The abdominal bulge is either separation of the rectus muscles or intraabdominal fat. Muscle repair is usually easy to perform at the time of a tummy tuck. If it is the problem, it could still be repaired. For intraabdominal fat, you would need to loss weight, not have anymore surgery
How will I look after an explant? 450cc implants, 6 months post-op, A to D cup
how will I look after removal.

If your implants are under the muscle, removal should result in your original appearance. The muscle should be support the weight of the implant and not the skin. If the implants are on top of the muscle your skin will stretch the longer the implants are present.

Will the body lift have any effect on inner thigh?
body lift and inner thighs
Sep 19, 2019
There will be minimal to no effect on the medial thighs with a lower body lift. There can be a noticeable improvement of the anterior and lateral thighs with a body lift. A medial thigh lift can be performed at the same time or later. An incision from the groin to knee will give the best contour, but leaves a long scar. An excision in the groin crease can help hide the scar, but typically only tightens 25% compared to the long incision.
Is it possible to reduce breast size without removing existing implants?
It is possible to reduce breast size without removing implants
Jul 15, 2019
I have performed liposuction only breast reductions on over 1300 women. The average reduction is 2 cup sizes. I have used liposuction on 20 women with implants to successfully reduce the size. This is possible is the implants are under the muscle. The average improvement is nipple position is 2 inches. If you need more than that a lift can be performed at the same time.
Terrified of BIA-ALCL Is it common to switch to smooth implants and remove the capsule completely?
Capsulectomy can be performed
Jul 15, 2019
Since you are extremely stressed, I agree with your decision to change to smooth implants. There is no good scientific data to support capsulectomy, but it is reasonable since it is in the capsule layer where ALCL develops.Any competent plastic surgeon can perform a capsulectomy. There is a small risk of bleeding and you should have a drain placed at the time of surgery.
Replacing Ideal Implants with silicone - How long should I ideally wait before having them replace
Replacing Ideal Implants With Silicone Implants
Jul 15, 2019
The ideal implant is a high profile saline implant. A silicone implant should feel better. You could switch to a moderate or moderate plus profile implant, which will have a more natural shape with less or an over-inflated, round look. There is no reason to wait longer. Your current surgeon should be the least expensive option. There should be virtually no pain and they should look good immediately.
