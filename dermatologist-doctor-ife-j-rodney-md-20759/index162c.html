I’m 19. Will I go bald if only a small spot of thin hair is present on my crown?
Hair Loss
Mar 25, 2021
In my experience, the cause of the hair thinning is a big factor when it comes to trying to get the hairs back. Harvesting PRP and injecting it into targeted areas promotes collagen growth to promote new healthier hair growth. Mar 16, 2021
Hello,
In my experience, chemical peels can be used on several locations on the body for dark spots. Be sure to see a board-certified dermatologist to ensure the the chemical peels are safe for the area, your skin type/tone, and to evaluate all of the risks and benefits of chemical peels.
