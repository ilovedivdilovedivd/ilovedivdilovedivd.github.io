Can a bump upwards on nose tip dislodge the tip/cartilage after 2 months post-op?
You don't want this to happen after rhinoplasty
Jun 5, 2018
Rhinoplasty healing is not final until approximately 12 months after surgery.  I recommend that patients avoid contact sports for at least 6 weeks after surgery.  The nasal bones and cartilages have gone through the early healing phases by 4-5 weeks and are more resilient to small bumps.  However, a significant strike to the nose can re-break the nasal bones or damage the tip cartilages even as far out as 2 months.  In the whole healing process, 2 months is still fairly early.
