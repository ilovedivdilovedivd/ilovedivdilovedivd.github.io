I had a Rhinoplasty 3 weeks ago and concerned about dr advice. Any suggestions?
Massage can be very helpful
Jun 3, 2016
Crooked noses are difficult to address and the nose will have a tendency to drift back slightly to its preoperative side. Massage can be very helpful in these cases. The swelling on the side of the nose can be one sided and cause the nose to look crooked. At 3 weeks most of the swelling should be gone. It never hurts to see someone else as a second opinion to make sure that the nose is healing well. I would revisit your surgeon and bring up your concerns. 
Nasal Birth Trauma.. I'm 18, is there any way I can have this fixed?
Nasal Trauma
Jan 15, 2016
Insurance may cover the surgery if it is from a trauma, but you may also be covered under insurance for the breathing issues. Your nasal bridge may be smaller from the trauma and straightening the nose may include augmentation of the nasal dorsum. If the problem is mostly cosmetic it may not be covered by insurance. Your best bet is to be evaluated by a surgeon that performs rhinoplasty regularly and has good reviews.
