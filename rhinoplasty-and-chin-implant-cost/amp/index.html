Rhinoplasty and chin implant. Can I get a discount for both procedures?

Most offices will offer a multiple procedure discount on the order of 15-25% on second, third, fourth procedures but this is not always the case. You should inquire of your chosen surgeon whether a discount was included in your quote.

With regard to combining a rhinoplasty and chin implant, many people who are initially concerned about only their nose can benefit from both procedures to add balance to their profile. (Stephen Weber, MD, FACS, Denver Facial Plastic Surgeon)

Multi-procedure discounts for plastic surgery?

Unfortunately, there is no firm answer, except that discounts vary from surgeon to surgeon. Some surgeons discount very little, if at all, while others will give a set discount for multiple procedures, for example 5% off.

The most important thing is to identify the right surgeon for your rhinoplasty and chin augmentation, gently try to negotiate, settle on the fees, and then save up for your surgery. It’s likely better to wait for the surgeon you want, than to move quickly with a surgeon who is a bargain. (Randolph Capone, MD, Baltimore Facial Plastic Surgeon)

Bundling procedures

In general, when surgeons combine procedures they will package a fee that takes into account that the patient is having separate procedures, yet done under one anesthesia and facility fee. Accordingly, it is more common than not that the fee is less than doing each procedure separately (e.g. maybe $8000 instead of $9000). However, it depends on your location and surgeon reputation how much the fees will be.

The most important thing is always going to be quality, so choose a surgeon who can show you photo examples of the combination you are requesting (e.g. rhinoplasty and chin implant). (John Frodel, MD, )
