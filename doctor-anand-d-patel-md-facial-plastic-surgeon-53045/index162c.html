I'm 21 and want my eyes to look less tired. Options?
HA Filler
Jan 14, 2022
Lower eyelid dark circles are affected by volume loss (with or without fat bags), thin skin, and hyperpigmentation. From the photos you have provided, it looks like volume loss is a big factor for you and you may benefit from hyaluronic acid (HA) filler injections to the area. HA comes in different varieties and it is important that the product you use does not absorb too much water, otherwise the filler can create an overly-swollen look.
What can I do about my chin/lower face?
Prejowl Chin Implant
Nov 8, 2021
From your description and photos, I would recommend a prejowl chin implant. Patients who have never had a nice jawline (even in their youth) often have a bony deficiency in the prejowl area (the part of the jawline just in front of the jowl) that makes it look like they have heavier jowls. This implant will create a smoother jawline. This can be coupled with a submentoplasty (a procedure that addresses neck laxity and fullness) to also sharpen the neckline.
Can you replace a chin implant with a larger sized implant?
Yes
Oct 19, 2021
In most cases, you can replace a smaller chin implant with a larger one. Scar tissue tissue will not normally restrict this process.
Can you get cheek implants after a face lift?
Cheek Implants After A Facelift
Sep 27, 2021
Augmenting cheek volume will likely only enhance the results of a facelift. Sometimes we like to do these procedures at the same time as aging often involves both volume loss and increased laxity.
I'm in my early 20s but have awful eye bags. What is the best treatment filler or surgery?
Undereye Bags
Aug 10, 2021
Fillers are good for patients who have dark circles due to volume loss but without a significant eyelid bag. Injecting fillers in a patient with significant eyelid bags can lead to the bag looking even bigger! These patients benefit most from surgery.

Revision rhinoplasty to widen nasal bones and bridge?
Yes, it's possible.
Jun 7, 2021
Yes, it is possible to widen the upper bridge during revision rhinoplasty. This requires mobilizing the "side panels" of the bridge and then placing cartilage grafts that will allow the bones to heal in a wider position. This will also likely improve your breathing.
Would I benefit from an upper blepharoplasty on just one eye?
Would Not Recommend
May 24, 2021
I would not recommend a blepharoplasty on one eye at this time. The greater heaviness on one side appears to be due to increased brow elevation on the less heavy side. The cause for this may be eyelid ptosis and the best way to diagnose this is through an in-person examination. If this is the case, a ptosis repair would be best.
Is it safe to get nose fillers after 2 rhinoplasty?
Yes
May 18, 2021
Yes, you can use filler prior to your revision, however, all the filler should be 'gone' at the time of the procedure. For this reason, I suggest using a hyaluronic acid filler that will not last too long. Your surgeon can help you plan the time frame.
