What can I do to minimise my prominent nasolabial folds and sunken cheeks long term?
Restore facial volume
Apr 30, 2020
Facial volume can be restored with your own fat or fillers. Each option have advantages and disadvantages. Age-related facial changes are also related to laxity and tissue descent. Facial rejuvenation surgical procedures can reposition the tissues and restore a youthful look. A board-certified plastic surgeon can analyze your face and discuss your options.

Can I have a rhinoplasty after I had Botox for bunny lines?
Rhinoplasty after Botox
Apr 30, 2020
In general, you can have rhinoplasty after Botox and filler injection. Your provider will discuss your specific case. If the filler altered your nasal profile, you will need to wait 15-18 months for the filler to be metabolized by the body. Radiesse can also be removed with a dissolving agent.
What can I do for my large pores and acne scars?
Acne scars and microneedling
Apr 14, 2020
The treatment of acne scars is a challenge. There are multiple treatments available and a consultation with a board-certified dermatologist or plastic surgeon is necessary to evaluate your case. Microneedling is a new addition to the treatment of acne scars with good clinical efficacy. This treatment stimulates new collagen and help smooth out your skin. Downtime is minimal. Patients usually require multiple treatments to see a significant result. Treatments are space out every 4-6 weeks.
