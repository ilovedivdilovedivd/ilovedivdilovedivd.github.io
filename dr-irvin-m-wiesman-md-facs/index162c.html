How Much Does a Brazilian Butt Lift Cost?
You get what you pay for
Mar 31, 2011
The Brazilian butt lift is really a combination of procedures. It is first liposuction of the waist and other areas then it is taking the aspirated fat, removing the oil and blood, and injecting/augmenting the butt. The cost is usually in the range of $8-12000 depending on how many areas of liposuction.
Is There a Safe Brazilian Butt Lift Under $2000?
Beware cheap surgery
Mar 31, 2011
Brazilian butt lift is a long and relatively costly procedure. Prices in my area range from 7-12000. There is no way someone reputable could do this operation for 2000. If it's too good to be true than it likely is!
Beware!!

How Much Fat Can Be Transferred to the Buttocks Area?
The bigger the better.
Mar 31, 2011
