What are my options to treat a red and discolored mark on the bridge of my nose after rhinoplasty?
Treatment Option
Jun 21, 2022
Hi Powerful793044,
The most common reason for the discoloration you are describing is telangiectasias, which are small spider veins. These are best treated by laser, which can specifically target these small vessels. People prone to telangiectasias are likely to have more appear – if you look closely, you may have some small ones over your cheeks.
It will take a series of laser treatments to make it better.
