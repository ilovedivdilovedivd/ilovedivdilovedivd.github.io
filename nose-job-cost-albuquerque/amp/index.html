Indications for rhinoplasty.

Incorrect nose shape creates the greatest psychological problems for their owners.

Therefore, indications for rhinoplasty are:

– the desire to reduce or enlarge the size of the nose;
– the presence of various defects on the nose (hump, thickening, large nostrils, the long wings of the nose);
– posttraumatic bending nose;
– violation of nasal breathing.
