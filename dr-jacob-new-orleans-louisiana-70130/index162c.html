Is a drainless facelift more dangerous?
No difference
Oct 7, 2020
The purpose of drains is to provide suction between the skin and the deeper layers. This leaves little area for blood to accumulate and also promotes any small bleeds from becoming larger. There are tissue "glues" that accomplish the same thing by effectively holding the skin to the deeper structures via adhesive properties. Neither has been shown to be more effective at preventing hematomas or bruising. My preference is for fibrin glues during surgery.
