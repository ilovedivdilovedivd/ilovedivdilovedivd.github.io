Rhinoplasty: What not to do after?
What not to do after rhinoplasty
Jan 7, 2015
Of course, follow your postoperative instructions, because every surgeon has their preferences and reasons for why they ask you to do and not do certain things. I tell my patients not to bend, lift,strain or blow your nose for 10 days. You can blow gently out of both sides, but don’t hold one side and blow until 6 weeks. They will have a splint on for 5 days and tape for another 5, so don’t get either of these wet in the shower. No eyebrow plucking or threading…
Rhinoplasty: What age is ideal for surgery?
Age for rhinoplasty
Jan 7, 2015
The rule is to wait until you are finished growing to avoid any alterations in your facial growth. It is a good idea to wait at least a year after the onset of puberty.Often, I will wait until you haven’t grown taller or grown in shoe size for at least a year.
Simple Rhinoplasty Question?
Changing the tip with rhinoplasty
Jan 7, 2015
Yes, you can raise the tip of the nose and reshape it during rhinoplasty. How you do it depends on each nose. It can be by removing cartilage, but there are other ways to do it that work better in different kinds of noses. In a consultation with a plastic surgeon, they can tell you what would be the best way for you, if you are a good candidate and what the possible outcome could be.Good luck!
How Much Does Rhinoplasty Cost?
Rhinoplasty Cost
Aug 6, 2014
The cost of rhinoplasty varies by the part of the country in which you live, whether or not you live in a large city, suburbs or small town, the surgeon and what is needed for your nose. If your concerns are only functional, insurance may cover some of your costs, however it will not cover any cosmetic portion. Also, keep in mind that you will have a facility fee and anesthesia fee, so make sure you ask about those, as well. Good luck!
Is it possible to lengthen a nose without losing projection?
Lengthening the nose without losing projection
Feb 18, 2014
Yes, you can lengthen the nose without losing projection. I do this frequently for patients during revision rhinoplasties. You should find an experienced rhinoplasty surgeon in your area and communicate what you have in mind.
Nose looks worse 4 months post op than 1 month post op, why?
Changes in nose 4 months after surgery
Feb 17, 2014
You should talk to your surgeon about the changes that you have seen in your nose. It seems that you may have developed a pollybeak deformity, which can happen months after surgery. This can respond well to things like steroid injections that your surgeon can administer.
Firm bump found on the side of nose two weeks after Rhinoplasty.
Bump 16 days after rhinoplasty
Feb 17, 2014
You should bring this up to your surgeon. He/she will know exactly what was done during the surgery and will be able to fully evaluate it. This early after a surgery, you can have swelling that will resolve and may change the way that this looks.
Do dorsal onlay grafts help with borderline piggy noses?
Help with over-rotated nose
Feb 17, 2014
You will get the best answer from a consultation with a plastic surgeon who is comfortable with revision rhinoplasties. However, an over-rotated or “piggy” nose can be corrected with a caudal septal replacement graft or extended spreader graft. Keep in mind that the amount of de-rotation that can be achieved can be limited by the elasticity in the nasal skin.
Osteotomy done on a wide nose.
Swelling after surgery
Feb 17, 2014
You are very early after surgery. Your nose is still very swollen, so it is difficult to make an accurate assessment about it. Hang in there! You will be able to tell more as the swelling goes down.
Do you have to break your nose to deviate your septum?
Unlikely to have caused damage to nose with pressing
Feb 17, 2014
It is unlikely that you caused permanent changes in your nose or septum by pressing on it. It takes a fair amount of force to cause this type of change. You can have an Otolaryngologist evaluate your septum and your breathing concerns.
Blepheroplasty – did the surgeon cut too high?
Still very soon after surgery
Feb 5, 2014
Wait to see how the incision looks as the swelling comes down and keep good communication with your surgeon. You are still very early after surgery (only 9 days) and are still swollen. You can take supplements, like Arnica, to help the bruising resolve.

How long after a lilateral blepharoplasty can I go back to exercise, swim and use steam room and sauna
Activity after blepharoplasty
Feb 4, 2014
Check with your surgeon and follow their post-op instructions. I have my patients wait 2 weeks before swimming, steam rooms and saunas and 3-4 weeks for vigorous exercise.
Is hiking a mountain too strenuous for 15 days post blepharoplasty?
Hiking after blepharoplasty
Feb 4, 2014
I would ask your surgeon for their advice and follow their post-op instructions. Generally, it depends on how strenuous the hike will be. A relaxing walk or hike would be ok, but I have my patients avoid vigorous activity for 3-4 weeks.
Hollowness Under Eyes: Non-Invasive Options?
Noninvasive options for hollowness under the eyes
Feb 4, 2014
I recommend getting a consultation with a dermatologist, facial plastic, oculoplastic or plastic surgeon so they can assess your facial features. However, you may require more filler or a different technique to fully correct the hollowness under your eyes without surgery.
