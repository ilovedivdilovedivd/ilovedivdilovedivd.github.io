Will I lose strength after gyno surgery? Will it affect my Powerlifting routine?
No loss of strength after gynecomastia surgery
Feb 20, 2018
Breast tissue is considered part of the skin elements.  Therefore removing the breast tissue should not affect the strength of the muscle below.  After the healing has fully occurred, the strength should be back to the pre-operative state minus the loss from inactivity secondary to the recovery.
Full or partial removal? I see conflicting opinions regarding gland removal for gynecomas
Amount of tissue removed in gynecomastia
Feb 20, 2018
A gynecomastia procedure is a cosmetic procedure.  Therefore the removal should be in line with the aesthetic goal.  If all the gland needs to be removed, then it should be removed.  I usually leave a 1 cm of tissue underneath the nipple to prevent a deformity there.  I have never experienced a return of gynecomastia after removal.
