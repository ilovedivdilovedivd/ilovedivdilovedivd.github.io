Recovery after rhinoplasty

Symptoms are postoperative pain at the surgical site, extending to the region of the upper jaw. A primary swelling usually lasts 1-1.5 weeks.

The residual swelling persists longer – 2-6 months, but it is almost imperceptible to others. It is strictly forbidden to rub your nose and blow your nose in the first days after rhinoplasty, as this may cause a recurrence of the bleeding. Dry blood clots is recommended to remove not early than 2-3 days after the procedure.

In our clinic is practiced much less traumatic, although more technically complex method of correcting defects of the nose – closed rhinoplasty in Nashville.

This technique involves penetration into the nasal cavity without making the outer incisions from the side mucosa through the nostrils. Implemented by cutting off the skin and in excess tissue are removed.

If is required the completion of missing volume, object is achieved by means of placement of implants. The use of this technique avoids postoperative external scars and significantly reduce the recovery period.

This method of surgery is more popular in patients due to excellent esthetic results, but closed rhinoplasty is more complex, and from the plastic surgeon requires great skill.
