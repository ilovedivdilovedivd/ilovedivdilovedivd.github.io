Doctors

Jessica Wright, MD

Name: Jessica Wright, MD
Statement: Dr. Jessica Wright has been recognized in the aesthetics community by Galderma and Allergan as a top injector. Board certified in general surgery, she is the owner and medical director of Rejuvenate Austin, a luxury medical practice that specializes in anti-aging and wellness.

Dr. Wright received her medical degree from West Virginia University School of Medicine in 2002, then matched with her number one general surgery residency choice—Louisiana State University School of Medicine in New Orleans—a program based at Charity Hospital, one of the best facilities in the country for critical care and trauma. After completing her residency, Dr. Wright became a trauma surgeon at Our Lady of the Lake Hospital in Baton Rouge.

Dr. Wright’s partner at Our Lady of Lake, Dr. Stewart Cayton, also performed aesthetic treatments—treatments that always left Dr. Wright feeling refreshed and more confident. Inspired by the opportunity to use her medical training to treat patients in a way that made them feel good about themselves, and that reverberated throughout their lives, she made the transition into aesthetics, using her deep understanding of anatomy as a basis for her new expertise. Dr. Wright recently won the recognition as The Face of Anti-aging and Rejuvenation by Austin Monthly.
