Can a wide nose be thinned?
Rhinoplasty Can Thin Your Nose
Oct 16, 2019
Yes! Rhinoplasty can make your nose appear thinner on the frontal view. It is true that thicker skin may affect your healing, but surgery is the best option to get the result you want. I do recommend you are examined in person to confirm that you are a good candidate. I would also seek a double board certified facial plastic surgeon.
Is my nose so big? Maybe I need to lose weight?
No Weight Loss Necessary for Rhinoplasty!
Oct 14, 2019
Great news. Your nose shape and size does not depend on weight, so no weight loss is necessary before a rhinoplasty. Looking at your profile, it appears that you may benefit from some procedures to the lower part of your face. A chin implant and submental liposuction would create a more balanced and aesthetically pleasing profile. Even with weight loss, submental fat may not change. You have excellent brow position and with these small changes you can create good definition and facial harmony.
