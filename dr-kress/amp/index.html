Why do I feel like my implants are still sitting high 5 years later? What options do I have?
Implants too high
Apr 29, 2020
I agree you implants are too high and slightly uneven. If you have a doc who has the Aspen system (usually used for capsular contracture), you could do Aspen with aggressive massage and banding at night. is it normal when having liposuction with general anesthesia to burn during the procedure?
Liposuction complications
Apr 21, 2020
Under "general anesthesia" you are asleep and don't feel anything. If you have burning and numbness after the procedure that is a different question. Both burning and numbness are not unusual complications which usually resolve in a few months although the numbness can take up to a year. A better question is why do you need the same area repeated. Consult with a board certified plastic surgeon and discuss these items.

breast concerns
