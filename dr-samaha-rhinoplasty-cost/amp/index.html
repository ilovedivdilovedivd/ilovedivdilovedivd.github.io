Tip Rhinoplasty: What Is It?
Tip pasty or tip rhinoplasty
Nov 4, 2017
It is simply a rhinoplasty where only the tip of the nose is addressed and modified. It can be refined, turned up, lowered, shortened, or lengthened. 
It is generally done for patients who are satisfied with the appearance of the upper portion of the nose (sometimes called “bridge”, whether on the face view or profile.

Nov 4, 2017
Non-surgical rhinoplasty consists of using fillers, appropriately dosed and strategically placed along the nasal anatomy to camouflage certain imperfections and enhance the overall nasal appearance. Of course, through this technique, no tissue is removed. 
However, a few drops of filler, placed above the starting point of a hump will give the profile a straight appearance, thereby “drowning” the hump.
