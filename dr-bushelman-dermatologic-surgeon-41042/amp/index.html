Okay to get eyebrows waxed before Botox?
Botox can be done after brow waxing
Sep 22, 2016
It is perfectly fine to have botox after waxing. In fact, it would be preferable to have waxing BEFORE botox, rather than after. If the botox were done first, depending on the areas being treated, undue pressure (from waxing) could cause the botox to migrate into an area that may create an unwanted effect. Although your skin may be somewhat irritated after waxing, having your botox treatment afterwards is not a problem.
