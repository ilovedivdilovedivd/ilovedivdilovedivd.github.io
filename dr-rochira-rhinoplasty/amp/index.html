Which would be the best rhinoplasty option for a bulbous supratip and undefined nose?
Bulbous tip
Nov 18, 2018
Most of the problem is due to the tip cartilage.
You are a great candidate for a "preservation rhinoplasty" which modifies the bridge very slightly resulting in very natural look and can reduce and support the tip efficiently and safely.
What procedure should I consider? Rhinoplasy or septoplasty?
Septorhinoplasty
Nov 18, 2018
All my procedure involves some kind of Septoplasty. Therefore my procedure is called Septorhinoplasty.
With the nose you cannot really think that the septum is a separate structure. They work and act together. It is extremely rare not to touch the septum at all, particularly if the nose is not straight.
