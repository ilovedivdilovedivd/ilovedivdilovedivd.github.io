Nose Surgery Chicago Cost

Rhinoplasty in Chicago (nose surgery) is an operation which changes the shape and size of the external nose and restoring the function of nasal breathing.

The reason for correcting the shape of the external nose can be congenital or acquired (posttraumatic) deformation.

During the operation performed intervention on the bone and cartilaginous part of the nose.

The surgeon can remove the “hump” to align or narrowed the bridge of the nose, lifting nose, shorten and narrow the tip of the nose.

Sometimes it is necessary to the intervention on the wings of the nose, with their asymmetry, or excessive width of the base of the nose.

Quite often, the deformation of the external nose is combined with the violation of nasal breathing function, as a result of the curvature of the nasal septum or nasal turbinate hypertrophy.

In such cases, rhinoplasty can be combined with septoplasty by means of which the nasal breathing function is restored.

Rhinoplasty can be open and closed. There is also the term “non-invasive” rhinoplasty.

Open rhinoplasty: Incisions are made across the columella (column) of the nose and in nasal cavity. The skin is separated from the nose bones and all stages of the nose job carried out under direct vision.

At the end of the nose reshape surgery the skin closed with stitches, which are removed on the 5th day. Incisions in the nasal cavity are sutured with absorbable sutures, which do not require removal.

Before And After Cosmetic Surgery For Nose Reshaping In Chicago, IL

The external cast is superimposed to the nose and tampons are inserted into the nostril holes.

Closed rhinoplasty: the incision on the skin is not done. All incisions are made in the nasal cavity and hidden from view.

Most steps of rhinoplasty are performed blindly, so closed rhinoplasty is performed with a small correction of nose.

At the end of the nose reshaping on the nose superimposed a cast and tampons are inserted into the nostril holes.

Non-surgical rhinoplasty is a medical procedure in which injectable fillers, such as collagen, are used to alter and shape a person’s nose without invasive surgery. The most popular a plastic fillers are based on hyaluronic acid.

This procedure does not you need to be screened and is in hospital. The drugs are administered through injections under the skin of the nose.

Rhinoplasty can be performed starting from the age of 18, when the formation of bones and cartilages of the nose comes to an end.

Rhinoplasty surgery time: 1-2 hours.

Anesthesia: General or local.

Location: In a hospital environment. Stay 1-3 days.

Rhinoplasty And Septoplasty Are Often Performed At The Same Time

Possible side effects: Swelling, bruising around the eyes, minor bleeding in the early postoperative period. A few days later similar phenomena pass.

Risks: infectious complications, the asymmetry of the nose, dissatisfaction of the nasal form.

Recovery: Special tampons are inserted in the nose after rhinoplasty surgery, which are removed in 2-3 days. You need to wear a cast bandage week after surgery, then a cast is worn for two weeks only at night.

Physical activity you can resume in 3 weeks. The closest result of the nose beauty surgery is estimated after 3 months. And the final result is estimated after 1 year. You can return to work seven days after rhinoplasty in Chicago IL.
