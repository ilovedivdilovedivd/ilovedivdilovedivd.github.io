Which implant is better saline or silicone? Which is more likely to give the effect of “hard” boo
silicone vs saline
Oct 16, 2017
I prefer silicone as it is more natural looking and feeling.  When you meet with a board-certified plastic surgeon you should be able to see and feel the different types of implants and discuss what will look best on your frame.  Good luck!
I had saline Breast implants 30 years ago behind the breast tissue, can they be exchanged?
implant replacement
Oct 16, 2017
You should be evaluated by a board-certified plastic surgeon.  Most likely your implants can be removed and replaced but you may require a breast lift or a pocket revision as well.  Your surgeon may also recommend staging your surgeries.  You do need to have these implants removed though it is not an emergency.  Best of luck
My breasts are too close together… can this be fixed? 1 month post-op.
symmastia
Oct 16, 2017
I would recommend smaller implants that are more narrow than your current implants.  Hopefully the internal pockets do not actually communicate with each other.  If they do this is a much more difficult correction
How is capsulotomy going to stop the capsular contraction from getting worse? What is recommended
capsular contracture
Oct 16, 2017
I am sorry that you are experiencing capsular contracture.  This can be a very frustrating problem.  Capsulotomies and/or partial capsulectomies are often used to treat this problem.  Combined with a pocket change (meaning transition of where implant is placed….from behind breast to behind muscle) this may be enough.  Sometimes surgeons will add certain meshes as well.  Nothing is guaranteed and if you have a capsule you are more likely to develop one again.  Best of luck to you!
How much narrower can I go with an implant when BWD is 14-15 cm and am doing an anchor lift? Most
breast lift
Oct 12, 2017
You can choose a moderate profile implant that is more narrow than your breast depending on the pinch thickness of tissue coverage in that area.  An experienced board-certified plastic surgeon can evaluate your tissues and give you a range of acceptable choices to achieve a natural look.  Good luck!
Left breast flat on left side and pointing outwards after lift with implants. 3 weeks post op, is
nipple asymmetry
Oct 12, 2017
Congratulations on your breast lift.  Much can happen in the first several weeks to months after surgery.  This asymmetry may improve or it may not but a final determination cannot be made for at least 8 weeks.  You likely have swelling that could be different between sides as well.  in 8-12 weeks you can decide whether a revision surgery is warranted and the best timing to do that.  
Can I wear a padded bra for an event? I’m 2 weeks post-op
bra after surgery
Oct 12, 2017
It is so important to have your surgeon evaluate and advise you on this topic.  In general the breasts should be supported well for at least 3-6 weeks after surgery.  However many bras can support well and if your event allows you to wear a different yet still supportive bra then it make very well be ok.  Good luck!
Ripple after Cellfina. What can I do?
cellfina
Oct 12, 2017
It may be that you still have a lack of fat in this area with a contour deformity.  Most likely this is not FROM cellfina but rather a change in the contour after the treatment.  Your dimple is better as it has been released but now you seem to still have less fullness there causing a ripple.  Fat transfer to that area may work well for you
1.5 years post op. I knew right after the surgery that something was attached incorrectly. Can an
labiaplasty
Oct 12, 2017
It can be very difficult to tell from photos alone whether or not there is too little or too much tissue in certain areas.  The biggest concern in my opinion is the discomfort and addressing that particular area.  I would seek several consultations before proceeding
Is it possible for implants to drop all the way in 1 month?
settling after surgery
Oct 12, 2017
NO 2 surgeries are the same.  The amount of settling or “drop” one may get can be very different from the next patient.  Many women will have a nice stable result immediately after surgery and this may be quite stable.  Other women will experience some settling.  This is dependent upon your surgeon’s technique and implant choice.  I would discuss your questions with your surgeon.  Congratulations on your breast augmentation
Healing okay six weeks post op? Not sure if stitches have dissolved am I okay to wear underwired
bras after augmentaion
Oct 12, 2017
Most of us have slightly different instructions after surgery but in general you do not want to wear anything that may irritate the incision until it is healed completely.  YOu can continue to keep the incision moist with a silicone scar gel too.  Best of luck!
6 weeks post op, stitch opening? I have a small spot on the side of my breast that keeps getting
Breast augmentation scar
Oct 12, 2017
Congratulations on your breast augmentation.  I am sorry you are having trouble getting in to see your surgeon sooner after surgery.  Can you see the nurse?  Or send your photo via email?  Most likely this is a small stitch irritation and will heal without trouble.  I would advise you to keep it clean with soap and water in the shower and apply ointment with a dressing.  Best of luck!
What’s the best type of non-invasive body contouring procedure for upper arms?
coolsculpting for arms
Jul 17, 2017
Coolsculpting is a fantastic option for arms and bra rolls.  We have a lot of success with this treatment.  If you have significant excess skin however you may need a brachioplasty.  You should seek evaluation by a board certified plastic surgeon who does bothgood luck!
Is Cellfina right for me?
Cellfina for cellulite
Jul 17, 2017
Cellfina is a great option for cellulite dimples.  It will work best on your discrete dimples and be less effective for overall skin tightening.Best of luck!
Will Cellfina help dings & dents from aggressive lipo?
no
Jul 17, 2017
You may be better off having fat transfer to these areas.  And perhaps a skin tightening procedure.  It is doubtful that Cellfina will help.  You should seek evaluation by a board certified plastic surgeon good luck!
