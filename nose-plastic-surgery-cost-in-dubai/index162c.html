Nose Plastic Surgery Cost In Dubai

Rhinoplasty in Dubai, or nose correction, is one of the most difficult sections of plastic surgery consists in change of external contours of the nose and the full restoration of breathing through the nose.

During the rhinoplasty surgeon needs to work with the skin and mucosa of the nasal cavity, bone and a cartilaginous framework of it.

This large number of objects and determines the complexity of this section of plastic surgery. In addition, from the surgeon is required filigree surgical technique, sense of proportion, harmony and proportions of the face.

It is not always possible to change the external appearance of the nose, as desired by the patient. There are always predetermined the nature, type face and the concrete conditions of the scope for which to get out simply impossible.

In this case, rhinoplasty does not help. It should be clearly understood that a small “nosik-button” will be funny looks at the large face. Each type of face is proportional to its own type of nose structure.
